      <h4><img src="/images/candidates.jpg" alt="candidates" width="484" height="190" /><br /><br />Celebrate UCR's 60<sup>th</sup>Commencement</h4> 
      <p>Welcome to the official Web site for UC Riverside's 60th Commencement to be held June 13 - 16, 2014.</p> 
      <p>If you are a student about to graduate, congratulations on your achievement – you have worked hard to get to this point of your life!</p> 
      <p>Making this day a memorable occasion for you and your proud family members and friends is very important to UC Riverside, and we've developed this Web site to provide you with information on the ceremonies, what to expect, where to go, and what to do. Additional details will be posted to this site as they become available, so please check back periodically.</p> 
      <div class="nota-bene"> 
       <p>Students who expect to graduate Fall 2013, Winter 2014 or Spring 2014 are eligible to participate in Commencement 2014. Graduation application deadlines and additional information can be found on the following web sites:</p> 
       <h4>Bachelor's Degree Candidates</h4> 
       <p><a href="http://student.engr.ucr.edu/">Bourns College of Engineering </a>(BCOE)</p> 
       <p><a href="http://chassstudentaffairs.ucr.edu/graduation_ceremony_info/index.html">College of Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences </a>(CHASS)</p> 
       <p><a href="http://cnasstudent.ucr.edu/graduation.html"> College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences </a>(CNAS)</p> 
       <p><a href="http://undergradbusiness.ucr.edu/undergraduate_advising/">School of Business Administration </a>(SOBA)</p> 
       <h4>Advanced Degree Candidates</h4> 
       <p>For specific eligibility information and application deadlines contact <a href="mailto:kara.oswood@ucr.edu">Kara Oswood</a> in the Graduate Division Student Affairs Office.</p> 
       <h4>Teaching Credential Candidates</h4> 
       <p>For specific eligibility information and application deadlines contact your <a href="mailto:leticia.singh@ucr.edu">Leticia Singh</a>.</p>

       <h3>Announcements</h3> 
       <h4>Music Department and Medical School Again Present “Music as Medicine” Series</h4> 
       <p class="l_date">February 2014</p> 
       <p>The Tabla Ensemble directed by Abhiman Kaushal and the Bagpipe Ensemble directed by Ian Whitelaw will perform at noon Thursday, Feb. 27, on the patio of the School of Medicine Education Building. The free performances are open to the campus and greater Riverside community. The performances kick off a series of five noontime performances in the “Music as Medicine” series presented jointly by the UCR Department of Music and School of Medicine.</p> 
       <h4>Population Health Seminar Series Continues March 3</h4> 
       <p class="l_date">February 2014</p> 
       <p>Tanya Nieri, Assistant Professor of Sociology, will present the seminar “Zumba Fitness, Physical Activity, and Health Disparities: Revolution without A Pause?” at 4 p.m. Monday, March 4, in Room G650 of the School of Medicine Education Building. The seminar is part of the on-going series Population Health Research at UC Riverside.</p> 
       <h4>Perceptual Learning to be Discussed at March 21 Grand Rounds</h4> 
       <p class="l_date">February 2014</p> 
       <p>Aaron Seitz, UCR Associate Professor of Psychology, will give the grand rounds presentation “Applying Perceptual Learning to Produce Broad-Based Benefits to Vision” at noon Friday, March 21, in Room 1670 of the School of Medicine Education Building.</p>
