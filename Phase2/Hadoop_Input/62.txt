     <h3>Get the Advice You Need</h3> 
     <ul class="striped alt2"> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://undergradbusiness.ucr.edu/undergraduate_advising/">Business Administration Advising</a></strong><br />All students entering either the business preparatory or business administration major are advised by the undergraduate academic advisers. The business administration program is unique in that it manages department and college approvals for major-changes, petitions, academic action, articulation, transfer courses, readmission decisions, preliminary and final degree checks for undergraduate students in the business major. Advisers work with students from the time they enter the program through graduation.</li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://student.engr.ucr.edu/">College of Engineering Student Affairs</a></strong><br />Academic Advisors assist in all department-level and college-level advising services, including: 
       <ul> 
        <li>Assessing academic qualifications and progress.</li> 
        <li>Assist students with quarterly and annual course planning, registration details, enrollment questions, and prerequisite satisfaction.</li> 
        <li>Counsel prospective students on academic requirements necessary to enter the College's programs.</li> 
        <li>Refer students to campus services and resources, as appropriate.</li> 
        <li>Review candidates' eligibility for readmission, special admission, major change, double majors, and minors.</li> 
        <li>Evaluate and advise students in academic difficulty. Act on student petitions for exceptions to College and/or department requirements.</li> 
        <li>Review student degree progress toward graduation. Advise students of discrepancies discovered in these reviews.</li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://chassstudentaffairs.ucr.edu/">College of Humanities, Arts, &amp; Social Sciences Student Affairs</a></strong><br />Information including details on the 51 majors in CHASS, breadth requirements, dates and deadlines and transferable courses.</li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://www.cnasstudent.ucr.edu/">College of Natural &amp; Agricultural Sciences Student Academic Affairs</a></strong><br />Provides a variety of services to help guide CNAS students to the resources they need to successfully complete their educational objectives. Services include: 
       <ul> 
        <li>Mandatory, academic advising every quarter to all CNAS undeclared undergraduate students.</li> 
        <li>Advising and required processing of student requests.</li> 
        <li>Certifying that baccalaureate degree requirements have been met for all CNAS graduating seniors.</li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://chassstudentaffairs.ucr.edu/pre-business_program/index.html">Pre-Business Student Affairs</a></strong><br />Though part of the CHASS Advising program, Pre-Business specializes in working with students who seeking the Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration.</li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://www.smi.ucr.edu/">Science/Mathematics Initiative (Pre-Teach Advising)</a></strong><br />To facilitate in the advising of students toward completing a Bachelor's degree in science, mathematics, or engineering along with the possibility of establishing eligibility towards entrance into an intern teaching credential program</li> 
      <li><strong><a href="http://chassstudentaffairs.ucr.edu/undeclared_program/index.html">Undeclared CHASS Student Affairs</a></strong><br />Undeclared students are advised in the College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences, Student Academic Affairs.</li> 
     </ul>

      <script type="text/javascript">
  history.navigationMode = 'compatible';

  var t, st;
  var lastidx;
  var ishover = false;
  var isscrolling = false;
  var tcar;
  var nbritems = 0;
  var idlist  = new Array();
  var vsl = 1;
  var sauto = 8;
  var sshow = 3;

function mycarousel_itemFirstInCallback(carousel, item, idx, state) {
  var idm = ((idx-1) % nbritems) + 1;
  if (idm <= 0) idm = nbritems + idm;
  $('#slider').slider('option', 'value', idm);
  lastidx = idx;
  $("#idxdata").html(lastidx + " (" + idm + ") /" + nbritems);
  return true;
};


function mycarousel_resetAuto() {
  if (ishover) {
    t = setTimeout("mycarousel_resetAuto();", 6000);
  } else {
    tcar.startAuto(sauto);
  }
  return true;
};

function mycarousel_setSlider() {
  clearTimeout(st);
  if (isscrolling) {
	$("#sldata").html("scrolling " + vsl);
    st = setTimeout("mycarousel_setSlider();", 250);
  } else {
	$("#sldata").html("set " + vsl);
    tcar.scroll(parseInt(vsl),false);
  }
  return true;
};

function mycarousel_initCallback(carousel) {
  tcar = carousel;
  if (nbritems > sshow ) {

	$('#slider').bind('slide', function(event,ui) {
		clearTimeout(st);
		clearTimeout(t);
		carousel.startAuto(0);
		vsl = ui.value;
		if (isscrolling) {
		  $("#sldata").html("scrolling " + vsl);
          st = setTimeout("mycarousel_setSlider();", 250);
		} else {
		  mycarousel_setSlider();
		}

		t = setTimeout("mycarousel_resetAuto();", 6000);
    });


    // Disable autoscrolling if the user clicks the prev or next button.
    carousel.buttonNext.bind('click', function() {
		clearTimeout(t);
		t = setTimeout("mycarousel_resetAuto();", 6000);
        carousel.startAuto(0);
    });

    carousel.buttonPrev.bind('click', function() {
		clearTimeout(t);
		t = setTimeout("mycarousel_resetAuto();", 6000);
        carousel.startAuto(0);
    });

    // Pause autoscrolling if the user moves with the cursor over the clip.
    //carousel.clip.hover(function() {
    $('#mycarousel').hover(function() {
        clearTimeout(t);
        ishover = true;
        //carousel.stopAuto();
		carousel.startAuto(0);
		$("#hdata").html("is hover");
    }, function() {
		clearTimeout(t);
		$("#hdata").html("is not hover");
		ishover = false;
        //carousel.startAuto(3);
		t = setTimeout("mycarousel_resetAuto();", 6000);
    });
  } else {
    carousel.startAuto(0);
    $("#slider").hide();
    $("#news_carousel").find(".jcarousel-next").hide();
    $("#news_carousel").find(".jcarousel-prev").hide();
  }
  return true;

};


function mycarousel_itemVisibleInCallback(carousel, item, i, state, evt) {
  // The index() method calculates the index from a
  // given index who is out of the actual item range.
  $("#iodata").html("in " + state);
  if (state != "init") isscrolling = true;
  var idx = carousel.index(i, idlist.length) - 1;
  var izz = idlist[idx];
  carousel.add(i, mycarousel_getItemHTML(izz));
  return true;
};

function mycarousel_itemVisibleOutCallback(carousel, item, i, state, evt) {
    carousel.remove(i);
	isscrolling = false;
	$("#iodata").html("out " + state);
  return true;
};

/**
 * Item html creation helper.
 */
function mycarousel_getItemHTML(item) {
  return '<div "id="newsactive' + item + '" >' + $("#newsid" + item).html() + '</div>';
};

$(document).ready(function() {

  $("#holder").hide();
  $("#slider").slider({ min: 1, max: 1 });

  $.getJSON(newsserver + "an_get_news_list.php?jsoncallback=?",
    function(nwdata) {
      //alert("Count: " + nwdata.nbrarticles);
      nbritems = parseInt(nwdata.nbrarticles);
      idlist   = nwdata.idlist;
      $('#slider').slider('option', 'max', nbritems-(sshow-1));
      $('#mycarousel').jcarousel({
        auto: sauto,
        scroll: 1,
        animation: 1000,
        wrap: 'circular',
        animatebuttons: false,
        initCallback: mycarousel_initCallback,
        itemFirstInCallback: {onBeforeAnimation: mycarousel_itemFirstInCallback},
        itemVisibleInCallback: {onBeforeAnimation: mycarousel_itemVisibleInCallback},
        itemVisibleOutCallback: {onAfterAnimation: mycarousel_itemVisibleOutCallback}
      });
  });
});

</script>

     <h2 class="intro"> A Wise Investment </h2> 
     <h3 class="verd intro"> If you’re ready to attend UC Riverside, <br /> nothing should stop you – especially money.</h3> 
     <div class="clearfix"> 
      <div class="grid_4 alpha"> 
       <p> You can pay for school (and graduate with less debt) thanks to UCR's top-ranking value and easy-to-apply-for financial aid. </p> 
       <p> We've worked hard to make sure that, regardless of your finances, you have access to a world-class education. Plus, your degree is an investment that literally pays off. </p> 
       <img alt="" width="178" height="96" src="/Images/payingCollege/dollarBill.png" style="margin-bottom: 28px;" /> 
       <h3> Value that Feels Good</h3> 
       <p> We know the value of a degree goes beyond just how much money you can earn with one. <a href="http://www.washingtonmonthly.com/college_guide/rankings_2013/national_university_rank.php"> UCR is 2nd in the nation in contributing toward public good</a> (Washington Monthly, 2013). </p> 
       <p>From founding <a href="http://soba.ucr.edu/involved/alumni_profiles/albers_profile.html"> renewable energy enterprises</a> and <a href="http://www.childleaderproject.org/sample-page/"> global nonprofits</a> to creating <a href="http://newsroom.ucr.edu/2070">Pulitzer-prize-winning art</a>, our diverse graduates change the world. And that’s pride you can’t put a price on! </p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="grid_8 omega"> 
       <h3 class="humongous lightBlue"> One-Fifth</h3> 
       <p> UCR students who borrow federal loans graduate with an average student loan debt of <strong>$20,427</strong> – just 1/5 of the actual total cost of tuition and housing. </p> 
       <p style="margin-top: 25px;"> <br /><br /> <img alt="" width="37" height="43" class="imgFloatLeft" src="/Images/payingCollege/calculatorIcon.png" /> <br /><a href="http://vcsaweb.ucr.edu/FinAidManualCalculator/" class="rightArrowCTA">See a Cost Estimator</a></p> 
       <a href="http://www.bls.gov/emp/ep_chart_001.htm" target="_blank"><img alt="Overall, the higher your degree, the more money you earn." width="419" height="297" class="imgFloatLeft" src="/Images/payingCollege/educationPays.png" style="margin-top: 74px;" /></a> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div style="margin-top: 15px;" class="doubleRoundedCorners borderGrey"> 
      <div class="clearfix gradientGrey" style="padding: 8px 8px 0 8px"> 
       <img width="260" height="152" src="/Images/payingCollege/bellTower.png" class="imgFloatRight" alt="" /> 
       <h3 style="margin-top: 10px;"> How Much Does UCR Cost?</h3> 
       <p> UCR is not only a <a href="http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/rankings/national-universities/best-value"> Top 25 Best Value School (</a>U.S. News &amp; World Report, 2013) we rank higher than <em>any other University of California campus</em>. That means you can earn a prestigious diploma and get an outstanding education without going broke. Smart choice!</p> 
       <p>&nbsp; </p> 
       <p> <a class="rightArrowCTA" href="/PayingForCollege/costEstimator?Length=16">See how affordable it is to attend UCR.</a> </p> 
       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="clearfix" style="margin-top: 38px;"> 
      <div class="grid_6 alpha suffix_1"> 
       <h3> A UCR Education <em>Really</em> Pays!</h3> 
       <p> UCR graduates are in demand. Recent <a href="http://www.payscale.com/best-colleges/top-state-universities.asp"> national research from PayScale</a> found that UCR graduates are in the top 50 for highest salary potential out of 400 leading national public universities. That’s not all - take a look at how your median income could increase over the years:</p> 
       <p class="pushSpanRight" style="font-size: 0.9em;"> <strong class="black">UCR Graduate Starting Median Salary:</strong><span> $43,300</span></p>

      <p><span>Welcome to the Department of Botany and Plant Sciences at the University of California, Riverside.&nbsp; Our website provides visitors with information about the Department, our faculty, students and staff, and our many programs and support facilities.&nbsp; Our mission is to conduct cutting-edge research in plant biology to advance fundamental scientific knowledge and solve critical issues for the state of California, and to educate and train graduate and undergraduate students to become science professionals and informed global citizens.&nbsp; The department has strong programs in basic plant cell biology, responses of plants to environmental stresses, plant ecology, genetics, genomics, and evolution.&nbsp; These strengths in basic research complement applied research programs that use the traditional tools of plant science and the new technologies of genomics to meet the evolving needs of California's $27 billion agricultural industry. Development of new varieties and crop management strategies help address the challenges of climate change and new pest and disease.</span></p> 
      <p><span>Please follow the links to explore the research programs of our outstanding faculty, the extension programs our Cooperative Extension Specialists have developed to implement research discoveries in agriculture and natural resources, and opportunities for enrollment in the undergraduate and the graduate degree programs in Plant Biology that we administer.</span></p>

      <div class="span4 hero-unit"> 
       <p>We manage and oversee systemwide financial management.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span4">
       <p>The CFO division is responsible for the systemwide management and oversight of all aspects of financial management at the campuses, academic medical centers and Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. Responsibilities include oversight on accounting and financial controls, risk management, strategic sourcing, tax compliance, payroll coordination and long range financial planning. We are also responsible for external relationships with rating agencies, investment houses, banks, financial auditors and financial regulators.</p>
