            $(".verticalslider_contents").hide(); //Hide all content
           
           
            $("ul.verticalslider_tabs li:first").addClass("active").append(arrowBlock).show();
            $(".verticalslider_contents:first").show(); 

            $("ul.verticalslider_tabs li").click(function () {
                $('.arrowLeft').remove();
                $("ul.verticalslider_tabs li").removeClass("selected");
                $(this).addClass("selected").append(arrowBlock); 
                $(".verticalslider_contents").hide();

                $("div#" + activeTab).fadeIn();

      <h3>How Course Proposals Work</h3> 
      <p>Use the steps below to see each stage of the course proposal process. Find out how faculty, departmental preparers and multiple approvers each contribute as the proposal makes its way through the system. See <a class="noUnderline" href="/crams/support-services/users-and-definitions.aspx">Users and Definitions</a> for an overview of CRAMS terminology. </p> 
      <p><strong>All successful course proposals go through the following process:</strong></p> 
      <div class="slider"> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-7 last"> 
      <div class="sidebar"> 
       <img src="/Images/magnifier.png" style="float: right; margin: 4px;" /> 
       <div class="title">
         CRAMS Resources: 
       </div> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="/Content/pdfs/Find_a_Preparer.pdf" target="_new">Find a Preparer</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/committee/8/Submission%20Deadlines%20for%202014-15.pdf">Dates and Deadlines</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/crams/courseproposal/reports.aspx">Request Reports</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/committee/?do=info&amp;id=8]">Committee on Courses</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/crams/locationofcourses/default.aspx">Track My Proposal</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/crams/supportservices/cramsfaq.aspx">FAQs (Priority Enrollment, Sunset Clause, NOFY, Cross-Listed)</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <br /> 
     </div> 
     <div class="wizard-wrapper" style="margin-top: 220px"> 
      <div class="wizard-content border1px" style="width: 379px; min-height: 325px; float: left; margin-right: 0px; padding-left: 20px;"> 
       <div id="tab1" class="verticalslider_contents"> 
        <p> When a department initiates a course proposal, its faculty members fill out specific <a href="../Forms/Default.aspx">worksheets</a> and a <a href="syllabus.aspx">syllabus</a> and give them to the designated <a href="../../Content/pdfs/Find_a_Preparer.pdf" target="_blank">preparer</a>. </p> 
       </div> 
       <div id="tab2" class="verticalslider_contents"> 
        <p> First (if required), the preparer <a href="../CourseProposal/Reports.aspx">requests reports</a> that show how other classes/majors/minors would be impacted by the course proposal.</p> 
        <p>The preparer must contact other departments impacted by the proposal (<a class="noUnderline" href="/crams/course-management/cross-listed-courses.aspx">cross-listed courses</a> etc.) so that they can adjust where needed. <br /> <br /> Then, the preparer meets <a href="../support-services/system-requirements.aspx">system requirements</a> and installs CRAMS on their computer. <br /> <br /> The preparer enters the course proposal into CRAMS using faculty-provided worksheets, additional <a href="../Forms/Default.aspx">checklists</a>, the <a href="../CourseProposal/styleguidelines.aspx">Style Guidelines</a> and the <a href="../course-management/Default.aspx">Course Management Guide</a>. </p> 
        <p>We encourage all preparers to sign up for CRAMS training. Please email <a href="mailto:melinda.miller@ucr.edu" target="_blank">melinda.miller@ucr.edu</a> for details. </p> 
       </div> 
       <div id="tab3" class="verticalslider_contents"> 
        <p> Once submitted, the proposal follows a specific <a href="approval-routing.aspx">approval routing path </a> in which approvers (the reviewer, faculty committee members and chairs) review the proposal in CRAMS and provide feedback. <br /> <br /> Having the proposal in CRAMS allows multiple approvers to see it and provide feedback in a centralized location. It also ensures correct formatting for the <a href="http://catalog.ucr.edu">General Catalog</a>. <br /> <br /> The preparer works with the faculty initiating the proposal to make adjustments as needed. </p> 
       </div> 
       <div id="tab4" class="verticalslider_contents"> 
        <p> The preparer continually tracks the <a href="../LocationOfCourses/Default.aspx">proposal’s progress</a> until it reaches the appropriate <a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/committee/?do=info&amp;id=8">Committee on Courses</a> meeting (where it may ultimately be approved). </p> 
       </div> 
       <div id="tab5" class="verticalslider_contents"> 
        <p>If the <a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/committee/?do=info&amp;id=8" target="_blank">Committee on Courses</a> approves the proposal, the preparer will receive an email indicating next steps. </p> 
        <p>If the proposal is not approved, the proposal returns to the CRAMS course preparer Work-in-Progress Queue (WIPQ) and the process begins again. </p> 
       </div>
