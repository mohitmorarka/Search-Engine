      <h3>About UC Riverside University Advancement</h3> 
      <p>Under the leadership of Vice Chancellor Peter A. Hayashida, the UC Riverside University Advancement team:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Raises public awareness of UCR's excellence in research, teaching and service.</li> 
       <li>Encourages, promotes, and secures public and private resources to support: 
        <ul> 
         <li>Recruitment and retention of world-class faculty.</li> 
         <li>High-quality education for the best and brightest students who are reflective of California's diverse population.</li> 
         <li>Leading-edge facilities and equipment necessary for inquiry and the creation of knowledge for the 21st century and beyond.</li> 
        </ul> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <p><a class="standout3" href="/org-chart.html">View organization chart</a></p>

       <h3><img id="box_img" src="/images/peter_hayashida.jpg" alt="Photo: Pater Hayashida" width="130" height="159" />About Vice Chancellor Peter A. Hayashida</h3> 
       <p><strong>Vice Chancellor for University Advancement Peter Hayashida</strong> joined UC Riverside on July 1, 2009. He came from UCLA, where he had served as assistant vice chancellor for external affairs and executive director of The UCLA Foundation since 2000.</p> 
       <p>After earning his bachelor's degree in communication studies at UCLA, Hayashida spent several years employed in private sector finance and marketing. He returned to his alma mater as a student affairs officer in UCLA's College of Letters &amp; Science. He then spent seven years working at the UCLA Alumni Association, ultimately serving as associate executive director for Finance and Administration and chief financial and operating officer before moving to The UCLA Foundation.</p> 
       <p>Hayashida earned a master of business administration degree from California State University, Northridge. He has served on non-profit boards and committees both for his professional association and in the community.</p>

       <h3>Current Quarter Tuition and Fees</h3> 
       <p>See a rundown of this quarter's tuition and fees for undergraduate, graduate, medical and MBA students. Select from the drop-down menu to see past, current and future quarters. Use <a href="/registrar/archives/default.aspx">our archives</a> or <a href="/registrar/help-desk/default.aspx">contact us</a> if you need access to records from more than a year ago. Hover over fee names to see descriptions and learn how each fee supports the dynamic students, programs and services that makes UCR a top-ranking university in affordability and value.</p> 
       <p>Keep in mind, these are estimates based on currently approved amounts. These figures may not be final. Actual tuition, fees and charges are subject to change by the Regents of the University of California and could be affected by state funding reductions. Final approved amounts (and a student's final balance due) may differ from the amounts shown.</p>
