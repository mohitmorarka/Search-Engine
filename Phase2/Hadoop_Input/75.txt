       <h3>Providing Services to Help You Succeed</h3> 
      </div> 
      <div class="spotlight">
       The Academic Resource Center is a team of educators, counselors, and advanced students who provide a variety of services to help students succeed in their college classes. The Academic Resource Center is for all students who are not satisfied with their current grades and/or academic performance. We regularly see first-year students (freshman and transfer) and graduate students, students on academic probation and those on the Dean's List. Any student who wants help can get it, and almost all of our services are free.
      </div>

The longtime show judge and music producer has been with the competition series since its inception, and this year he returned as a mentor. Ellen has already grilled the new panel of judges on their selections, and she made an idol out of castoff Jessica Bassett. Now, Ellen and Randy will continue the discussion, as the &quot;Idol&quot; vet gives his take on this season's competitors, as well as his creative vision for his new role on the show!</p> 
       <p class="p1">Proving beauty and brains go far in Hollywood, MARIA MENOUNOS is always up to something! The former Miss Massachusetts Teen USA currently serves as an entertainment news correspondent on &quot;Extra,&quot; in addition to acting in films, TV shows, and wrestling professionally in her spare time! With so much going on, Maria is opening up her life as the subject of a new OWN docu-series, &quot;Chasing Maria Menounos.&quot; She'll take a brief moment to tell Ellen all about it!</p> 
       <p class="p1">Then, PANIC! AT THE DISCO, one of Ellen's favorite bands, raises the roof! Their rock and pop-fused melodies have topped the Billboard charts since they debuted in 2005 with a double-platinum album. These days, the band is touring the world, firing up spirits with their disco-esque fever and incredible energy, and now it's their debut on Ellen! The Grammy-nominated group will perform their hit “This Is Gospel” from their fourth studio album, “Too Weird to Live, Too Rare to Die!”</p>

      <div class="span4">
       <p>Janet Napolitano was named the 20<sup>th</sup> president of the University of California on July 18, 2013, and took office on Sept. 30, 2013. She leads a university system with 10 campuses, five medical centers, three affiliated national laboratories, and a statewide agriculture and natural resources program. The UC system has more than 234,000 students, about 208,000 faculty and staff, more than 1.6 million living alumni and an annual operating budget of more than $24 billion.&nbsp;<a href="about/index.html">Read more</a></p>

      <h3>We Give Your Course a Home</h3> 
      <br /> 
      <h4>Academic Scheduling serves you by:</h4> 
      <ul class="orange-bullets wide"> 
       <li>Scheduling academic course activities (lectures, discussions) in general assignment classrooms for winter, spring and fall quarters.</li> 
       <li>Scheduling one-time events tied to academic courses (review sessions, guest speakers) for winter, spring and fall quarters.</li> 
       <li>Providing training for the course-scheduling component of the Student Information System (SIS).</li> 
       <li>Connecting you to classroom technology and maintenance support.</li> 
      </ul>
