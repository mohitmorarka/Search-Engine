     <h3>UCR: A Friend and Partner in the Community</h3> 
     <p> You don't have to be a student or employee at UC Riverside to take advantage of the campus' opportunities and resources. </p> 
     <p>Whether you are a business owner looking to work with the university, a community member looking to attend one of our performing arts or athletics events, or someone looking to continue your education or to volunteer your time and resources, UC Riverside has something for you. </p> 
     <h4>Come Visit Us</h4> 
     <p>We hope you will come to visit the campus. It is a vibrant place that is growing and changing daily. Contact our <a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/avc">Alumni and Visitors Center</a> at (951) 827-2586 for information about seeing the campus.</p> 
     <ul class="striped big_links"> 
      <li><a href="/friends/contact.html"><strong>Who to Contact</strong>Links and contact information for organizations for our friends and neighbors.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="/friends/invited.html"><strong>You're Invited</strong>There is a lot going on at UC Riverside, and virtually all of it is open to the public. Links to the campus Happenings page, our campus calendar, as well as a list of some of our premier resources.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="/friends/neighborhood.html"><strong>Being Good Neighbors</strong>Good neighbors build great neighborhoods. See the campus' Good Neighbor Guidelines as well as links to campus policies and Riverside city codes.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="/friends/industry.html"><strong>Opportunities for Industry &amp; Employers</strong>UCR is educating the next generation of employees and conducting cutting-edge research that will result in the development of new technology and inventions.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="/friends/volunteer.html"><strong>Getting Involved</strong>There are many ways that you can become involved at UCR. Here's a list of a few.</a></li> 
     </ul>

     <h3>International Resources</h3> 
     <p><strong>UC Riverside is committed to providing</strong> international education and collaboration opportunities for students, faculty and other scholars. We recognize the value of academic and social interactions between students and faculty who represent different cultures from the U.S. and throughout the world. It is our goal to facilitate study and work abroad experiences for UCR students, to encourage applications from potential students who are citizens of other countries, to provide short-term educational experiences for visiting international students, and to encourage international scholarly collaborations.</p> 
     <p>Recognizing the value of international collaboration, UCR partners with universities throughout the world to maintain the highest standards of teaching and research, to keep up with academic trends, and to share innovations.</p>

         <h2>Technology</h2> 
         <p>More than just a place for discovery, UCR has earned a reputation for incubating pathbreaking ideas and translating them into actionable solutions.</p> 
         <p>We engineer tissues with polymers to create new supplies of compatible bone, cartilage, organs and limbs. We collaborate with a global network of academic, government and industry partners to explore the commercial potential of emerging technologies. We develop new materials for energy, manufacturing, communications and ultra-fast computing. We educate 21st century architects, engineers, urban planners and entrepreneurs.</p> 
         <p>We don’t just ride the wave of technological change shaping our era, we harness its power to invent the future.</p>
