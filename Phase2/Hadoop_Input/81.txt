     <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="mainContent" -->The&nbsp;
     <strong>Administrative Studies</strong>&nbsp;majors are designed to educate students in the art and science of management in combination with coursework completed in one of five departments. Departments offering an administrative studies component with selective courses from their own disciplines are:&nbsp;
     <br /> 
     <br /> 
     <strong>Art History</strong>&nbsp;- examines art pieces in various contexts including history, culture and politics.&nbsp;
     <br /> 
     <br /> 
     <strong>Economics</strong>&nbsp;- examines the factors determining national income, inflation, unemployment, output, growth and inequality as well as the behavior of individual decision-making units like households and firms, the role of markets, money and interest rates, the forces affecting international trade, and other problems of production and distribution.&nbsp;
     <br /> 
     <br /> 
     <strong>History</strong>&nbsp;- provides the broad foundation of understanding people's past experiences from different times and places, with particular specializations in Europe and in the Americas.&nbsp;
     <br /> 
     <br /> 
     <strong>Political Science</strong>&nbsp;- focuses on the number of political experiences examined from democracy and dictatorship while teaching the ideologies and philosophies behind them.&nbsp;
     <br /> 
     <br /> 
     <strong>Sociology</strong>&nbsp;- examines societal issues including: race and class inequality, stratification, the impact of the environment and crime and deviance.&nbsp;

                  &nbsp;

                 </div>

                  &nbsp;
                 </div>

         <h4 class="dotted"><img class="img_L" src="/SiteCollectionImages/ladylookingrightup.jpg" />Frequently<br />Asked<br />Questions</h4> 
         <h5><br />How much money do I owe?</h5> 
         <p>Access the national student loan database, which has a record of all federal student loans.</p> 
         <p><a title="National Student Loan Database" href="http://www.nslds.ed.gov/nslds_SA/" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p> 
         <h5>What's the difference between a &quot;subsidized&quot; and an &quot;unsubsidized&quot; loan?</h5> 
         <p>&quot;Subsidized&quot; means that the federal government pays the interest on a borrowers's loan, while the student is enrolled at least half the time.</p> 
         <p><a title="" href="/typesAid/Pages/Loans.aspx">Learn More</a></p> 
         <h5>What are the 2014-2015 deadlines?</h5> 
         <p>You must submit your FAFSA by March 2, 2014, to be considered for state and institutional awards, including Cal Grant and UCR financial aid. The deadline to file a <a title="" href="http://www.fafsa.ed.gov/" target="_blank">FAFSA on the Web</a> for federal financial aid is June 30, 2015.</p> 
         <p><a title="" href="/qualify/Pages/default.aspx?p=1">Learn More</a></p> 
         <h5>What are the GPA requirements for maintaining my financial aid?</h5> 
         <p>Undergraduate and teaching credential students must have a 2.0. Graduate students need to maintain a 3.0.</p> 
         <p><a title="" href="/maintainEligibility/Pages/Academic.aspx">Learn More</a></p> 
         <h5>Can&nbsp;I get financial aid for summer school?</h5> 
         <p>Summer financial aid applicants must complete and submit the online&nbsp;Request for Summer Aid form on <a title="GROWL" href="http://growl.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">GROWL</a>.</p> 
         <p><a title="Summer Financial Aid" href="http://summer.ucr.edu/faid.html" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p>

      <div class="padding">

       <div class="tab-content"> 
        <div id="tab1-content"> 
         <h3>Change Your Name</h3> 
         <p>To change or correct your name on academic records, you will need:</p> 
         <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
          <li>A photo ID</li> 
          <li>Legal documentation (court order, marriage license, birth certificate or passport)</li> 
          <li>A completed <a href="/content/pdfs/change_or_correct_name.pdf" target="_blank">Change or Correction of Name Form</a></li> 
         </ul> 
         <p>Bring the above to the Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS) on the first floor of the <a href="http://campusmap.ucr.edu/imap/index.html?loc=SSB" target="_blank">Student Services Building</a> or mail it to:</p> 
         <address> University of California, Riverside<br /> Office of the Registrar<br /> 2249 Student Services Building<br /> Riverside, CA 92521-0118<br /> </address> 
         <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
          <li>All requests for changes or corrections of name must be accompanied by the required legal documentation.</li> 
          <li>Photocopies are fine if you are mailing in your request.</li> 
         </ul> 
         <p><strong>IMPORTANT:</strong> If you want your new name to appear on your diploma (without paying an additional fee), make your name change request before the first day of finals during your graduation quarter. <a href="/registrar/academic-calendar/default.aspx">See the calendar</a> for the first day of finals.</p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab2-content" class="hide"> 
         <h3>Change Your Address or Phone Number</h3> 
         <p> <br /> You can change your address or phone number in <a href="https://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html" target="_blank">Growl.</a> </p> 
         <p><strong>Update Other Offices</strong></p> 
         <p>Keep in mind, the address and phone number you submit is also used by:</p> 
         <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
          <li>Financial Aid</li> 
          <li>Student Business Services</li> 
          <li>Academic Departments</li> 
         </ul> 
         <p>It is NOT used by:</p> 
         <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
          <li>Rivera Library</li> 
          <li>Transportation and Parking Services</li> 
          <li>Residence Halls</li> 
          <li>The Campus Health Center </li> 
         </ul> 
         <p>That means you will need to contact those offices and inform them of your change of address and/or phone number.</p> 
         <p><strong>If You Don't List an Address</strong></p> 
         <p>If there is no billing address on file, your monthly billing statements and refund checks are sent to your permanent address.</p> 
         <p><strong>Directory Information is Public</strong></p> 
         <p>Directory information is considered public information and is distributed upon request to outside parties. You can customize which parts of your directory information are made public. <a href="/registrar/privacy-ferpa/disclosure-policies.aspx">Learn how</a>.</p> 
        </div>
