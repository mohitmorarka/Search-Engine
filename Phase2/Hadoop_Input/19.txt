     <h3 class="l_blue_gold"><span>How Can We Help You </span>Succeed?</h3> 
     <p>The Office of Corporate Partnerships, housed within the <a href="http://or.ucr.edu/">Research and Economic Development (RED) office</a>, provides a one-stop concierge services for businesses interested in tapping the potential of UCR’s cutting-edge research, market-ready technologies, world-renowned faculty, accomplished students, and state-of-the-art facilities.</p> 
     <p>As Inland Southern California’s only public research university, UCR consists of seven schools and colleges including our recently accredited School of Medicine and a new School of Public Policy. The campus contributes $1.4 billion per year to the state of California through research, teaching, employment, investment and community involvement.</p> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Why<span> UCR?</span></h3> 
     <ul class="l_big_arrows"> 
      <li><strong>Recruiting</strong> — Find our graduating students seeking permanent employment opportunities or our continuing students looking for internships.</li> 
      <li><strong>Corporate Sponsored Research and Development</strong> — Fast-track your R &amp; D through partnerships with research groups and/or individual faculty on specialized projects tailored to your interests and needs.</li> 
      <li><strong>Advanced Facilities &amp; Equipment</strong> — UCR Research labs house advanced equipment from scanning electron microscopes to nuclear magnetic resonance. Arrangements can be made for your company to tap into these resources for a fee.</li> 
      <li><strong>Philanthropic Investment</strong> — Support UCR programs educating the next generation of corporate leaders and receive recognition for supporting UCR.</li> 
      <li><strong>Specialized Training</strong> — Invest in your staff to continually secure your organization’s place in the global market. UCR provides an array of certificate programs and specialized classes. We can design classes to meet your individual needs.</li> 
      <li><strong>Services</strong> — UCR can assist your company in many ways including statistical analysis of data and teaching you to search for prior art in patents.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Research Funding<span> Overview</span></h3> 
     <p>In FY 2011-12, we attracted approximately $114 million in research funding, including 867 contracts and grants.</p> 
     <p><img src="/industry/images/research-funding-piechart.png" alt="Research Funding Pie Chart. Click description link below for text-only version." width="452" height="175" /> <a title="Text description" href="/industry/accessibility/research-funding-piechart-desc.html">[d]</a></p> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Recognition<span> Opportunities</span></h3> 
     <p>UCR provides a variety of focused opportunities for your organization to gain visibility and build brand awareness with our extended campus community and industry partners. Through an active presence on campus, companies may enjoy increased exposure through enhanced interaction with our students and faculty, visibility in UCR’s online and printed publications, on-campus signage or through naming opportunities such as facilities, laboratories, endowed chairs and academic programs.</p>

     <h3 class="l_blue_gold"><span>How Can We Help You </span>Succeed?</h3> 
     <p>The Office of Corporate Partnerships, housed within the <a href="http://or.ucr.edu/">Research and Economic Development (RED) office</a>, provides a one-stop concierge services for businesses interested in tapping the potential of UCR’s cutting-edge research, market-ready technologies, world-renowned faculty, accomplished students, and state-of-the-art facilities.</p> 
     <p>As Inland Southern California’s only public research university, UCR consists of seven schools and colleges including our recently accredited School of Medicine and a new School of Public Policy. The campus contributes $1.4 billion per year to the state of California through research, teaching, employment, investment and community involvement.</p> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Why<span> UCR?</span></h3> 
     <ul class="l_big_arrows"> 
      <li><strong>Recruiting</strong> — Find our graduating students seeking permanent employment opportunities or our continuing students looking for internships.</li> 
      <li><strong>Corporate Sponsored Research and Development</strong> — Fast-track your R &amp; D through partnerships with research groups and/or individual faculty on specialized projects tailored to your interests and needs.</li> 
      <li><strong>Advanced Facilities &amp; Equipment</strong> — UCR Research labs house advanced equipment from scanning electron microscopes to nuclear magnetic resonance. Arrangements can be made for your company to tap into these resources for a fee.</li> 
      <li><strong>Philanthropic Investment</strong> — Support UCR programs educating the next generation of corporate leaders and receive recognition for supporting UCR.</li> 
      <li><strong>Specialized Training</strong> — Invest in your staff to continually secure your organization’s place in the global market. UCR provides an array of certificate programs and specialized classes. We can design classes to meet your individual needs.</li> 
      <li><strong>Services</strong> — UCR can assist your company in many ways including statistical analysis of data and teaching you to search for prior art in patents.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Research Funding<span> Overview</span></h3> 
     <p>In FY 2011-12, we attracted approximately $114 million in research funding, including 867 contracts and grants.</p> 
     <p><img src="/industry/images/research-funding-piechart.png" alt="Research Funding Pie Chart. Click description link below for text-only version." width="452" height="175" /> <a title="Text description" href="/industry/accessibility/research-funding-piechart-desc.html">[d]</a></p> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Recognition<span> Opportunities</span></h3> 
     <p>UCR provides a variety of focused opportunities for your organization to gain visibility and build brand awareness with our extended campus community and industry partners. Through an active presence on campus, companies may enjoy increased exposure through enhanced interaction with our students and faculty, visibility in UCR’s online and printed publications, on-campus signage or through naming opportunities such as facilities, laboratories, endowed chairs and academic programs.</p>

      <h3>English Language Programs</h3> 
      <div class="l_iep_left_marg l_iep_firstp_color" style="width:460px"> 
       <p><strong>The University of California, Riverside International Education Programs offers you a wide variety of short-term and long-term English language programs.</strong> </p>
       <p>Our programs are offered year-round and are designed for all levels of English speakers, from beginner to advanced. You can choose from short, three-week intensive courses to year-long courses that allow you to explore many areas of American culture and the English language. </p> 
       <div class="clear"></div> 
       <a id="l_iep_apply_link_box_left" style="width: 170px;" href="/apply-now/"><span class="accessibility">Interested in English Language Programs, Apply Now</span></a> 
       <div class="clear"></div> 
       <p>Our programs are offered year-round and are designed for all levels of English speakers, from beginner to advanced. You can choose from short, three-week intensive courses to year-long courses that allow you to explore many areas of American culture and the English language. </p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="l_iep_center">
       <img class="l_iep_ben_img_title" src="/images/english/english_programs_img_01.png" alt="Co-operative learning environment with other students" width="290" /> 
       <h3 class="section">Our Programs</h3> 
       <h4><a href="/english/intensive.html">Intensive English Programs (INT)</a></h4> 
       <p>From beginner to advanced levels, the Intensive English Program provides opportunities to focus on language fluency and academic skills.</p> 
       <h4><a href="/english/business.html">English for International Business (EIB)</a></h4> 
       <p>English for International Business is designed for upper-intermediate/advanced level students. The program focuses on improving specific English skills in a business environment and includes site visits to local businesses.</p> 
       <h4><a href="/english/fluency.html">Improving Oral Fluency (IOF)</a></h4> 
       <p>Improving Oral Fluency helps upper-intermediate/advanced level students communicate more effectively by focusing on specific oral skills.</p> 
       <h4><a href="/english/eap.html">English for Academic Purposes (EAP)</a></h4> 
       <p>English for Academic Purposes is designed for advanced-level students preparing to enter a college or university undergraduate program.</p> 
       <h4><a href="/english/culture.html">Conversation and American Culture (CAC)</a></h4> 
       <p>Offered in three-week sessions, the Conversation and American Culture program gives visitors the opportunity to gain confidence in speaking and listening and to experience California and American culture.</p> 
       <h4><a href="/english/summer.html">Summer English Programs (SEP)</a></h4> 
       <p>All of our English language programs have summer options during the months of June, July, August and September.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
      <div class="clear" style="height: 40px;"></div> 
      <div class="l_iep_benefits"> 
       <img class="l_iep_ben_img_title" src="/images/english/english_programs_title_box.png" alt="Benefits of the IEP English Language Program" /> 
       <img class="l_iep_img_floatR" src="/images/english/english_programs_img_02.png" alt="Students in UNEX cafeteria" width="250" /> 
       <h5>Study with American students</h5> 
       <p>If you are a student in the upper-intermediate/advanced level of any 10-week English language program, you have the opportunity to study with American students by taking a UCR Extension class. Programs are subject to availability; selection cannot be guaranteed. You must meet all prerequisite requirements and additional fees apply.</p> 
       <h5>Get University of California credit for your English Program</h5> 
       <p>If you successfully complete the advanced levels of any 10-week English language program, you are eligible to receive University of California undergraduate elective credits, which are normally transferable to any of the ten University of California campuses and to many other universities in the United States.</p> 
       <img class="l_iep_image_positionL" src="/images/english/english_programs_img_03.png" alt="Hollywood and Vine street sign" width="210" /> 
       <h5>Enroll in the American Conversation Partner Program</h5> 
       <p>As a University of California, Riverside IEP 10-week program student, you can request an American Conversation Partner Program. You will be matched with a selected American student or community member for weekly conversations. </p> 
       <p>The American Conversation Partner Program is a great way to meet new friends, learn about the American culture and practice your English. </p> 
       <img class="l_iep_image_positionR" src="/images/english/english_programs_img_04.png" alt="Dolphins swiming in pool" width="240" height="207" /> 
       <h5>Social and Cultural Activities</h5> 
       <p>Whether you only have three weeks to improve your English or are studying for several sessions, we know you will want to see all the famous sights of California. We offer a range of optional social and cultural activities, which may include trips to Disneyland, LA, San Diego, Palm Springs, San Francisco and Las Vegas. We also organize regular social activities so that you have many opportunities to meet and make friends with American students and students from around the world. </p>
