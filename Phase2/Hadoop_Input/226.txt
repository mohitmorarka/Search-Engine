      <p>Application &amp; Multimedia Development specializes in creating and integrating custom online applications. We also offer a robust assortment of graphics services related to application and interface design. But our multimedia services don't just stop there. Take a look at some&nbsp;<a style="color: #2668b2;" title="gallery" href="/amd/amd_graphics.html">examples of our work</a>&nbsp;and you'll find a&nbsp;surprising range of distinct and custom web content, printed material and illustration services. In all respects, we take pride in supporting research, education, administration, marketing and public relations at UCR.&nbsp;Our services are available to faculty, staff, departments, organizations, and special interest groups affiliated with the university.</p>

      <h1>UCR ARTSblock</h1> 
      <br />
      <br /> 
      <p>University of California, Riverside, has recently brought together its three premier art institutions to create a new cultural complex: UCR ARTSblock. ARTSblock is composed of the California Museum of Photography, the Sweeney Art Gallery, and the Barbara and Art Culver Center of the Arts. Located on a single block in adjacent historical buildings in downtown Riverside, easily accessible to the public as well as the campus, ARTSblock's mission is to provide a cultural presence, educational resource, community center and intellectual meeting ground for the university and the community. <br /><br /> ARTSblock's programs investigate the art of our time, media arts, and the history and practice of photography through exhibitions, performances, screenings, workshops, artist residencies, permanent collections, and creative laboratories. ARTSblock presents significant national and international art and photography, and showcases UCR student artwork and regional artists. <br /><br /> ARTSblock's activities embody University of California, Riverside's commitment to broadly-based public education and cutting-edge research. ARTSblock offers innovative programs that engage diverse audiences, nourish the imagination and challenge assumptions.</p>

     <h5> <em>UCR School of Business Administration and The City of Riverside have begun a partnership that gives City employees a tuition reduction.</em></h5> 
     <p> <img alt="CSI MOU" src="http://soba.ucr.edu/images/news/170.png" style="width: 500px; height: 333px; margin: 5px; float: right;" /><em>(January 24, 2014)</em></p> 
     <p> The UCR School of Business Administration (SoBA) and the City of Riverside have signed an agreement that extends a discounted tuition to City employees. City of Riverside employees are eligible to enroll in the Flex MBA, Master of Professional Accountancy (MPAc), or Master of Finance (MFin) graduate business programs at a reduced cost.</p> 
     <p> The memorandum of understanding was signed at the City-University Task Force meeting that included Riverside Mayor Rusty Bailey, UCR Chancellor Kim Wilcox, SoBA Interim Dean Yunzeng Wang, as well as many other city officials and community members.&nbsp;</p> 
     <p> “This agreement marks an important step forward in the advancement and strengthening of our community. We are truly living the promise to invest everything that we can to make Riverside a more prosperous place to live,” states Wang.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
