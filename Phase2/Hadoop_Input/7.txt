     <h3>UCR: The Home of the Highlanders!</h3> 
     <p><img id="highlanders_logo" class="img_R_borderless" src="/athletics/images/ucr_athletics_logo.png" alt="UC Riverside Athletics Logo" width="173" height="178" />The proud history of Highlander athletics dates back to the university's inception in the mid-1950s. Today, UC Riverside’s Intercollegiate Athletics program features 17 sports competing at the NCAA Division I level in the Big West Conference.</p> 
     <h3>Pride in our Academic and Athletic Accomplishments</h3> 
     <p>The Highlanders compete in 17 sports at the NCAA Division I level, the highest level of collegiate competition in the country. We are proud members of the Big West Conference, a group of nine schools all based within the state of California. The Big West is the only conference in the nation that can claim this distinction.</p> 
     <p>UC Riverside has made a splash on the national scene with NCAA post-season appearances in the sports of Baseball, Women's Basketball, Women's Soccer, Men's Golf, Men's and Women's Cross Country and Men's and Women's Track and Field. To learn the latest news about <a href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">UCR Intercollegiate Athletics</a>, check out the website at <a class="external" title="extermal site: Highlander Athletics" href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">www.gohighlanders.com</a>.</p> 
     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <img id="big_west" class="img_R_borderless_close" src="/athletics/images/intercollegiate_big_west.png" alt="Big West Conference" width="195" height="76" /> 
      <h4>The members of the Big West are:</h4> 
      <p>UC Riverside, UC Davis, UC Irvine, UC Santa Barbara, Hawaii, Cal Poly (San Luis Obispo, CA), Cal State Fullerton, Cal State Northridge, Long Beach State University</p> 
     </div> 
     <h3><img id="men_basketball" class="img_R_borderless_close" src="/athletics/images/intercollegiate_basketball.png" alt="Men's basketball" width="201" height="399" />It's more than just &quot;playing games&quot;</h3> 
     <p>The goals of Intercollegiate Athletics at UC Riverside are to create a program that:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Enhances the reputation of the university by striving for athletic and academic excellence.</li> 
      <li>Provides student-athletes with the opportunity for growth outside the classroom.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>The student-athlete's experiences on the practice field, the competition field and in the classroom combine to create a well-rounded individual with a dedication to integrity, teamwork, excellence, personal growth and individual respect.</p> 
     <p>Student-athletes at UCR are held to the same or higher academic standards than ordinary students, abiding by the rules and restrictions of the NCAA and Big West, in addition to the rules of UC Riverside. <strong>Highlander student-athletes often have higher GPA's than their non-athlete counterparts.</strong></p> 
     <div class="clear"></div>

     <h3 class="section">Want to Come Root for Your Highlanders?</h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li><strong>All UCR undergraduate students are admitted for free to any home UCR sporting events</strong> with their student I.D. cards. Just show your card at the ticket booth to get in.</li> 
      <li>For non-students, tickets for UCR home events are available at the venue on the day of the game, online at <a class="external" href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">gohighlanders.com</a> or in advance from the UCR Athletics Ticket Office. For group sales, contact the Ticket Office at (951) 827-4653 or <a href="mailto:highlandertickets@ucr.edu?subject=web%20site%20inquiry">via e-mail.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="http://gohighlanders.com/calendar.aspx">Complete schedules for all</a> 17 UCR teams are available.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <img id="highlanders_mascot" class="img_L_borderless" src="/athletics/images/mascot_01.png" alt="UCR Mascot" width="182" height="245" /> 
     <h3>The UC Riverside Athletics Association:<br /> Helping UCR Athletics</h3> 
     <p>Made up of alumni, friends and family, the <a href="http://athleticsassociation.ucr.edu/">UC&nbsp;Riverside Athletics Association</a> is the booster organization for UCR Athletics. It encourages involvement in and financial support for UCR’s athletics program and the 300 young people who balance the rigors of “student” and “athlete.”</p> 
     <p><a class="button" href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/Givingform.aspx?DU=Athletics">Make a donation now</a></p>

     <h3>UCR: The Home of the Highlanders!</h3> 
     <p><img id="highlanders_logo" class="img_R_borderless" src="/athletics/images/ucr_athletics_logo.png" alt="UC Riverside Athletics Logo" width="173" height="178" />The proud history of Highlander athletics dates back to the university's inception in the mid-1950s. Today, UC Riverside’s Intercollegiate Athletics program features 17 sports competing at the NCAA Division I level in the Big West Conference.</p> 
     <h3>Pride in our Academic and Athletic Accomplishments</h3> 
     <p>The Highlanders compete in 17 sports at the NCAA Division I level, the highest level of collegiate competition in the country. We are proud members of the Big West Conference, a group of nine schools all based within the state of California. The Big West is the only conference in the nation that can claim this distinction.</p> 
     <p>UC Riverside has made a splash on the national scene with NCAA post-season appearances in the sports of Baseball, Women's Basketball, Women's Soccer, Men's Golf, Men's and Women's Cross Country and Men's and Women's Track and Field. To learn the latest news about <a href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">UCR Intercollegiate Athletics</a>, check out the website at <a class="external" title="extermal site: Highlander Athletics" href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">www.gohighlanders.com</a>.</p> 
     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <img id="big_west" class="img_R_borderless_close" src="/athletics/images/intercollegiate_big_west.png" alt="Big West Conference" width="195" height="76" /> 
      <h4>The members of the Big West are:</h4> 
      <p>UC Riverside, UC Davis, UC Irvine, UC Santa Barbara, Hawaii, Cal Poly (San Luis Obispo, CA), Cal State Fullerton, Cal State Northridge, Long Beach State University</p> 
     </div> 
     <h3><img id="men_basketball" class="img_R_borderless_close" src="/athletics/images/intercollegiate_basketball.png" alt="Men's basketball" width="201" height="399" />It's more than just &quot;playing games&quot;</h3> 
     <p>The goals of Intercollegiate Athletics at UC Riverside are to create a program that:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Enhances the reputation of the university by striving for athletic and academic excellence.</li> 
      <li>Provides student-athletes with the opportunity for growth outside the classroom.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>The student-athlete's experiences on the practice field, the competition field and in the classroom combine to create a well-rounded individual with a dedication to integrity, teamwork, excellence, personal growth and individual respect.</p> 
     <p>Student-athletes at UCR are held to the same or higher academic standards than ordinary students, abiding by the rules and restrictions of the NCAA and Big West, in addition to the rules of UC Riverside. <strong>Highlander student-athletes often have higher GPA's than their non-athlete counterparts.</strong></p> 
     <div class="clear"></div> 
     <div class="column2">

     <h3 class="section">Want to Come Root for Your Highlanders?</h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li><strong>All UCR undergraduate students are admitted for free to any home UCR sporting events</strong> with their student I.D. cards. Just show your card at the ticket booth to get in.</li> 
      <li>For non-students, tickets for UCR home events are available at the venue on the day of the game, online at <a class="external" href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">gohighlanders.com</a> or in advance from the UCR Athletics Ticket Office. For group sales, contact the Ticket Office at (951) 827-4653 or <a href="mailto:highlandertickets@ucr.edu?subject=web%20site%20inquiry">via e-mail.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="http://gohighlanders.com/calendar.aspx">Complete schedules for all</a> 17 UCR teams are available.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <img id="highlanders_mascot" class="img_L_borderless" src="/athletics/images/mascot_01.png" alt="UCR Mascot" width="182" height="245" /> 
     <h3>The UC Riverside Athletics Association:<br /> Helping UCR Athletics</h3> 
     <p>Made up of alumni, friends and family, the <a href="http://athleticsassociation.ucr.edu/">UC&nbsp;Riverside Athletics Association</a> is the booster organization for UCR Athletics. It encourages involvement in and financial support for UCR’s athletics program and the 300 young people who balance the rigors of “student” and “athlete.”</p>

     <div class="col col-lrg" id="col-2">
      <h2>Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan</h2>
      <div class="intro">
       <p>The Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan is a financial aid program intended to expand access to UC for lower-income students.</p>
      </div>
      <p>UC's Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan will ensure that you will not have to pay UC’s systemwide tuition and fees out of your own pocket if you are a California resident whose total family income is less than $80,000 a year and you qualify for financial aid — and that's just for starters.</p>
      <p>Blue and Gold students with sufficient financial need can qualify for even more grant aid to help reduce the cost of attending.</p>
      <p><strong>Type of aid</strong>: <a href="../../how-aid-works/grants-and-scholarships/index.html">Gift aid</a> (from multiple sources)</p>
      <p><strong>How to apply</strong>: <a href="../../apply-for-aid/index.html">FAFSA/California Dream Act Application + Cal Grant GPA Verification Form</a> (you will be automatically considered when you submit these forms by March 2; there is no separate application for this program) </p>
      <div class="primary">
       <h3>What's covered</h3>
       <p> If you are eligible, your systemwide tuition and fees will be fully covered by scholarship or grant money. The plan combines all sources of scholarship and grant awards you receive (federal, state, UC and private) to go toward covering your tuition and fees. </p>
       <p>Students with greater financial need can qualify for even more grant support to help defray other educational expenses (like books, housing, transportation, etc.). </p>
       <p>You don't need to fill out a separate application to qualify for the Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan. You will receive the benefits of the Blue and Gold plan automatically if you qualify. </p>
      </div>
      <div class="primary">
       <h3>Eligibility requirements</h3>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Submit a FAFSA or California Dream Act Application and Cal Grant GPA Verification Form by March 2</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">California resident or have <a href="../../tuition-and-cost/ab540/index.html">AB 540 status</a></li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Demonstrate total family income below $80,000 and financial need, as determined for federal need-based aid programs</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Be in your first four years as a UC undergraduate (first two for transfer students)</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Meet other campus basic requirements for UC grant aid (for example, be enrolled at least half-time during the academic year, meet campus academic progress standards, not be in default on student loans, etc.)</li>
       </ul>
      </div>

     <h2 class="intro big"> Transfers</h2> 
     <img class="centerImg header" alt="" width="582" height="247" src="/Images/header_transfer.jpg" /> 
     <div class="grid_10 prefix_1 suffix_1 alpha omega"> 
      <div class="clearfix centerTxt"> 
       <h3 class="verd intro grey" style="margin-bottom: 0;"> Guaranteed admission, financial aid, how to apply<br /> and just being reassured that you belong at UCR. </h3> 
       <a class="rightArrowCTA xlarge" href="/Content/PDFs/checklist_transfer.pdf">Print a Transfer Checklist</a> 
      </div> 
      <hr class="style1" style="margin-bottom: 30px;" /> 
      <div class="genericBody version2 verd clearfix"> 
       <div class="grid_5 alpha"> 
        <h4> How to Transfer </h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>See UCR’s&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Admissions/pathsAdmissions#transferStudents">transfer admission and major requirements.</a></li> 
         <li>Use the <a href="http://admission.universityofcalifornia.edu/transfer/transfer-admission-planner/index.html">Transfer Admission Planner (TAP)</a> to plan and track your course work.</li> 
         <li>Learn about our&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/WhyUCR/ourGuarantee#transferStudents">Transfer Admission Guarantee (TAG)</a> program.</li> 
         <li>Explore <a href="http://www.assist.org/web-assist/welcome.html">ASSIST</a> to <a href="http://web1.assist.org/web-assist/help/help-transferable_courses.html">find out if your courses will transfer</a> and <a href="http://www2.assist.org/exploring-majors/findMajorListByInstitutionAbbrev.do?abbrev=UCR">see prerequisites for your intended major</a>.&nbsp;&nbsp; </li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4> How You Can Afford It</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Find out how&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/PayingForCollege/costEstimator">how affordable</a>&nbsp;it is to attend UCR.</li> 
         <li>Discover the&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/PayingForCollege/financialAidScholarships">many ways</a>&nbsp;UCR makes college possible, regardless of financial challenges.</li> 
         <li>See if you qualify for our <a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=357">Re-entry Scholarship</a>.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4> How You Will Fit In</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Meet&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/WhyUCR/ourStudents">our students.</a></li> 
         <li>Plug right in to&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife">campus life.</a></li> 
         <li>Live in our&nbsp;<a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/liveUCR/special-interest-housing.aspx">Transfer Student Theme Hall</a>, <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/housing-options/campus-apartments/default.aspx">campus apartments</a> or <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/housing-options/family-student-housing.aspx">family housing</a>.</li> 
         <li>Come to <a href="http://highlanderday.ucr.edu/">Highlander Day.</a></li> 
         <li>Join transfer student organizations such as the <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/tausigma">Tau Sigma National Honor Society</a>, <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/tsa">Transfer Student Association</a> and the <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/puenteconnection">Puente Connection</a> club (once enrolled).</li> 
         <li>Schedule&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR/tourOptions">a campus tour</a>&nbsp;or take a <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR/virtualTours">virtual tour</a>&nbsp;right now.</li> 
        </ul> 
       </div> 
       <div class="grid_5 omega"> 
        <h4> How to Apply</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>See a&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Apply">step-by-step guide</a>&nbsp;to the application process.</li> 
         <li><a href="/Content/PDFs/checklist_transfer.pdf">Print a Transfer Checklist</a> for a detailed timeline.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4> How We Support You</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Academics/supportServices">See support services</a> for military, those with special needs and more.</li> 
         <li>Discover <a href="/Academics/transferprep">unique transfer programs</a> including the Smart Start Transfer Academy and Puente Connection.</li> 
         <li>Find out how we boost your <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife/safetyWellness">health and wellness</a>. </li> 
         <li>Discover our unique <a href="http://www.ucr.edu/students/ethnic_gender.html">Ethnic &amp; Gender Programs.</a></li> 
         <li>Learn about free tutoring at the <a href="http://arc.ucr.edu/">Academic Resource Center.</a></li> 
         <li>Explore&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Academics/honorsPrograms">honors opportunities</a>&nbsp;for transfer students.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4> How to Stay Connected</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Like us on <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ucradmissions" target="_blank">Facebook</a>.</li> 
         <li>Follow us on <a href="https://twitter.com/ucradmissions" target="_blank">Twitter</a>.</li> 
         <li>Follow us on <a href="http://lifeatucr.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">Tumblr</a>.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4> How to Get Help</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a href="mailto:transfer@ucr.edu">Email a transfer admissions counselor.</a></li> 
         <li>See answers to&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Home/FAQ">frequently asked questions.</a></li> 
        </ul> 
       </div>
