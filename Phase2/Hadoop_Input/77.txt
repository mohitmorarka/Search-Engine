       <p class="p1">If anyone can make Ellen laugh, it's WANDA SYKES! The standup comedian returns to tell our host more hilarious stories of life on the road. When they’re together, Ellen and Wanda can get into almost any subject, from the kinks of getting older (and the bathroom struggles that come with it), to family life and day-to-day mishaps. Wanda puts it out there fresh and honest, and she's been doing it that way since she broke into the business opening for Chris Rock. There's always something new from Wanda, and Ellen's ready for another round!</p> 
       <p class="p1">Then, JORDANA BREWSTER makes her debut on the show! The &quot;Fast &amp; Furious&quot; star has been sizzling on the popular &quot;Dallas&quot; redo, adding drama and flair as one of the hottest female action stars in the business. Jordana will have a lot to share from her evolving career when she stops by for the first time!</p>

The acrobatics and surprises characteristic of the group are coupled with the soul of one of music's most revered legends. This time, what happens in Vegas, happens on Ellen's stage!</p>
