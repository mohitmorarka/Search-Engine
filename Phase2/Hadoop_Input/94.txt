      <div class="span4 hero-unit"> 
       <p>We oversee human resources programs and policies for the UC system as a whole.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span4">
       <p>The Office of Human Resources is responsible for systemwide human resources and programs. This includes strategic planning, employee and labor relations, talent management, staff development, senior management recruitment, compensation, performance management and the development and maintenance of staff personnel policies with the intent of ensuring compliance in all areas. We oversee all aspects of universitywide health and welfare programs and policies as well as administration of UC retirement operations. <a href="mission-strategic-plan/index.html">Learn more</a></p>

      <h3>Meet Advising Requirements Before Registering</h3> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-11"> 
      <p>You may have to complete certain requirements before registering. Common requirements range from meeting with an advisor to submitting goals for the quarter.</p> 
      <p>You can see advising requirements that apply to you in <a href="https://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html" target="_blank">Growl</a> once online registration appointments become visible. Check the <a href="/registrar/academic-calendar/default.aspx">Academic Calendar</a> for that date and time. </p> 
      <p>Complete all of your advising requirements before your registration appointment so that you can register at the assigned time. If you have any questions about an advising requirement, please contact the department that initiated it.</p> 
      <p> <img src="/Images/bulb.png" style="float: left; margin: 6px; margin-top: 0px;" alt="" /> <span class="warning">Important:</span><br /> Submit documents to advisors early to allow time for review. Submissions are prioritized according to enrollment dates. When your work has been reviewed, you will receive a notification via Rmail.</p>
