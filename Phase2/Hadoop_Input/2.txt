     <h3>The Path to Success</h3> 
     <p>There’s a reason why UC Riverside routinely <a href="/about/rankingsandfacts.html">ranks among the top public universities</a> in the country. We have world-class faculty members who challenge, stimulate and inspire. We offer 88 majors, 56 minors, 48 master's programs and 17 state-approved credential programs, including a number of up-and-coming departments and initiatives. Read on to learn more about some of the <a href="#resources">resources</a> and <a href="#opportunities">opportunities</a> available to students.</p> 
     <div class="boxD"> 
      <h3><a name="resources"> </a>Resources</h3> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.childrenservices.ucr.edu/">Academic Resource Center</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/academic_resources_center2.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />The <a href="http://www.learningcenter.ucr.edu/">Academic Resource Center</a> is comprised of a team of educators, counselors and advanced students. You will find everything you need to help you succeed in your college classes. </p> 
      <p>Services available include:</p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li>Drop in tutoring.</li> 
       <li>Study skills.</li> 
       <li>Supplemental instruction.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/resources/libraries/">Libraries</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/libraries_thumb.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />UC Riverside libraries are among the UC's most complete and are among the largest academic research libraries in the United States. With two million volumes, 28,000 print and electronic serial subscriptions, 1.7 million microforms, 300,000 government information sources, and access to 165,000 e-books, we’re ready to connect you with the resources you need.<a href="/resources/libraries/"> Gain access to the resources you need.</a></p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/academics/">Majors, Minors &amp; Programs</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/majors_thumb.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />Within our three schools (College of Engineering, College of Humanities, Arts and Sciences, and College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences), the university offers majors ranging from art to anthropology, botany to business administration, English to environmental science, and mechanical engineering to music. UC Riverside isn't just a world-class university, it's your college of choice. <a href="/academics/">Find out more about our academic programs.</a></p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/academics/resources.html">Student Resources</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/students_thumb.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />Whether it’s accessing GROWL, checking the Academic Calendar, contacting an academic adviser, registering for classes or getting a schedule of classes, <a href="/academics/resources.html">we've put all your essential resources in one place</a>.</p>
     </div> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h3><a name="opportunities"> </a> Opportunities</h3> 
      <h4 class="section"><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/">Summer Sessions</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/summer_thumb.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />The UCR Summer Sessions program offers eight different sessions ranging from three-weeks to 10-weeks in length. Courses are held during the day and evening. These courses are identical in content to those taught during the regular school year. With 450 courses available, you can take those high-demand classes in a more relaxed atmosphere. <a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/">Find out more about enrolling into Summer Session courses.</a></p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a href="http://summerstudyabroad.ucr.edu/">Summer Study Abroad Program </a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/summer_abroad2.jpg" alt="" width="93" height="46" />Experience spending the summer abroad while staying on track to graduate by taking UCR courses through the Summer Study Abroad Program. The program offers UCR general catalog courses taught by UCR faculty in an international location during a five-week session. <a href="http://summerstudyabroad.ucr.edu/generalinfo.html">Learn more about this unique opportunity to spend your summer studying abroad. </a></p>

     <h3>Getting the Most Out of UCR</h3> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4><a class="standout2" href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife/housing">Housing Services</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/housing_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife/housing">UCR Housing Services</a> is committed to providing our students with safe, convenient, comfortable housing. We offer three housing styles to meet the needs of all students. Whether our students choose to live in residence halls, campus apartments or family housing, each provides the opportunity to participate in a variety of activities that will enrich their college experience. In addition, our campus police force, which patrols 24 hours a day, and our on-call housing staff are committed to providing a safe and secure environment.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://dining.ucr.edu/">Dining Services</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/dining_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://dining.ucr.edu/">UCR Dining Services</a> offers more than great food – we provide a place where our students can take a break and gather with friends to socialize, study and relax. Whether it’s in the dining halls or our retail locations, we pride ourselves in providing a wide variety of high-quality, authentic and healthy options daily in a friendly, welcoming environment.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/">Fees &amp; Financial Aid</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/fees_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Regardless of your income level, a wide range of <a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/">financial aid</a> options put UCR excellence within reach. In fact, 70 percent of all UCR students receive some form of financial aid. UCR encourages you to investigate all opportunities for financial assistance, including grants, loans and scholarships. See our <a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/UCRCost/Pages/default.aspx">annual budget of projected costs</a> and learn more about financial aid options. In addition, our staff in <a href="http://www.sbs.ucr.edu/">Student Business Services</a> can assist you with everything from payment and cashier services to loan and debt counseling.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://registrar.ucr.edu/">Registrar</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/registrar_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />The <a href="http://registrar.ucr.edu/">Office of the Registrar</a> is responsible for student registration, publishes class schedules, maintains academic records and distributes diplomas. It also upholds <a href="http://registrar.ucr.edu/registrar/privacy-ferpa/family.aspx">The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)</a>, which governs and protects students’ rights to education records.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://ucrcard.ucr.edu/">R'Card</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/rcard.png" alt=" R'card" width="93" height="46" />The <a href="http://ucrcard.ucr.edu/">R'Card</a> is your student’s single most important piece of student identification. But it’s also the quickest, most convenient way to pay for things on campus and access services. This multi-purpose ID allows students to gain access to buildings, pay for meals, borrow books from the library, ride public transportation for free, and use vending services – just to name a few benefits.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/resources/libraries/">Libraries</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/libraries_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><a href="/resources/libraries/">UCR's libraries</a> are among the UC’s most complete and among the largest academic research libraries in the United States. With 2 million volumes, 28,000 print and electronic serial subscriptions, 1.7 million microforms, 300,000 government information sources, and access to 165,000 e-books, we’re ready to connect our students with the resources they need.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/academics/academic_advising.html">Academic Advising</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/advising_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Whether your student is an incoming freshman, or a senior trying to figure out his or her next step, there are <a href="/academics/academic_advising.html">academic advisors</a> at UCR to help them figure out the right path to success.</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a href="http://www.success.ucr.edu/">The First Year Success series</a> and <a href="http://firstyear.ucr.edu/learning.html">First Year Learning Communities</a> are designed to help students navigate their first year at UCR, explore what the university has to offer, meet new friends – all while excelling academically.</li> 
       <li>Our <a href="http://www.learningcenter.ucr.edu/">Learning Center</a> is a team of educators, counselors, and advanced students who provide a variety of free services to help all students succeed in their classes.</li> 
       <li>Our <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">Career Center </a>offers counseling to help students decide what major fits them and what careers suit them.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p>Our goal is that our students always have someone to talk to and help them successfully navigate their years at UCR.</p>

     <p><img src="/giving/images/main/areas_support.jpg" alt=" " width="484" height="144" /></p> 
     <p><strong><img class="img_L_borderless" src="/giving/images/ttl_areas_support.png" alt="Where to Support" width="157" height="131" />Through a tradition of service and excellence</strong>, UC Riverside offers donors and supporters many choices and opportunities to make a difference through their philanthropic investments. The links below will provide you with information on particular giving areas as well as links to additional information and online giving form. You can also learn more about the <a href="/giving/ucr-fund.html">UCR Fund</a> as well as <a href="/giving/meet_telefund.html">meet the students</a> who make up our calling team.</p> 
     <ul class="l_expander_border"> 
      <li> <h4>School of Medicine</h4> <p>Projected to enroll its first incoming class in fall 2012, California’s first new public medical school in four decades will be built in the region with one of the state’s lowest doctor-to-resident ratios, taking on the unique health challenges of a diverse and rapidly growing population.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/giving/">Learn more about the UCR School of Medicine</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG40%20-%20School%20of%20Medicine">Make a gift to the UCR School of Medicine</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>College of Humanities, Arts, &amp; Social Sciences</h4> <p>The College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences (CHASS), the largest and most diverse college at UC Riverside, is home to more than half of the undergraduates at UCR and nearly 700 graduate students. Graduates leave with the confidence to go anywhere and explore anything. As our motto states, they are “at home in the world.” CHASS is also the home of UCR ARTSblock, a regional performing and visual arts center in downtown Riverside made up of the Barbara and Art Culver Center of the Arts, the UCR/California Museum of Photography and the Sweeney Art Gallery.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://chass.ucr.edu/giving/">Learn more about giving to the College of Humanities, Arts, &amp; Social Sciences</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG12%20-%20Coll%20of%20Hum,%20Arts%20and%20Social%20Sciences">Make a gift to CHASS</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>College of Natural &amp; Agricultural Sciences</h4> <p>UC Riverside’s College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences is unique among land-grant university colleges in that we combine life sciences, physical sciences and mathematical sciences under one umbrella. This creates a strong interdisciplinary environment and has positioned the college to move to the forefront of the nation's research universities with a comprehensive range of fundamental and applied sciences.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://cnas.ucr.edu/supporting_cnas/">Learn more about giving to the College of Natural and Agricultural Resources</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG14%20-%20College%20of%20Nat%20and%20Agr%20Sciences">Make a gift to CNAS</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Graduate School of Education</h4> <p>The Graduate School of Education is a national leader in educational research. Our faculty members are among the nation’s top experts in the areas of learning disabilities, special education and autism.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://www.education.ucr.edu/support.html">Learn more about giving to the Graduate School of Education</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?du=ORG10%20-%20School%20of%20Education">Make a gift to GSOE</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Bourns College of Engineering</h4> <p>Support from a variety of sources - including grants and gifts from individuals, corporations, and foundations - make the difference in allowing the Bourns College of Engineering to maintain its growth in capabilities and excellence.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://www.engr.ucr.edu/giving/">Learn more about giving to the Bourns College of Engineering</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG11%20-%20Bourns%20College%20of%20Engineering">Make a gift to BCOE</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Libraries</h4> <p>The UCR Libraries serve the research and teaching needs of the campus and also serve the community as the regional research library for the Inland Empire. We are a member of the Association of Research Libraries and part of the consortium of University of California Libraries that support and benefit from the California Digital Library.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://library.ucr.edu/?view=about">Learn more about the UCR Libraries</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG16+-+University+Library">Make a gift to the UCR Libraries</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>UCR Athletics</h4> <p>Generous donations from alumni, family and friends, to UCR Athletics through the Highlander Athletics Association help provide the critical financial support required for UCR to provide life changing educational experiences for more than 300 exceptional student-athletes.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://athleticsassociation.ucr.edu/">Learn more about the Highlander Athletics Association and UCR Athletics</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/Givingform.aspx?DU=Athletics">Make a gift to UCR Athletics</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>School of Business Administration</h4> <p>The School of Business Administration trains the future business leaders of California. Our undergraduate program is the largest in the UC system and our graduate programs continue to offer a variety of high-quality degrees, including MBAs, Executive MBAs and soon, a new Ph.D. program.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://soba.ucr.edu/">Learn more about the School of Business Administration</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG13%20-%20School%20of%20Business%20Administration">Make a gift to the School of Business Administration</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>UCR Extension</h4> <p>UC Riverside Extension operates independently as a nonprofit division of UCR. For more than 50 years UCR Extension has provided educational opportunities to people of all ages — with or without a college degree — seeking professional advancement, personal fulfillment or a new career.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/admin/about/">Learn more about UCR Extension</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG15%20-%20University%20Extension">Make a gift to UCR Extension</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>UCR Palm Desert Center</h4> <p>UCR Palm Desert Center is Coachella Valley’s site where students, faculty, supporters, and friends gather to advance knowledge and wisdom; tackle society’s challenges; and pursue intellectual and personal fulfillment.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="http://palmdesert.ucr.edu/support/">Learn more about UCR Palm Desert Center</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=ORG35%20-%20Palm%20Desert%20Center">Make a gift to UCR Palm Desert Center</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Scholarships</h4> <p>Gifts towards UCR scholarship funds play a critical role in enabling the best and brightest students to reach their potential and achieve their dreams.</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="/giving/scholarship.html">Learn more about giving to scholarships at UCR</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/ScholarshipGivingForm.aspx">Make a gift to a scholarship</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li> <h4>University-Wide Giving Opportunities</h4> <p>Rather than supporting a particular college, school or program, these gifts support the university and at a higher level, including scholarship and general fund opportunities..</p> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li><a href="/giving/universitywide.html">Learn more about university-wide giving opportunities.</a></li> 
       </ul> </li> 
     </ul>

     <p> <img src="/faculty/images/why_main.jpg" alt="Why UC Riverside?" width="484" height="180" /> </p> 
     <h3>Our Campus</h3> 
     <p> UCR transforms lives. The university has a reputation as a place where knowledge-seekers can thrive, including students who are the first in their families to go to college and graduate students who are well into defining their life's work. UCR faculty provide the launching pad for another generation of leaders - people who will make a difference not just in the region, but in the world. </p> 
     <p> At UCR, we offer our faculty: </p> 
     <ul> 
      <li><img class="img_R" src="/faculty/images/faculty.jpg" alt="At UCR, we offer our faculty:" width="165" height="165" />State-of-the art facilities, laboratories and offices.</li> 
      <li>Proximity to Los Angeles, San Diego and Orange county's metropolitan areas at a much lower cost of living.</li> 
      <li>Multitudes of support services.</li> 
      <li>A location close to recreation areas in the mountains, the desert and the beach. </li> 
     </ul> 
     <p> The university exists as a living laboratory where scholars can delve deep into issues facing California and the world. From issues like clean air and water and safe and secure food supplies, to the development of groundbreaking technologies are all woven into the fabric of the UCR learning experience. </p> 
     <ul> 
      <li><a class="standout2" href="/faculty/resources.html">Learn more about the resources available to faculty</a> </li> 
      <li><a class="standout2" href="/faculty/rankings.html">Learn more about how our faculty and university rank</a></li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section">Our Research</h3> 
     <p> <img class="img_R" src="/faculty/images/reasearch.jpg" alt="Our Research" width="165" height="165" />UCR's innovative discoveries carry with them the economic, scientific and social impacts that make the rest of the world stand up and take notice. UCR is a place where dedicated researchers get the support and resources they need to make great strides. </p> 
     <p> Even with those lofty goals, UCR remains accessible to students seeking an excellent education from dedicated and respected faculty. It provides an affordable option to faculty members who want to continue their work in California, with close access to the cultural amenities of major metropolitan areas nearby. </p> 
     <h3 class="section">Our People</h3> 
     <p> <img class="img_R" src="/faculty/images/people.jpg" alt="Our People" width="165" height="165" />Our 850 faculty members run the gamut from established professors in the prime of their research to up-and-coming academics who are bringing exciting new ideas to established fields of study. </p> 
     <p> UCR's faculty are known throughout the world as experts in their fields of study. Seven current and former faculty members have been elected to the <a class="external" href="http://www.nasonline.org">National Academy of Sciences</a>, which honors distinguished scholars for their work to advance science and technology and for helping society as a whole. </p> 
     <p> <a class="external" href="http://www.gf.org">Guggenheim Fellowships</a>, grants that help provide researchers with time - from six months to a year -- in which they can explore their chosen discipline in depth, have been awarded to more than 50 UCR faculty members. </p> 
     <p> More than 100 UCR faculty members have been elected fellows of the <a class="external" href="http://www.aaas.org">American Association for the Advancement of Science</a>, a recognition of how these faculty members have made notable contributions to the advancement of science and its role in society. </p> 
     <p> <a class="standout2" href="/faculty/benefits.html">Learn about the benefits available to UCR Faculty.</a> </p> 
     <div class="profile"> 
      <img class="border_image" src="/faculty/images/faculty_profile.png" alt="Faculty Profile" width="484" height="50" /> 
      <h3 class="accessibility">Faculty Profile</h3> 
      <h4>Norm Ellstrand and Tracy Kahn</h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/faculty/images/Ellstrand_Kahn.jpg" alt="Norm Ellstrand and Tracy Kahn" width="213" height="146" />UCR is more than just a place where solitary researchers reach their goals of personal discovery and professional advancement. The university is home to an increasing number of husband-wife faculty members, some of whom work together in the same department, often within walking distance of one another's labs. </p> 
      <p> Norman Ellstrand, a professor of genetics and director of the Biotechnology Impacts Center, and Tracy Kahn, a lecturer in the Department of Botany and Plant Sciences and curator of the UCR Citrus Variety Collection, have been married for 25 years. </p> 
      <p> They met when he was an assistant professor and she was a graduate student. Now they are both established members of the department. </p> 
      <p> &quot;There are just three doors between us,&quot; said Kahn, a lecturer in the Department of Botany and Plant Sciences and the curator of the UCR Citrus Variety Collection. Ellstrand is a professor of genetics in the same department. &quot;We feel very lucky. I know his grad students and he knows mine. We enjoy each other's company and give each other added perspective on things.&quot; </p>
