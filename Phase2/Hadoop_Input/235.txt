      <div class="spotlight"> 
       <h3>Why a CMS?</h3> 
       <p>The UC Riverside Content Management System (CMS) is designed with two purposes in mind: The first directive is to allow campus units to develop and maintain timely and relevant web resources, and the second objective is to allow UCR to develop a common and consistent method of displaying content on the internet.</p> 
      </div> 
      <h3>Benefits of the CMS</h3> 
      <p>The UCR CMS fulfills both of these directives. The CMS gives campus constituents access to a library of high-quality web templates, and makes web editing as simple as using a word processing program. Technical staff retain the ability to develop complex and dynamic web solutions, while others gain the ability to effortlessly maintain their websites.</p> 
      <p>In addition, the UCR CMS will offer a host of features enabling campus units to easily provide access to news, directory &amp; campus calendar information, multimedia content, and emergency notifications.</p>

       <h3>Mission&nbsp;of the UCR Alumni Association</h3> The UCR Alumni Association serves to preserve and promote the relationships between UCR and all its alumni. Through its various services and programs, the Association represents alumni and assists the University in its quest for excellence.

       <h3>UCR Alumni Association Board of Directors</h3> The UCR Alumni Association Board of Directors is a dedicated group of volunteers which establishes Association policy, reviews programs, and provides feedback and suggestions to the University administration. 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=304">Find out who your alumni leaders are and apply to serve as a member on the board.</a>

ARTSblock also offers programs initiated by faculty and students that bring distinguished artists to UCR. ARTSblock supports faculty and students by providing performance and exhibition space, technical expertise, publicity, and grant application support. Culver Arts Research Laboratories (CARL) Residencies further provide modest financial support to faculty and students who are awarded CARL grants. All these ARTSblock programs help inform the Riverside and Southern California audience about significant artistic research in the twenty departments that comprise the College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences. Other UCR colleges and departments have also been featured at ARTSblock. <br /><br /> <iframe width="560" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/jAlqqImxr_A" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe> <br /> Excerpts from exhibitions and performances that have occurred in the atrium of UCR ARTSblock's Culver Center of the Arts since 2010. Please note that some excerpts do not have sound. <br /><br /><br /> Click on a headings below to view past collaborations between UCR departments and ARTSblock. </p>
