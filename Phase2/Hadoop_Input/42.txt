      <h3>Live and learn in exciting Southern California!</h3> 
      <p> The University of California, Riverside is one of the world’s most highly ranked academic institutions, as well as one of the most diverse and friendly universities in the United States. As a member of the prestigious University of California, UC Riverside offers a world-class education to international students from more than 60 countries. UC Riverside’s 1,200 acre park-like campus is a short distance from Hollywood, Disneyland, snowy mountain resorts and Southern California’s legendary beaches! </p> 
      <br /> 
      <div class="thinBorderBottom"></div> 
      <br /> 
      <h3>Come to UC Riverside</h3> 
      <ul class="yellowBullet"> 
       <li>Perform undergraduate research at one of UC Riverside’s 24 nationally recognized, technologically advanced research centers and institutes.</li> 
       <li>Study and earn a respected University of California, Riverside degree, ranked among the top universities for research impact, and featuring the highest ranked engineering school for its size.</li> 
       <li>Learn from UC Riverside’s 900 prominent scholars and researchers <br />— 98 percent of whom have earned Ph.D.’s.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <div class="thinBorderBottom"></div> 
      <br /> 
      <h3>International Admission Requirements</h3> 
      <ul class="yellowBullet"> 
       <li>You are an <strong>international applicant</strong> if you need a visa to live and study in the United States.</li> 
       <li>You are a <strong>freshman applicant</strong> if you are still in secondary school or have never enrolled at a university.</li> 
       <li>You are a <strong>transfer applicant</strong> if you registered at a university or college after completing secondary school.</li> 
       <li>Permanent <strong>U.S. citizens</strong>, who have completed course work abroad, should follow the international admissions requirements for the course work that has been completed outside of the United States.</li> 
      </ul>

         <h3 class="clkr top">Academic Affairs</h3>
         <div>
          <div class="row-fluid">
           <div class="span4 hero-unit">
            <p>We are a systemwide resource for academic excellence in research, teaching and public service.</p>
           </div>
           <div class="span4">
            <p>The Office of the Provost builds on the energy and academic strength of UC’s campuses to enhance the system’s educational impact. We help recruit and retain outstanding professors and provide them with the support they need to succeed. We promote excellence through programs that help prepare, recruit and support undergraduate, graduate and professional students. We manage programs that help position UC and California as engines of economic, social and cultural development. And we ensure that UC remains on the cutting edge of innovation in teaching and learning by supporting new academic initiatives.</p>

           <div>

We have universitywide responsibility for academic appointments and compensation and serve as steward for the university’s Academic Personnel policies covering all academic appointments: faculty, research and health science clinical faculty, lecturers, librarians, graduate students and postdoctoral scholars.            <h4 class="clkr">Agriculture and Natural Resources</h4>
           <div>
            <div class="row-fluid">
             <div class="span4 hero-unit">
              <p>We are the bridge between local issues and the power of UC research.</p>
             </div>
             <div class="span4">
              <p>The Division of Agriculture and Natural Resources (ANR) is a statewide network of UC researchers and educators dedicated to the creation, development and application of knowledge in agricultural, natural and human resources. ANR's advisors, specialists and faculty bring practical, science-based answers to Californians. We work hand in hand with industry to enhance agricultural markets, help the balance of trade, address environmental concerns, protect plant health, and provide farmers with scientifically tested production techniques and Californians with increased food safety.</p>

             <h5 class="clkr">Research Grants Program Office</h5>
             <div>
              <div class="row-fluid">
               <div class="span4 hero-unit">
                <p>We fund research to address some of California's most critical needs.</p>
               </div>
               <div class="span4">
                <p>The Research Grants Program Office (RGPO) oversees a broad grantmaking portfolio of nearly $65 million a year to support research that is critical to California, the nation and the world. RGPO programs enhance UC’s research capacity and excellence, which helps attract top faculty, graduate students, government funding and companies to our state. These grants also enable researchers and community agencies to collaborate and solve the most pressing problems in the state. RGPO also provides grants for training undergraduates, graduate and postdoctoral researchers, whose work will benefit California communities.&nbsp;<a href="/research-grants-program/about-research-grants-program-office-rgpo.html" title="About RGPO">Learn more</a></p>

             <h5 class="clkr">Natural Reserve System</h5>
             <div>
              <div class="row-fluid">
               <div class="span4 hero-unit">
                <p>We provide a network of protected natural areas throughout California.</p>
               </div>
               <div class="span4">
                <p>The Natural Reserve System aims to contribute to the understanding and wise stewardship of the Earth and its natural systems by supporting university-level teaching, research, and public service at protected natural areas throughout California. &nbsp;Our 38 sites include more than 750,000 acres, making it the largest university-administered reserve system in the world. Most major state ecosystems are represented, from coastal tidepools to inland deserts, and lush wetlands to Sierra Nevada forests.</p>

We also support <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/ott/" target="_blank">technology transfer</a> operations by providing information systems, financial management, patent prosecution and intellectual property management services.</p>
