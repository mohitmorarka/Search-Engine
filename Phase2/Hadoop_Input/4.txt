     <p> <img src="/students/images/computer_main.jpg" alt="Computer &amp; Technology Recources" width="484" height="180" /> </p> 
     <h3>Get Connected</h3> 
     <p> UCR and <a href="http://www.cnc.ucr.edu/">Computing and Communications</a> (C&amp;C) offer a wide variety of services to expand and enrich the educational opportunities of UCR students. From the <a href="http://ilearn.ucr.edu/">iLearn </a>learning management system to the on-campus wireless network, the face of student computing and technology at UCR is always changing to meet the academic needs of UCR students and to promote the exchange and creation of knowledge and ideas. </p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4><a class="standout2" href="http://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html">GROWL</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/growl_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Look up grades, confirm courses, pay fees and accept financial aid awards. You can do it all on <a href="http://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html">GROWL</a>, UCR's online record-keeping system. </p> 
      <p> <a href="http://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html">GROWL</a> allows students to: </p> 
      <div class="column2"> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li>Enroll and add/drop classes. </li> 
        <li>Check their degree audit. </li> 
        <li>Change their address information. </li> 
        <li>Look up grades. </li> 
        <li>View/accept financial aid awards. </li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul class="tight"> 
        <li>View their current bill. </li> 
        <li>Confirm their courses. </li> 
        <li>View their Student ID. </li> 
        <li>Set their privacy restrictions. </li> 
        <li>Change their PERM PIN. </li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"> 
      </div> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/iguide/">iGuide for for New Students</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/iguide_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />From computer lab locations and iLearn to setting up a R'Mail account, <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/iguide/">UCR's iGuide</a> has everything new students need to know about the technology and computer resources at their fingertips. We have also included links to information about computing resources available through other organizations on campus, such as the RESNET and the library. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.cnc.ucr.edu/index.php?content=student_services/network_services/resnet/">Residence Hall Networking</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/networking_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Technology and technological support are more important than ever to student success. The Residential Housing technology team, with support from Computing and Communications, has developed <a href="http://www.cnc.ucr.edu/index.php?content=student_services/network_services/resnet/">RESNET</a> a comprehensive approach designed to ensure <a href="http://www.cnc.ucr.edu/index.php?content=student_services/network_services/resnet/">all students have immediate access to the university's high-speed network</a>. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://ilearn.ucr.edu/">iLearn</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/ilearn_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://ilearn.ucr.edu/">iLearn</a> is your online access to class notes, discussion boards, announcements and other class materials. Most, if not all, of your classes will have content posted on <a href="http://ilearn.ucr.edu/">iLearn</a>. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/scs/complabs.html">Computer Labs</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/labs_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Whether you prefer to work on a PC or a Mac, just need to type a Word document, or need to edit a film project in Final Cut Pro, the <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/scs/complabs.html">student computer labs</a> are here to support you. We offer 200 computers in six computer lab locations open a total of 285 hours per week. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://library.ucr.edu/?view=help/technology/software.html">Library Computer Resources</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/laptop_thumb.jpg" alt="." width="93" height="46" />The UCR Libraries offer <a href="http://library.ucr.edu/?view=help/technology/software.html">public computers loaded with software</a> as well as <a href="http://library.ucr.edu/?view=services/laptops">laptop computers that can be checked out</a> and used within the library. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/wireless/">Wireless Network</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/students/images/wireless_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />With nearly 800 wireless access points, UCR has an <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/wireless/">extensive wireless network</a> that continues to expand and improve to better serve the UCR community. </p>

     <h3>Providing a Safe &amp; Healthy Campus</h3> 
     <p> Student safety is a top priority at UC Riverside. In addition to the <a href="http://www.police.ucr.edu/">UCR Police Department’s</a> professional law enforcement and patrol services, we also have a variety of programs to ensure your child’s safety, including a <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/ens/">sophisticated emergency notification system</a>. </p> 
     <p> We know that with all that college life demands, it can be tough for students to maintain a healthy mind, body and spirit. At UCR, we’re committed to keeping our students well and improving their physical, emotional, social and academic well-being. We know that when our students feel their best, they can do their best. </p> 
     <p> Our <a href="http://campushealth.ucr.edu/Services">Campus Health Center</a>, <a href="http://counseling.ucr.edu/">Counseling Center</a>, and <a href="/resources/rec_center.html">Student Recreation Center</a> are resources for our students to use to get healthy and stay healthy. All fees are affordable, and student insurance is available. </p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4><a class="standout2" href="http://www.police.ucr.edu/">Campus Safety</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/police_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />UCR's <a href="http://www.police.ucr.edu/">Police Department</a> is here to provide a safe and secure learning environment at UCR. In addition to law enforcement services, we also provide: </p> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a href="http://www.escortservice.ucr.edu/">An escort program.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://police.ucr.edu/prevention.html">Crime prevention safeguards.</a></li> 
       <li>A <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/ens/">Campus Emergency Notification System</a> to quickly and reliably communicate with students, staff and faculty in an emergency. Planned upgrades to the system include expanding to include notification for parents.</li> 
       <li>A Communications Center staffed by trained Public Safety Dispatchers 24-hours-a-day, seven-days-a-week. To learn more, please visit our <a href="http://www.police.ucr.edu/">Campus Police website</a>.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://campushealth.ucr.edu/Services">Campus Health Center</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/healthcenter_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />UCR's <a href="http://campushealth.ucr.edu/Services">Campus Health Center</a> provides a variety of medical services for our students — from walk-up clinic care to urgent care for medical emergencies. Students with the UC Student Health Insurance Plan have no out-of-pocket expenses for doctor’s visits. Students with private insurance will have a $20 fee for doctor's visits. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.recreation.ucr.edu/">Student Recreation Programs</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/recreation_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />The UCR <a href="http://www.recreation.ucr.edu/">Student Recreation Center</a> is committed to getting our students involved and keeping them fit and healthy. Students have access to a variety of recreational, intramural and club sports. They can take a yoga class, join a game of pick-up basketball, or sign up for an adventure like rock climbing, surfing or camping with our Outdoor Excursions Program. The expanded Outdoor Complex includes 10 tennis courts, a full size roller hockey rink, two sand volleyball courts, and two full-length basketball courts. The facilities are free to UCR students with a current ID. Others may purchase quarterly memberships or day passes. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://counseling.ucr.edu/">Counseling Center</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/counseling_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />The <a href="http://counseling.ucr.edu/">Counseling Center</a> is dedicated to creating a positive, healthy atmosphere at UCR and working with students to provide an environment that promotes their academic, career, personal and social development. The center’s clinical team provides counseling services to undergraduate and graduate students to allow them to grow in ways that help them take advantage of the educational opportunities at UCR. </p> 
      <p> Our counselors are available by phone 24 hours, seven days a week, at (951) 827-5531. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/">Environmental Health &amp; Safety</a></h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/environmental_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />From earthquake preparedness to fire safety, the <a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/">Department of Environmental Health &amp; Safety</a> is here to provide planning, guidance and coordination of services. </p> 
      <p> Our services include: </p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/biosafety/">Biosafety</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/emergency/">Emergency Management</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/environmentalhealth/">Environmental Health</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/environmentalprograms/">Environmental Programs</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/ergonomics/">Ergonomics</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/fire/">Fire Safety</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/hazardousmaterials/">Hazardous Materials</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/laboratory/">Laboratory &amp; Research Safety</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/radiation/">Radiation Safety</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/risk/">Risk Management</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/safety/">Safety &amp; Industrial Hygiene</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/sustainability/">Sustainability</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/training/">Training</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/waste/">Waste Management</a></li> 
      </ul> 
      <p> We encourage students to familiarize themselves with <a href="http://ehs.ucr.edu/resources/emergencyprocedures.pdf">emergency procedures</a> and find out how they can help keep our campus safe </p>

     <p><img src="/giving/images/main/foundation_partnerships.jpg" alt=" " width="484" height="144" /></p> 
     <p><strong>With a long and successful track record of partnering with national, regional and family foundations</strong>, UCR regularly seeks new opportunities to collaborate with forward-thinking philanthropic leaders. Contact our knowledgeable staff to begin exploring the possibilities.</p> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Support <span>Opportunities</span></h3> 
     <ul class="l_big_arrows"> 
      <li><strong>Scholarships &amp; Fellowships</strong> — Connect with the best and brightest of our undergraduate and graduate students</li> 
      <li><strong>Endowed Programs &amp; Chairs</strong> — Provide in-depth support for a specific area of interest</li> 
      <li><strong>Buildings and Laboratories</strong> — Make a permanent, visible contribution to the institution and campus community</li> 
      <li><strong>Technology Transfer</strong> — Support or collaborate with path-breaking resarchers in business, science and engineering to create new products, manufacturing processes and materials</li> 
      <li><strong>Sponsored Research</strong> — Partner with research groups and/or individual faculty on specialized projects tailored to your interests and needs</li> 
      <li><strong>Event Sponsorships</strong> — Enjoy networking with leading academics, future employees, alumni and peer institutions as you give back to the community</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section3 l_blue_gold">Recognition <span>Opportunities</span></h3> 
     <p>UCR provides a variety of focused opportunities for your organization to gain visibility and build brand awareness with our extended campus community and industry partners. Through an active presence on campus, companies may enjoy increased exposure through enhanced interaction with our students and faculty, visibility in UCR’s online and printed publications, on-campus signage or through naming opportunities such as facilities, laboratories, endowed chairs and academic programs.</p>
