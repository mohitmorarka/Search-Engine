       <h3 style="font-size: 1.6em; font-weight: bold;">YOU’RE COVERED WITH SHIP</h3> 
       <p>As a student, you are automatically enrolled in UCR’s<strong> Student Health Insurance Program (SHIP)</strong> and exempt from purchasing a plan on the Covered CA website or your home state exchange. <strong>SHIP meets the requirements of the Affordable Care Act, </strong>gives you nationwide coverage, and offers the big advantage of convenient, on-campus care. </p> 
       <h4 style="font-size: 1.2em; font-weight: bold;">SHIP – TOTAL CONVENIENCE AND CARE</h4> 
       <p>With SHIP, you can go to <strong>Student Health Services</strong> right here on campus to see a doctor, get lab testing, and get X-rays at no out-of-pocket cost. We also have a pharmacy on site so you can get your medicine without having to leave campus. SHIP even includes dental and vision coverage.</p> 
       <h3 style="font-size: 1.2em; font-weight: bold;">SHIP – DARE TO COMPARE</h3> 
       <p>See for yourself how convenient and affordable SHIP is. <a href="../SiteCollectionDocuments/ucr-benefits-chart.pdf" target="_blank"><strong>Click here</strong></a> to see the many benefits of SHIP compared with Covered CA. We've got you covered, right here on campus and beyond.</p> 
       <p> <em><strong>Note:</strong> To opt out of SHIP, submit a waiver by March 12, 2014. Late waivers will be accepted through March 31, 2014 with a $50 late fee. For further assistance please contact The Student Health Insurance Office at 951-827-5683.</em><br /> </p> 
       <h3>Closure Notice: Ceasar Chavez Holiday</h3> 
       <p>Our facility will be closed on <strong>March 28th</strong> in observance of the holiday.</p>

      <p>The Finance &amp; Administration unit is responsible for managing daily administrative activities and financial strategic planning for Computing and Communications. This support includes: general payroll, human resource management, fiscal management, purchasing, reception, administrative and clerical support, facilities management, and recharge rate analysis.</p> 
      <h3>Mission Statement</h3> 
      <p>The Finance &amp; Administration unit is charged with providing day-to-day support to all units of Computing and Communications. Our job is to ensure C&amp;C employees, campus staff and faculty have the support and resources needed to get their jobs done. This includes various activities; interacting with the majority of the public, managing budgets, overseeing calendars, and providing policies and procedures specific to C&amp;C based on the overall UCOP policies, as well as numerous other activities. We strive to provide friendly professional support to C&amp;C employees and to the campus community.</p>
