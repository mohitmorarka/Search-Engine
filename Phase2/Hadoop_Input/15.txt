     <h3>A Place to Teach, a Place to Learn, a Place to Grow</h3> 
     <div class="nota-bene float_R"> 
      <h4>Faculty Contact Information</h4> 
      <p>Looking for a faculty member's contact information, their CV or information on their research? <a href="http://facultydirectory.ucr.edu/">Visit the UCR Faculty Directory</a>.</p> 
     </div> 
     <p> UCR provides faculty members with the resources, support and stimulation needed to reach their goals. and helps them make a difference in the world. </p> 
     <p> The university has a commitment to excellence and a proven track record of innovation and collaboration. Every day, our faculty brings an entrepreneurial spirit to showing what a public university is capable of in the 21st century. In the process, our faculty makes a global impact while remaining leaders for innovation in Inland Southern California.</p> 
     <ul class="standout"> 
      <li> <a href="/faculty/employment.html"> Learn more about the hiring process and the benefits of working at UC Riverside.</a> </li> 
      <li> <a href="/faculty/why.html">Learn why so many people are choosing UCR</a>.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <div class="faculty_inventing"> 
      <h3 class="accessibility">UCR Faculty: Inventing Your Future</h3> 
      <p> <img id="globe-bulb" class="img_R_borderless" src="/faculty/images/bulb.png" alt="." width="189" height="166" />Meet some of UCR's outstanding faculty and read about how their research is making a difference both locally and globally at our <a href="http://promise.ucr.edu/">Living the Promise</a> website. </p> 
     </div> 
     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <h3>Academic Senate</h3> 
      <p> The voice of the UCR faculty and a major conduit for faculty initiatives, the <a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/">Academic Senate</a> is a partner in the shared governance model of UC Riverside, overseeing the university's educational course. The senate's responsibilities include: </p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li>Authorize, approve, and supervise all courses (except those of certain graduate professional schools).</li> 
       <li>Determine the conditions of admissions, certificates and degrees.</li> 
       <li>Recommend the hiring and promotion of faculty members. </li> 
       <li>Approve the publication of manuscripts by the University of California Press. </li> 
       <li>Advise on the administration of the UC libraries.</li> 
       <li>Assist in the searches for deans, chancellors, and presidents.</li> 
       <li>Advise the university president and the chancellors on budget and administrative matters under the rules of &quot;shared governance&quot;.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h3 class="section">Office of Academic Personnel</h3> 
      <p> The <a href="http://academicpersonnel.ucr.edu/">Office of Academic Personnel</a> facilitates the recruitment, development and retention of academic employees. The office provides leadership, training and assistance on policy issues associated with the employment and advancement of academic employees. </p>

      <h4>Faculty Contact Information</h4> 
      <p>Looking for a faculty member's contact information, their CV or information on their research? <a href="http://facultydirectory.ucr.edu/">Visit the UCR Faculty Directory</a>.</p>

     <p> UCR provides faculty members with the resources, support and stimulation needed to reach their goals. and helps them make a difference in the world. </p> 
     <p> The university has a commitment to excellence and a proven track record of innovation and collaboration. Every day, our faculty brings an entrepreneurial spirit to showing what a public university is capable of in the 21st century. In the process, our faculty makes a global impact while remaining leaders for innovation in Inland Southern California.</p>

      <p> <img id="globe-bulb" class="img_R_borderless" src="/faculty/images/bulb.png" alt="." width="189" height="166" />Meet some of UCR's outstanding faculty and read about how their research is making a difference both locally and globally at our <a href="http://promise.ucr.edu/">Living the Promise</a> website. </p>

     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <h3>Academic Senate</h3> 
      <p> The voice of the UCR faculty and a major conduit for faculty initiatives, the <a href="http://senate.ucr.edu/">Academic Senate</a> is a partner in the shared governance model of UC Riverside, overseeing the university's educational course. The senate's responsibilities include: </p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li>Authorize, approve, and supervise all courses (except those of certain graduate professional schools).</li> 
       <li>Determine the conditions of admissions, certificates and degrees.</li> 
       <li>Recommend the hiring and promotion of faculty members. </li> 
       <li>Approve the publication of manuscripts by the University of California Press. </li> 
       <li>Advise on the administration of the UC libraries.</li> 
       <li>Assist in the searches for deans, chancellors, and presidents.</li> 
       <li>Advise the university president and the chancellors on budget and administrative matters under the rules of &quot;shared governance&quot;.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h3 class="section">Office of Academic Personnel</h3> 
      <p> The <a href="http://academicpersonnel.ucr.edu/">Office of Academic Personnel</a> facilitates the recruitment, development and retention of academic employees. The office provides leadership, training and assistance on policy issues associated with the employment and advancement of academic employees. </p>

      <h3>Virginia Blumenthal</h3> 
      <p> Whether in court, on the court, or in the game of life, well-known attorney and Riverside community leader Virginia Blumenthal knows a thing or two about what it takes to win. As an enthusiastic fan of UCR women's basketball, she enjoys attending games both at home and on the road. Last year, she and her husband Duane helped cheer the Lady Highlanders to a Big West Championship. They later made a gift to provide keepsake championship rings for the team. </p> 
      <h4>Life So Far</h4> 
      <p> As one of Riverside's most prominent criminal defense attorneys and owner of a successful law firm, she appears in the &quot;Best Lawyers in America&quot; directory and was recently named as one of the &quot;Most Influential Attorneys in California&quot; by the Daily Journal. She is the 2004 recipient of the ATHENA Award for outstanding community service, and served as president of the Riverside Symphony. For more than 25 years, she has worked to establish a nationally competitive &quot;mock-trial&quot; program involving students from more than 35 high schools that is today recognized as one of the best in the country. She is a mother of five, including three UCR graduates: Zachary '95, Jon '04 and Heather '06. </p> 
      <h4>A Favorite UCR Experience</h4> 
      <p> &quot;Serving as an Honorary Coach for the team has been great fun, as has being a member of the Riverside ‘Wild Women'...a group of exceptionally accomplished community and business leaders who love our Highlanders. We wear our blue and gold, paint our faces, cheer our women out on the court and sometimes meet with the team afterwards. Once, after a particularly tough loss, we were there to encourage these young players...to respect and honor them for their talent, dedication and ambition in the face of extraordinary challenges...and to remind them that, for each of us, the road to success almost always involves the opportunity to learn from failure.&quot; </p>

     <h3>Partnership Opportunities for Industry and Employers</h3> 
     <p>The University of California, Riverside welcomes the opportunity to work with local industries and employers. Read on to learn more about how your organization can benefit from a partnership with UCR.</p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4>Hire UCR Students Through the Career Center</h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/art_career_thumb.jpg" alt="UCR Career Center" width="93" height="46" />Looking to hire the next generation of high-impact employees? The <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">UCR Career Center</a> can help.&nbsp; The <a href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/employers/">SCOTJobs system</a> allows employers to post jobs and internships and select dates for on-campus interviews resources for perspective employers. </p> 
      <p>To learn more about the services available to employers, <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">visit our website</a> or call (951) 827-3631.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">UCR Governmental Relations</h4> 
      <p> <a href="http://gcr.ucr.edu/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/government_thumb.jpg" alt="UCR Government Relations" width="93" height="46" />Governmental Relations</a> is the primary link between the campus and local, regional, state and federal elected officials. It supports and promotes the university's missions of research, teaching and public service through advocacy, issue management, and functioning as a liaison with government, community and campus constituencies.</p> 
      <p>It also oversees the <a href="http://gcr.ucr.edu/advocacy.html">UCR Advocacy Network</a>, a group of alumni and friends who actively support the University of California's mission through lobbying efforts.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">UCR Technology Commercialization</h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/technology_thumb.jpg" alt="UCR Technology Transfer" width="93" height="46" />One way that UCR serves the public at large is by making the results of its research available for use. <a href="http://www.gcr.ucr.edu/">UCR's Office of Technology Commercialization</a> in the Office of Research licenses intellectual property to industry to promote development and commercialization of inventions. Royalties from these licenses helps support further research and education.</p> 
      <p>The office also oversees patents and material transfer agreements. <a href="http://techtransfer.universityofcalifornia.edu/default.aspx?campus=RV">Learn more about the available technology at the technology commercialization website</a>.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">Partnering with Our Schools and Colleges</h4> 
      <p> <img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/handshake.jpg" alt="Partnering with schools and colleges" width="93" height="46" />Many of our schools, colleges and programs have their own corporate partnership programs. To learn more about a particular program, please visit their website.</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a href="http://oscar.cert.ucr.edu/wwwcertucredu/opportunities/cpp.asp.html">CE-CERT Corporate Partners Program</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://www.engr.ucr.edu/industry/">Bourns College of Engineering (BCoE) Corporate Partners Program</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://soba.ucr.edu/involved/corporate.html">School of Business Administration (SoBA) Corporate Partners Program</a></li>

     <h3 class="l_blue_gold"><span>Meeting Your </span><br />Workforce Training Needs</h3> 
     <p>UC Riverside, through University Extension, offers a wide variety of programs to meet the educational needs of your workforce. For more information, visit <a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/">http://www.extension.ucr.edu/</a>.</p> 
     <h4>Custom Training and Group Programs:</h4> 
     <p>UC Riverside is recognized as a leader in international education, in the U.S. and around the world. Our Custom Training and Group Programs service is designed for businesses, educational institutions, governments and industries that are upgrading technologies, training new staff, improving services, or developing management techniques.</p> 
     <p><strong>Subject areas include but are not limited to:</strong></p> 
     <ul class="bulletless"> 
      <li>Information technology</li> 
      <li>Executive and corporate training</li> 
      <li>Business management development</li> 
      <li>Human resource development and planning</li> 
      <li>Environmental management</li> 
      <li>Forensic investigations</li> 
      <li>Geospatial technologies</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h4>Certificate Programs:</h4> 
     <p>A certificate is a non-degree program designed to provide specialized knowledge. A certificate from UCR Extension demonstrates that a participant has completed a planned, sequential program of study in a particular field of knowledge and that course of study. For more information visit <a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/academics/certificates/">http://www.extension.ucr.edu/academics/certificates/</a></p> 
     <p><strong>Subject areas include but are not limited to:</strong></p> 
     <ul class="bulletless"> 
      <li>Agriculture and Landscape</li> 
      <li>Business and Management</li> 
      <li>Engineering and Technology</li> 
      <li>Environment and Sustainability</li> 
      <li>Healthcare and Wellness</li> 
      <li>Languages and Interpretation</li> 
     </ul>

         <h2>Health</h2> 
         <p>Every day in our labs, classrooms and field research, UCR geneticists, biologists, psychologists, anthropologists, entomologists and engineers are living the promise of world-class healthcare.</p> 
         <p>We invent DNA-based technologies to diagnose and treat cancer. We research the biology and behavior of insects to help prevent the spread of devastating diseases. We develop powerful new pharmaceuticals and employ nanomedicine to heal wounds. We educate future neuroscientists, microbiologists and physicians, and we are home to California’s newest medical school, the UCR School of Medicine.</p> 
         <p>We unlock the mysteries of the human genome, because the power to heal lies within us all.</p>
