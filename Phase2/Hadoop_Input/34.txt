            function cleanHREF(href, extension){
              // Found RE on StackOverflow:
              // http://stackoverflow.com/questions/27745/getting-parts-of-a-url-regex)
              var uri_regex = /^((http[s]?|ftp):\/)?\/?([^:\/\s]+)(:([^\/]*))?((\/\w+)*\/)([\w\-\.]+[^#?\s]+)(\?([^#]*))?(#(.*))?$/;

              //Regex returns array with indexes as follows:
              var SCHEMA = 2, DOMAIN = 3, PORT = 5, PATH = 6,

              var match_container = clean_href.match(uri_regex)
            
              return match_container[PATH] + match_container[FILE].replace(extension, '');

      <h3>Voicemail (broadcast system)</h3> 
      <p>Authorized individuals may send an emergency message to any campus phone. When the emergency message is active you may see a light indicating that a voicemail message is awaiting your response.</p> 
      <p>Please note that during testing or large-scale use of the system:</p> 
      <p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Red light stays on</strong>. The red light may remain on, even after you have deleted the voicemail. Please disregard.</p> 
      <p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Delay</strong>. You may experience a delay in accessing the system if there many people are trying to access the system at the same time. Please hang up, wait a few moments, and then try again.</p> 
      <p>These issues are temporary, and plans are in place for a resolution in the near future.</p>

     <h3>Take a Tour</h3> 
     <p>There are several ways to get to know UCR better. The university offers a variety of tour options, including guided tours that can be scheduled in advance. The campus also is easily accessible for anyone planning to be in the Southern California area.</p> 
     <h3>Walking Tours Available</h3> 
     <p>Campus tours are available throughout the year from the <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR">Campus Tours Office</a>. The tours include up-close look at the campus and its facilities and are a great way to learn about UCR's and its history.</p> 
     <p><a class="button" href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR/tourOptions">Schedule a Tour</a></p> 
     <h3>Self-Guided Tours</h3> 
     <p>Much of the campus is available for viewing at your leisure if you are in the Southern California area. The university's address is:</p> 
     <address>900 University Avenue<br />Riverside, CA<br />92507</address> 
     <p>From the freeway, exit Highway 60/Interstate 215 at University Avenue and turn left. The campus is immediately ahead of you.</p> 
     <ul class="standout"> 
      <li><a href="/about/directions.html">For directions to the university</a></li> 
      <li><a href="http://www.campusmap.ucr.edu/map.pdf">For a printer-friendly version of a campus map</a></li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3>Virtual Tours</h3> 
     <p>You can navigate around UC Riverside at your leisure by taking one of several <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR/virtualTours">virtual tours</a> available online. The virtual tours provide a unique perspective of our beautiful campus, vibrant student life, and high academic quality, and can be accessed from the comfort of your home.</p>
