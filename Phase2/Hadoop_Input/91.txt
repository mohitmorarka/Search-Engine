     <h3>Supporting the Needs of the University</h3> 
     <p>The UCR Fund is an unrestricted fund that allows the university to support needs and commitments that arise during the school year. These unrestricted funds are raised through tele-fundraising, direct mail, and e-philanthropy. </p> 
     <h4>Fulfilling Your Pledge Online</h4> 
     <p> Making your <a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx?DU=UCR Fund">UCR Fund pledge payment</a> online is easy. <br /><br /> </p> 
     <h4 class="section">Goals of the UCR Fund</h4> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Raise funds to support the areas of greatest need as determined by the chancellor, deans and other university leaders.</li> 
      <li>Increase awareness of UCR within the local and broader communities.</li> 
      <li>Create future leadership for UCR.</li> 
      <li>Increase alumni participation and involvement with UCR.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h4 class="section">How the Fund Has Made a Difference</h4> 
     <p> The gifts to the UCR Fund from thousands of alumni, parents and friends have helped UCR: </p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Attract and retain the nation’s brightest students through scholarship and fellowship support. </li> 
      <li>Recruit world-class faculty in each department. </li> 
      <li>Create new and innovative programs and curricula. </li> 
      <li>Renovate classrooms and laboratories, and equip them with the necessary equipment and supplies to maximize our students' learning experiences. </li> 
      <li>Renew subscriptions to scholarly journals and keep our libraries up to date with the information our students need to support their educational experience.</li> 
      <li>Provide support for UCR Intercollegiate Athletics, the UCR Botanic Gardens and other programs and projects.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h4 class="section2"><a name="chancellorsassociates"> </a>Chancellor's Associates</h4> 
     <p>Donors who contribute $2,500 or more annually to any UCR school, college, program or department become members of The Chancellor’s Associates.</p> 
     <p>These loyal supporters want to make a difference at UCR — and they do. Chancellor’s Associate members provide the backbone of critical private support that makes UCR a leader for positive developments in our community, our region and beyond. Their gifts make a profound difference in the lives of countless people locally and around the world who benefit from the UCR’s teaching, research and public service programs. </p> 
     <p>To learn more about the Chancellor's Associates level, <a href="/giving/staff-directory.html">contact any member of our staff.</a></p> 
     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <h3>The UCR Telefund</h3>
      <img id="l_ucr_fund_girl" class="img_R_borderless" src="/giving/images/ucr_fund_girl.png" alt="The UCR Call Center student" width="162" height="183" /> 
      <p> The <strong>UCR Telefund </strong>is an integral part of the annual giving program. </p> 
      <p>Student callers connect with alumni, parents and friends nationwide to keep them informed of UCR news and events. Students also share with our constituencies the importance of private support and giving back to UCR. </p>

      <div class="span4 hero-unit"> 
       <p>We provide strategic leadership, planning and support for undergraduate admissions, student financial support and student services.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span4">
       <p>Student Affairs supports UC campuses, K-12 schools, California community colleges and community organizations in their efforts to prepare, matriculate, support and enhance the academic, social and intellectual life of a diverse current and future student body. We provide policy analysis and development, research, legislative and regulatory analysis, and program management. We also advise and support campuses in maintaining positive campus climates that are conducive to student success, diversity, health and wellness, leadership and involvement, and student retention.</p>

     <div class="span-11"> 
      <h3>Get Your Diploma</h3> 
      <p> An email will be sent to your R'Mail account when your diploma becomes available (about four to six months after your graduation date). Make sure that you <a href="https://support.google.com/mail/answer/10957?hl=en" target="_blank">forward your R’mail</a> to your personal email account so that you don’t miss the notice. </p> 
      <div class="accordion"> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> Pick up your diploma in person</h4> 
       <div> 
        <ul> 
         <li>You can pick up your diploma at the Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS), on the first floor of the <a href="http://campusmap.ucr.edu/imap/index.html?loc=SSB" target="_blank">Student Services Building</a>. </li> 
         <br /> 
         <li>You must provide a valid photo ID and signature when you pick up your diploma.</li> 
        </ul> 
       </div> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> Have your diploma mailed to you</h4> 
       <div> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Mail your completed <a href="/content/pdfs/diploma_request.pdf" target="_blank">Diploma Mailing Request Form</a> and a check or money order made payable to “UC Regents” to:<br /> <br /> <strong>University of California, Riverside<br /> Office of the Registrar <br /> 2249 Student Services Building<br /> Riverside, CA 92521-0118</strong> 
          <ul> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <br /> 
         <li>$7.75 for domestic addresses, $25 for international. Prices are based on current USPS rates and subject to change. </li> 
        </ul> 
       </div> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> Authorize someone to pick up your diploma</h4> 
       <div> 
        <p>If you would like to authorize a friend or family member to pick up your diploma, please write a brief letter to that effect that includes your:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><strong>Full name</strong></li> 
         <li><strong>Student identification number</strong></li> 
         <li><strong>Phone number</strong></li> 
         <li><strong>Signature</strong></li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>You can fax the above to 951-827-7368 or your friend or family member can bring it in-person to the HOSS on the first floor of the <a href="http://campusmap.ucr.edu/imap/index.html?loc=SSB" target="_blank">Student Services Building</a>. </p> 
        <p>We do <strong>NOT</strong> accept emails. </p> 
        <p>Remind your friend or family member to bring his or her valid photo ID when picking up your diploma. </p> 
       </div> 
      </div>
