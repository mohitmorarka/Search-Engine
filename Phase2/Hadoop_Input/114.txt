         <h5 class="dotted-bottom">Compensation</h5> 
         <ul class="dotted-list"> 
          <li class="active"><a href="index.html">Academic salaries</a></li> 
          <li class=""><a href="academic-ctos-titles-and-title-codes/index.html">Academic CTOs, titles, and title codes </a></li> 
          <li class=""><a href="historic-academic-salary-scales/index.html">Historic academic salary scales</a></li> 
         </ul> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span8 dotted-top" role="main"> 
       <p>Faculty members in the Professorial series or equivalent ranks at all campuses are paid on academic-year (nine-month) and fiscal-year (twelve-month) salary scales. Salary scales for most academic appointees provide for various levels of salary within each rank, generally referred to as steps within each rank, and for a normal period of service at each step. Some academic appointees are paid within an established salary range which is a published listing of the minimum to maximum salary for a particular title. The same scales and ranges are used by all University of California campuses for the same categories of academic appointees.</p> 
       <p>The Academic Salary Scales below were issued &nbsp;effective July 1, 2013. For more detailed information about these scales, please refer to the <a href="../_files/1213/memo-2013-2014-academic-salary-scales.pdf">issuance letter regarding 2013-14 Academic Salary Scales (pdf).</a></p> 
       <p><strong>For information on specific campus implementation of the Academic Salary Scales</strong>, and on salary rates for local exclusively represented units, please refer to your&nbsp;<a href="../staff/campus-academic-personnel-offices/index.html">campus Academic Personnel Office</a>&nbsp;and/or <a href="campus-salary-scales-websites.html">campus salary scale websites</a>.</p>

      <h3>We Are The Office of The Registrar</h3> 
      <br /> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-9"> 
      <div class="span-5"> 
       <h4>Location</h4> 
       <p> Our office is located on the second floor of the Student Services Building, room 2249. For in-person service, start at the Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS) on the first floor of the <a href="http://campusmap.ucr.edu/imap/index.html?loc=SSB">Student Services Building</a>. </p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span-4 last"> 
       <img src="/Images/student-services.png" alt="" /> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span-9 last"> 
       <div class="span-5"> 
        <h4>Office Hours</h4> 
        <p> Monday through Friday<br /> 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. </p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="span-4 last"> 
        <h4>Main Phone/Email </h4> 
        <p>(951) 827-7284<br /> <a href="mailto:reghelpdesk@ucr.edu">reghelpdesk@ucr.edu</a><br /> 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. </p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="span-9 last"> 
        <h4>Our mission is to:</h4> 
        <ul class="orange-bullets wide"> 
         <li>Deliver accurate, prompt, courteous and consistent service to all students and constituents of the university, consistent with its educational mission.</li> 
         <li>Take a leadership role in the accurate and efficient management of data in the primary functional areas of the office: registration, student academic records, course and classroom scheduling, classroom utilization, publications production and data distribution.</li> 
         <li>Promote and use the fullest available resources in the performance of our duties and responsibilities.</li> 
        </ul> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-9 last"> 
      <div class="sidebar"> 
       <img src="/Images/About_Us_Girl.png" alt="" style="float: right; margin: 4px" ;="" /> 
       <h4>What is a registrar?</h4> 
       <p>A university registrar maintains the academic records of all registered students. Our office provides a variety of services that support the campus community including: </p> 
       <ul class="wide"> 
        <li><strong>Preserving</strong> student academic records and releasing transcripts</li> 
        <li><strong>Verifying</strong> student status</li> 
        <li><strong>Providing</strong> detailed tuition and fee information</li> 
        <li><strong>Determining</strong> residency for tuition and fee purposes</li> 
        <li><strong>Confirming</strong> graduation (with colleges) and <strong>issuing</strong> diplomas</li> 
        <li><strong>Helping </strong>students explore, register for, and adjust their classes</li> 
        <li><strong>Highlighting</strong> important dates and deadlines</li> 
        <li><strong>Protecting</strong> students’ privacy through <strong>FERPA</strong></li> 
        <li><strong>Assisting</strong> faculty and staff with course proposals and classroom scheduling</li> 
       </ul> 
      </div>
