     <h3>Mentoring Summer Research Internship Program (MSRIP)</h3> 
     <p><img src="/images/GroupPhotoMSRIP2013.jpg" alt="MSRIP 2013" width="665" height="437" onmouseover="this.src='/images/GroupRopesCourse.JPG';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/GroupPhotoMSRIP2013.jpg';" onfocus="this.src='/images/GroupRopesCourse.JPG';" onblur="this.src='/images/GroupPhotoMSRIP2013.jpg';" /></p> 
     <p>&nbsp;Established in 1987, the Mentoring Summer Research Internship Program (MSRIP) is our eight-week summer research &nbsp; &nbsp;program designed for rising juniors, seniors (and some masters students) from educationally and/or economically &nbsp; &nbsp;disadvantaged backgrounds. Participants work under the supervision of a faculty mentor on the mentor’s research project.</p> 
     <p>The goal of MSRIP is to increase the number of outstanding students from diverse backgrounds who pursue the Ph.D. by strengthening their academic and professional development for admission to the University of California campuses and UC Riverside, in particular, as well as colleges and universities nationwide. Its primary objectives are to:</p> 
     <ol> 
      <li>Provide preparation for graduate studies through a variety of workshops</li> 
      <li>Provide a mechanism that fosters faculty/student interrelationships</li> 
      <li>Introduce students to graduate opportunities at UCR</li> 
      <li>Assist the students in realizing their potential for graduate studies, in general, and at UCR, in particular</li> 
      <li>Expose the faculty mentors to the possibility of recruiting their interns to their departments</li> 
     </ol> 
     <p>Many MSRIP alumni have successfully completed their doctorates here at UCR or at other Ph.D.-granting institutions. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Though state law prohibits selection with preference based on race, color, national origin, or gender, minorities and &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; women in underrepresented fields, who meet the eligibility criteria, are strongly encouraged to apply.</p> 
     <p><img src="/images/MSRIP2013SilvestreP.jpg?ox=" alt="2013 MSRIP Research" width="325" height="216" onmouseover="this.src='/images/MSRIP 2013MG.jpg';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/MSRIP2013SilvestreP.jpg';" onfocus="this.src='/images/MSRIP 2013MG.jpg';" onblur="this.src='/images/MSRIP2013SilvestreP.jpg';" /><img lang="/images/RopesCourse13.jpg" src="/images/ropesCourse2013.jpg" alt="MSRIP 10 ropes course 1" width="325" height="216" onmouseover="this.src='/images/RopesCourse13.jpg';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/ropesCourse2013.jpg';" onfocus="this.src='/images/RopesCourse13.jpg';" onblur="this.src='/images/ropesCourse2013.jpg';" /><img src="/images/Seminar2013.JPG?ox=" alt="DavidPoster" width="325" height="216" onmouseover="this.src='/images/ThursdaySeminar2013.JPG';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/Seminar2013.JPG';" onfocus="this.src='/images/ThursdaySeminar2013.JPG';" onblur="this.src='/images/Seminar2013.JPG';" /><img src="/images/UNAM2013.JPG" alt="OralPresentation" width="325" height="216" onmouseover="this.src='/images/MSRIPGroup13.jpg';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/UNAM2013.JPG';" onfocus="this.src='/images/MSRIPGroup13.jpg'" onblur="this.src='/images/UNAM2013.JPG';" /></p>

     <h4>Eligibility</h4> 
     <ul class="star">
