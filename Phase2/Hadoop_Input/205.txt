         <h4>Find UCR Campus Housing</h4> 
         <p>Our three housing options – <a href="housing-options/residence-halls/default.aspx">Residence Halls</a>, <a href="housing-options/campus-apartments/default.aspx">UCR Campus Apartments</a> and <a href="housing-options/family-student-housing.aspx">Family Housing</a> – provide you with a convenient, student-centered environment to complement university education. </p> 
         <p>With zero commute, friends next door and plenty of recreational activities happening down the hall or in the next building over, you are in the center of the action. </p> 
         <p>When it’s time to buckle down and study for a big exam, you'll find computer labs, study buddies and plenty of academic resources.</p> 
         <p>Plus, UCR Police and on-duty Residence Life staff provide 24-hour assistance and are committed to delivering a safe and secure environment.</p> 
         <p>When the campus is at your doorstep, the only thing you need to do is step outside.</p>
