      <div class="span4 hero-unit"> 
       <p>We provide systemwide financial management, reporting, policy, procedures and administrative services in support of UC's mission.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="span4">
       <p>Financial Accounting provides numerous systemwide support functions, including financial management and reporting in connection with employee benefits, debts, federal and other extramurally funded projects, endowment funds, and campus foundations. We oversee financial accounting policy, initiate changes to the Payroll/Personnel System, and develop systemwide policy and procedures regarding payroll, disbursements and tax matters.&nbsp;For UCOP, we operate the shared administrative services center.</p>

      <h3>Your Right to Privacy</h3> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-9"> 
      <p>Some of your records are automatically private, like your grades, transcripts and financial statements. Other information is automatically public, unless you make it private, like your phone number, address and other directory information.</p> 
      <h3>Grant Accesss to Your Records</h3> 
      <p>You can customize who has permission to access your records. <a href="/registrar/privacy-ferpa/disclosure-policies.aspx">Learn how.</a></p> 
      <h3>Make Directory Information Private</h3> 
      <p>You can ensure that certain directory information is not made public. <a href="/registrar/privacy-ferpa/disclosure-policies.aspx">Learn how.</a></p> 
     </div>

     <div class="span-18 last"> 
      <h3>Know Your Rights</h3> 
      <p>FERPA governs and protects your rights as a student to control your educational records. The primary rights protected under FERPA are:</p> 
      <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
       <li>The right to inspect and review your educational records</li> 
       <li>The right to seek amendment to your educational records</li> 
       <li>The right to consent to the disclosure of your educational records (see FERPA Disclosure Policies below)</li> 
       <li>The right to obtain a copy of <a href="/registrar/privacy-ferpa/ucr-ferpa-policy.aspx">UCR's FERPA Policy.</a> </li> 
       <li>The right to file a complaint with the <a href="http://www2.ed.gov/policy/gen/guid/fpco/ferpa/students.html" target="_blank">FERPA office in Washington, D.C.</a></li> 
      </ul>
