     <h2 class="intro"> Living Here</h2> 
     <h3 class="verd intro"> Our residence halls, campus apartments and family homes are <br /> comfortable, safe and full of amenities. Life is good here.</h3> 
     <div class="ContentFlow" id="myFantasicFlow"> 
      <div class="loadIndicator"> 
       <div class="indicator"> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="flow"> 
       <div class="item"> 
        <img alt="Mom and baby playing with ball" class="content" src="/Images/housingContentFlow/FamilyHousing_1.jpg" /> 
        <div class="caption"> 
         <h3> Family Student Housing</h3> 
         <p> Canyon Crest Family Student Housing is economically priced with families in mind. Choose from unfurnished two- and three-bedroom duplex homes. Enjoy tree-lined streets, park and playground areas and family-oriented activities. <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/housing-options/family-student-housing.aspx"> Learn more about our family student housing.</a> </p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="item"> 
        <img alt="Mundo theme hall mural" class="content" src="/Images/housingContentFlow/ThemeHousing.jpg" /> 
        <div class="caption"> 
         <h3> Special-Interest Housing </h3> 
         <p> Opt to live in theme halls or living-learning communities with students who share your cultural and academic passions. Experience college life with new friends who share your vision, while networking with faculty and campus mentors. <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/liveUCR/special-interest-housing.aspx">Discover our special-interest communities.</a></p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="item"> 
        <img alt="Campus apartments at sunset" class="content" src="/Images/housingContentFlow/CampusApts_3.jpg" /> 
        <div class="caption"> 
         <h3> Campus Apartments</h3> 
         <p> UCR's campus apartments include Bannockburn Village &amp; Plaza, Falkirk, Stonehaven, Glen Mor and Oban. Communities feature furnished and unfurnished one-bedroom, two-bedroom and loft suites, plus swimming pools, barbecue areas, computer labs and activities. <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/housing-options/campus-apartments/default.aspx">Learn more about our campus apartment communities</a> </p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="item"> 
        <img alt="Two female students sit outside a residence hall" class="content" src="/Images/housingContentFlow/residenceHalls_1.jpg" /> 
        <div class="caption"> 
         <h3> Residence Halls</h3> 
         <p> UCR has three residence hall communities: Aberdeen-Inverness, Lothian and Pentland Hills. Communities feature double and triple furnished rooms, TV lounges, music rooms, study areas, computer labs and more. <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/housing-options/residence-halls/default.aspx"> Learn more about our residence hall communities.</a> </p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="item"> 
        <img alt="Students eating at The Barn" class="content" src="/Images/housingContentFlow/Dining_3.jpg" />

        <h3>Where To Look For Jobs &amp; Internships</h3> 
        <p>This&nbsp;section will provide you with essential information on where to locate job listings. You will find information regarding SCOTjobs, job posting websites, internship listings, </p> 
        <div class="floatspecial"> 
         <h3 class="callout02-blue-img"><a title="" href="https://auth.ucr.edu/cas/login?service=https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/login&amp;"><img style="border-bottom:0px solid;border-left:0px solid;border-top:0px solid;border-right:0px solid" border="0" alt="Contact Us With Your Needs" src="../../SiteCollectionImages/CallToActionButtons/calltoactionbuttonJobLanding.jpg" /></a></h3>
        </div> 
        <p>Federal Work Study positions, on-campus interviewing program, and other job search tools.</p> 
        <h3 class="openuptext-bottom"><span style="font-family:arial">What is SCOTjobs?</span></h3> 
        <p><span style="font-family:arial;color:black">SCOTjobs is where you start your job search.<span>&nbsp; </span><a title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTjobs </a>is a massive, invaluable online jobs listing database found in <a title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTLink.</a><span>&nbsp;</span>It is a resource EXCLUSIVELY for UC Students and Alumni. You can search for full-time, part-time, internship and summer jobs at the Career Center, from your home or from any place that has an Internet connection. Additionally you have access to Nacelink,&nbsp;a nationwide job database&nbsp;with&nbsp;more than&nbsp;25,000 job postings each year. (Please note that Alumni are charged a small fee to continue using SCOTLink services.)</span></p> 
        <p><span style="font-family:arial;color:black">You can login to SCOTjobs by clicking on the student login under the <b>SCOTLink</b> logo available on any page in the website. <a title="" href="../../FlashVideos/scotlink/scotlink%20job%20search.swf"><span style="font-family:arial;color:black">Watch video on how to use SCOTjobs.</span></a><br /><br /></span><span style="font-family:arial;color:black">In addition to job listings information you are going to want to take advantage of services in our other sections to get your winning job: <a title="" href="../../careerFairsWorkshops/Pages/CareerFairsWorkshops.aspx">Career Fairs / Workshops</a>, <a title="" href="../../essentialJobSearch/Pages/EssentialJobSearchSkills.aspx">Essential Job Search Skills</a>. Get to know your Career Center. A little time will reward your effort with years of satisfaction on the career front.</span>&nbsp;</p>

         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> I want to go straight to SCOTjobs. What is SCOTjobs?&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl03__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p><a style="zoom:1" title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTjobs</a> is a massive, invaluable, online jobs listing database found in <a style="zoom:1" title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTLink</a>. It’s where you will start your job search. <a style="zoom:1" title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTjobs</a> is a resource EXCLUSIVELY for UC students and alumni. You can search for full-time, part-time, internship and summer jobs at the Career Center, from your home or from any place that has an Internet connection.<a title="" href="http://www.nacelink.com/" target="_blank">NACElink </a>is also integrated, providing students with&nbsp;more than&nbsp;25,000 job postings each year. (Please note that alumni are charged a small fee to continue using SCOTLink services.)</p>

         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> How do I use SCOTjobs?&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl05__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>Simply log in to <a title="" href="https://ucr-csm.symplicity.com/sso/students/">SCOTLink</a> or click on one of the other resources to start searching for your next job or internship.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div>

        <div class="ui-accordion-group">
         <!-- 3 --> 
         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> How do I get the job I really want?&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl07__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>Getting the job is all about fit. Finding the place where your unique talents and education and drive all combine for the perfect fit. That takes work, time and systematic effort. Self-knowledge is critically important. What are your strengths? What are your limitations? What do you really like doing? <a title="" href="../../careerPlanningCounseling/assessments/Pages/Assessment.aspx">Exploring yourself</a> can be the first step to finding the job you want.</p> 
           <p>One final question. Are you persistent? It’s rare for people to get immediate results and being turned down is always part of finding a job. Keep trying and you will reach your goals.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="ui-accordion-group">
         <!-- 4 --> 
         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> Discover goals and resources&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl09__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>Familiarize yourself with the Career Center. Explore all of your options in various career paths. Check out all you can do online. Guess what? You’ve only seen the tip of the iceberg when it come to the tools and services available for UCR students. Career fairs, workshops, career evaluation guides, counseling, resume training, interview practice... check it out.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="ui-accordion-group">
         <!-- 5 --> 
         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> What do I want to do?&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl11__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>Before launching your search, ask yourself, &quot;What do I want to do?&quot; Whether you have an answer to this question or not, stop by during <a title="Make an Appointment" href="../../aboutUs/makeanappointment/Pages/MakeanAppointment.aspx">Drop-in Hours</a> and talk to a career counselor. We’ve got lots of ways to help you create a plan of action that will organize your job search.</p> 
           <p>This section of the UCR Career Center Website is designed to help you plan and organize a successful job search.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="ui-accordion-group">
         <!-- 6 --> 
         <h3 class="ui-accordion-header"><a href="#"> Take an inventory of yourself&nbsp;</a></h3> 
         <div class="ui-accordion-content"> 
          <div id="ctl00_ctl00_PlaceHolderMainBase_PlaceHolderMain_ctl13__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>How’s that resume? Was it copied from your best friend’s or downloaded from resumes-r-us.com? We can help with that. How about your interview skills? Have you been taking tips from your roommate whose last job was in middle school? Should you be hours early or fashionably late? Dark suit or you favorite jeans and flip-flops? We can help you steer the right path between too much confidence and too little, between too formal and too relaxed — what we will help you do, really, is to be yourself. Show your genuine skills, ease and confidence. You are the answer to their employee search</p> 
           <p>Use this Website to address your growth edges and take your game to the next level. Don’t wait until the last minute. There are things you can begin doing as early as your freshman year to create an advantage when you finally start looking for a career position.</p>
