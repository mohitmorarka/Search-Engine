     <h4>The UCR Alumni Association</h4> 
     <p><strong>UCR provided you with the tools necessary to succeed in today and tomorrow's world.</strong> By becoming a lifetime member of the UCR Alumni Association, you are continuing to build upon your strong relationship with the campus.</p> 
     <p>It is an exciting time to be a Highlander, as our campus is thriving.<br /><strong><em>Don't miss out!</em></strong> </p>
     <p> <a class="button" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=287">Become a Member</a> </p> 
     <p class="section"></p> 
    </div> 
    <hr /> 
    <!-- End Sidebar --> 
    <!-- Start Content Area --> 
    <div id="main"> 
     <p><a title="UCR Alumni Association" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/"><img id="home_main" src="/alumni/images/main/alumni_bttn.png" alt="You may leave UCR... but UCR never leaves you." width="484" height="196" /></a></p> 
     <p>UC Riverside alumni enjoy a lifetime of friendships and other benefits. The university takes pride in providing our alumni with a variety of continuing education and networking opportunities along with travel and entertainment options.</p> 
     <p>There is something for every UCR alumni, from the members of the inaugural class to the most recent graduates. The campus is growing, changing and evolving, and we hope you will come home to visit.</p> 
     <p><a class="standout" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=565">Visit the UCR Alumni Association website.<br /></a></p> 
     <ul class="striped big_links"> 
      <li><a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/"><strong>The UCR Alumni Association</strong>The UCR Alumni Association preserves and promotes the relationships between alumni and UCR. Joining the association helps you to keep in touch with your classmates, connect with fellow UCR alumni and stay up to date on the campus as a whole. Visit the association website to learn more about becoming a member of the UCR Alumni Association.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/avc"><strong>The Alumni &amp; Visitors Center</strong>The Alumni&nbsp;&amp; Visitors Center is the home of the UCR Alumni Association and a beautiful place for UCR friends and family to gather</a></li> 
     </ul> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h3>Legacy Brick Program</h3> 
      <p><img id="legacy_brick" class="img_L_borderless" src="/alumni/images/brick_bang.png" alt="." width="118" height="80" />For a tax-deductible gift of $500, you can purchase a 4x8 inch brick inscribed with a three-line, 20 character name or message of your choice that will be displayed in the entrance courtyard of the Alumni &amp; Visitors Center. Learn more about the <a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=275">Legacy Brick Program</a>.</p> 
      <p><img class="img_R_borderless" src="/alumni/images/colors.png" alt="Wear Your Colors Proudly" /></p> 
      <h3 class="section">Get Your Official Gear at the UCR Campus Store</h3> 
      <p>Show and share your Highlander pride by wearing official UC Riverside apparel from the <a href="http://www.ucrcampusstore.ucr.edu/">UCR Campus Store</a>. Can't make it to campus? You can purchase everything you need through our online store. UCRAA members can save 10% on clothing and emblematic items.</p> 
      <p><a class="standout2" href="http://www.ucrcampusstore.ucr.edu/StoreHours.aspx">Check store hours.</a></p>

     <h4>The UCR Alumni Association</h4> 
     <p><strong>UCR provided you with the tools necessary to succeed in today and tomorrow's world.</strong> By becoming a lifetime member of the UCR Alumni Association, you are continuing to build upon your strong relationship with the campus.</p> 
     <p>It is an exciting time to be a Highlander, as our campus is thriving.<br /><strong><em>Don't miss out!</em></strong> </p>
     <p> <a class="button" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=287">Become a Member</a> </p>

     <p><a title="UCR Alumni Association" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/"><img id="home_main" src="/alumni/images/main/alumni_bttn.png" alt="You may leave UCR... but UCR never leaves you." width="484" height="196" /></a></p> 
     <p>UC Riverside alumni enjoy a lifetime of friendships and other benefits. The university takes pride in providing our alumni with a variety of continuing education and networking opportunities along with travel and entertainment options.</p> 
     <p>There is something for every UCR alumni, from the members of the inaugural class to the most recent graduates. The campus is growing, changing and evolving, and we hope you will come home to visit.</p> 
     <p><a class="standout" href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=565">Visit the UCR Alumni Association website.<br /></a></p>

      <li><a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/"><strong>The UCR Alumni Association</strong>The UCR Alumni Association preserves and promotes the relationships between alumni and UCR. Visit the association website to learn more about becoming a member of the UCR Alumni Association.</a></li> 
      <li><a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/avc"><strong>The Alumni &amp; Visitors Center</strong>The Alumni&nbsp;&amp; Visitors Center is the home of the UCR Alumni Association and a beautiful place for UCR friends and family to gather</a></li> 
     </ul>

      <p><img id="legacy_brick" class="img_L_borderless" src="/alumni/images/brick_bang.png" alt="." width="118" height="80" />For a tax-deductible gift of $500, you can purchase a 4x8 inch brick inscribed with a three-line, 20 character name or message of your choice that will be displayed in the entrance courtyard of the Alumni &amp; Visitors Center. Learn more about the <a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=275">Legacy Brick Program</a>.</p>

You can purchase everything you need through our online store. UCRAA members can save 10% on clothing and emblematic items.</p>

     <div class="col col-lrg" id="col-2">
      <h2>Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan</h2>
      <div class="intro">
       <p>The Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan is a financial aid program intended to expand access to UC for lower-income students.</p>
      </div>
      <p>UC's Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan will ensure that you will not have to pay UC’s systemwide tuition and fees out of your own pocket if you are a California resident whose total family income is less than $80,000 a year and you qualify for financial aid — and that's just for starters.</p>
      <p>Blue and Gold students with sufficient financial need can qualify for even more grant aid to help reduce the cost of attending.</p>
      <p><strong>Type of aid</strong>: <a href="../../how-aid-works/grants-and-scholarships/index.html">Gift aid</a> (from multiple sources)</p>
      <p><strong>How to apply</strong>: <a href="../../apply-for-aid/index.html">FAFSA/California Dream Act Application + Cal Grant GPA Verification Form</a> (you will be automatically considered when you submit these forms by March 2; there is no separate application for this program) </p>
      <div class="primary">
       <h3>What's covered</h3>
       <p> If you are eligible, your systemwide tuition and fees will be fully covered by scholarship or grant money. The plan combines all sources of scholarship and grant awards you receive (federal, state, UC and private) to go toward covering your tuition and fees. </p>
       <p>Students with greater financial need can qualify for even more grant support to help defray other educational expenses (like books, housing, transportation, etc.). </p>
       <p>You don't need to fill out a separate application to qualify for the Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan. You will receive the benefits of the Blue and Gold plan automatically if you qualify. </p>
      </div>
      <div class="primary">
       <h3>Eligibility requirements</h3>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Submit a FAFSA or California Dream Act Application and Cal Grant GPA Verification Form by March 2</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">California resident or have <a href="../../tuition-and-cost/ab540/index.html">AB 540 status</a></li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Demonstrate total family income below $80,000 and financial need, as determined for federal need-based aid programs</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Be in your first four years as a UC undergraduate (first two for transfer students)</li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li class="last">Meet other campus basic requirements for UC grant aid (for example, be enrolled at least half-time during the academic year, meet campus academic progress standards, not be in default on student loans, etc.)</li>
       </ul>
      </div>

      <div>
       The UCR Alumni Association has realized a long-held objective: completion of an Alumni &amp; Visitors Center for the University of California, Riverside. The Alumni &amp; Visitors Center serves as the &quot;front door&quot; to the campus - a distinctive arrival point that showcases alumni achievements while welcoming friends and visitors. The 13,865-square-foot, split-level building designed by local architects HMC, houses a large lobby, meeting rooms, a library of works by UCR authors, and a formal board room. The facility is available for special events, meetings and receptions.

         <h2>Policy</h2> 
         <p>Powered by innovation, an entrepreneurial spirit, and a commitment to enabling social and economic mobility, UCR is a dynamic catalyst for the continued growth of California and the nation.</p> 
         <p>We research new methods to improve the quality of our air, water, soil and food supplies. We partner with urban planners, government officials and law enforcement experts to combat crime and enhance public safety. We serve as an honest broker among legislative, regulatory and commercial interests seeking independent, data-driven facts and analysis.</p> 
         <p>We help develop the forward-looking social, economic, environmental and educational policies that serve an increasingly complex, multicultural, interconnected world.</p>
