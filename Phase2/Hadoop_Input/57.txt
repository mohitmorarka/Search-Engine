     <h2 class="intro"> Dive In</h2> 
     <h3 class="verd intro"> Meet friends, spark talents and make history with student organizations and leadership opportunities.</h3> 
     <div class="clearfix"> 
      <div class="grid_4 alpha"> 
       <img alt="Paraglider flying through sky" width="223" height="368" src="/Images/paraglide_getting_involved.png" /> 
       <h3 class="lightBlue fingHuge centerTxt" style="margin-top: 10px;"> 30</h3> 
       <p> <br /> The number of peer programs on campus. Lead as a peer mentor or benefit as a mentee. <a href="http://well.ucr.edu/Peers/Pages/NetworkofPeers.aspx">Increase the &quot;Power of the Peers</a>.&quot;</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="grid_8 omega"> 
       <div class="grid_4 alpha"> 
        <p> Perform at games with the <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/highlanderband" target="_blank">Highlander Band</a>, write articles for <a href="http://csp.ucr.edu/nuestracosa/Pages/default.aspx" target="_blank">Nuestra Cosa</a> or perfect diplomacy as part of our <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/munatucr" target="_blank">Model United Nations</a>. </p> 
        <p>Rally with <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/CollegesAgainstCancer" target="_blank">Colleges Against Cancer</a>, build homes with <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/h4hucr" target="_blank">Habitat for Humanity at UCR</a> or create change with <a href="https://asucr.ucr.edu/?doing_wp_cron=1381962110.2884569168090820312500" target="_blank">Associated Students</a>. </p> 
        <p>From competitive teams to community service clubs to packed-house events, UC Riverside offers you the most opportunities to get involved. <a href="http://studentlife.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">Explore them all at Student Life</a>.</p> 
        <h3> Historic Power</h3> 
        <p> Volunteer with the <a href="http://csp.ucr.edu/studentinvolvement/Pages/chicano.aspx"> Chicano Link Peer Mentor Program</a>, come to a <a href="http://nasp.ucr.edu/events/Pages/UCR%20Pow%20Wow.aspx"> Pow Wow</a> or create change with the <a href="http://wrc.ucr.edu/programsandservices/Pages/LeadershipOpportunities.aspx#144"> Leadership Community for Women Club</a>. </p> 
        <p>Speak out on an <a href="http://out.ucr.edu/programs/Pages/CommunityOUTreachEducators.aspx"> LGTBT Resource Center student panel</a>, tutor local youth with <a href="http://asp.ucr.edu/studentinvolvement/Pages/outreach.aspx">SOAR</a> and help plan <a href="http://apsp.ucr.edu/getinvolved/Pages/default.aspx">Lunar New Year</a>. </p> 
        <p>Our historic <a href="https://www.ucr.edu/students/ethnic_gender.html">Ethnic &amp; Gender Programs</a> provide impressive opportunities to get involved with leadership, outreach, events and activism that benefit us all.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="grid_4 omega"> 
        <h3 class="darkBlue fingHuge centerTxt" style="margin-top: 10px;">400+</h3> 
        <p class="right"> The number of student organizations you can join at UCR. <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organizations">See them all</a>. </p> 
        <img alt="Ballet folklorico dancer" width="191" height="267" src="/Images/festivalDancer_getting_involved.png" class="right" style="margin-top: 70px;" /> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="doubleRoundedCorners borderGrey" style="margin-top: 15px;"> 
      <div class="clearfix gradientGrey"> 
       <img alt="Sorority sisters smiling" class="imgFloatLeft noMargin" width="320" height="171" src="/Images/women_getting_involved.png" /> 
       <h3 style="margin-top: 10px;"> Strong Connections</h3> 
       <p> Find academic camaraderie with <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/aaus">African Americans United in Science, </a>make big plans with the <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/ama"> American Marketing Association</a> or build clout as part of an <a href="http://studentlife.ucr.edu/studentorganizations/pages/honoraryandhonorsocieties.aspx"> honor society</a>. It’s easy to meet people who share your dreams at UCR. </p> 
      </div>

       <p>The banner headline on Feb. 15, 1954 tells the story - the first day of classes at the new University of California, Riverside.</p>

      <p>RIVERSIDE, Calif. — On February 15, 1954, the University of California, Riverside opened its doors for the first day of classes. That day, <em>The Riverside Daily Press</em> and <em>Riverside Enterprise</em> newspapers (the forerunners of today’s <em>Press-Enterprise</em>) published a 56-page advertising supplement to commemorate the campus’ opening. In honor of the 60-year anniversary of UCR’s first day of classes, we’ve reproduced the complete original supplement electronically. </p> 
      <p>“This is an occasion warranting a memorial. We present here a supplement of the <em>Riverside Daily Press</em> and <em>Riverside Enterprise</em> in the hope that it will memorialize the opening of classes at the University of California at Riverside,” reads the introduction on page three.</p> 
      <p>The document was scanned into a PDF from a microfilm copy of the paper in the UC Riverside Tom&aacute;s Rivera Library collection. Artifacts on the images or blurring are from the original microfilm.</p> 
      <p>To read the supplement, click the image below and it will open in a larger window. You can zoom in to read the content, which includes pieces by Provost Gordon S. Watkins, Dean Robert A. Nisbet, Dean of Students Thomas L. Broadbent, Life Sciences Chair Herman T. Spieth, Humanities Chair John W. Olmstead, and Physical Sciences Division Head W. Conway Pierce.</p> 
      <p>The supplement also contains some amazing pictures of the campus, including some places that will look familiar to the students of today. For example, look at the light fixtures in the photo of the library on page 18, or the photo of the pool on page 21. Page nine has a beautiful, hand-drawn campus map showing the buildings that existed at the opening and the plans for expansion.</p>
