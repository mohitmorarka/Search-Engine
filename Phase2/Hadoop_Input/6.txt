     <h3>The Backbone of the Educational Process</h3> 
     <p>UCR's <a href="/academics/colleges.html">colleges, divisions and schools</a> are the organizations that keep the educational process working. </p>
     <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     <div class="boxA"> 
      <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="maincontent1" group="Everyone" button="707" -->

      <h3 class="accessibility">Undergraduate Programs: Where Do You Want to Be Tomorrow?</h3> 
      <p>With 65 undergraduate majors and programs to choose from, UC Riverside has a course of study that fits your interests and will help you reach your goals. <a href="/academics/undergradmajors.html">Explore our list of majors</a>. </p> 
      <h4><img id="student1" class="img_R_borderless" src="/academics/images/student1.jpg" alt="." width="139" height="185" />Resources to succeed in your education</h4> 
      <p>The <a href="http://www.ue.ucr.edu/">Office of Undergraduate Education</a> is committed to student success and the creation of a supportive educational environment at UCR. The office oversees the development of programs designed to support student learning, research and experiential opportunities. Visit our offices to learn about the support programs that can help you be more competitive in the job market and in the pursuit of graduate and professional education. </p>
      <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     </div>

      <h3 class="accessibility">Graduate Programs: Completing the Process</h3> 
      <p>UCR's graduate programs open new doors to our students. <a href="/academics/gradprograms.html">Explore our graduate programs</a>. </p>
      <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     </div> 
     <div class="boxD"> 
      <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="maincontent3" group="Everyone" button="707" -->

      <img class="border_image" src="/academics/images/summer_sessions.png" alt="." width="332" height="30" /> 
      <h3 class="accessibility">Drive to Succeed With UCR Summer Sessions</h3> 
      <p>The <a href="/academics/summer.html">Summer Sessions program</a> provides the opportunity for students to attend summer classes at the university without being a full-time enrolled UCR student. </p> 
      <p>Classes may be open to: </p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>College and university students</li> 
       <li>High school graduates</li> 
       <li>High school students entering grades 10-12 in 2010 through the <a href="http://summeracademy.ucr.edu/">Summer Academy For Advanced High School Students</a></li> 
       <li>Adult learners</li> 
       <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/international_students.html">International students</a></li> 
      </ul>

      <h3 class="accessibility">Undergraduate Programs: Where Do You Want to Be Tomorrow?</h3> 
      <p>With 65 undergraduate majors and programs to choose from, UC Riverside has a course of study that fits your interests and will help you reach your goals. <a href="/academics/undergradmajors.html">Explore our list of majors</a>. </p> 
      <h4><img id="student1" class="img_R_borderless" src="/academics/images/student1.jpg" alt="." width="139" height="185" />Resources to succeed in your education</h4> 
      <p>The <a href="http://www.ue.ucr.edu/">Office of Undergraduate Education</a> is committed to student success and the creation of a supportive educational environment at UCR. The office oversees the development of programs designed to support student learning, research and experiential opportunities. Visit our offices to learn about the support programs that can help you be more competitive in the job market and in the pursuit of graduate and professional education. </p>

      <h3 class="accessibility">Drive to Succeed With UCR Summer Sessions</h3> 
      <p>The <a href="/academics/summer.html">Summer Sessions program</a> provides the opportunity for students to attend summer classes at the university without being a full-time enrolled UCR student. </p> 
      <p>Classes may be open to: </p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>College and university students</li> 
       <li>High school graduates</li> 
       <li>High school students entering grades 10-12 in 2010 through the <a href="http://summeracademy.ucr.edu/">Summer Academy For Advanced High School Students</a></li> 
       <li>Adult learners</li> 
       <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/international_students.html">International students</a></li>

     <h3>Riding, Driving &amp; Parking at UCR</h3> 
     <p> There are a variety of transportation options for UCR students.&nbsp; Whether you have your own car or take the bus, ride a bike or need help getting around campus, we have a variety of options that can help get you where you need to go.</p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4><a class="standout2" href="http://www.RiversideTransit.com">Riding the Bus</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/gettingaround_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />The <a class="external" href="http://www.RiversideTransit.com">Riverside Transit Agency (RTA)</a> provides public transportation for Western Riverside County. <strong>UCR students ride for free with a valid student ID card.</strong></p> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a class="external" href="http://www.riversidetransit.com/home/index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=116&amp;Itemid=106">Get bus route and schedule information.</a></li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://campusmobility.ucr.edu/">Campus Mobility Service</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" title="Campus Mobility Service" src="/students/images/campus_mobility2.jpg" alt="Campus Mobility Service" width="93" height="46" />The Campus Mobility Service provides free curb-to-curb transport services to and from campus locations for mobility impaired students, faculty or staff. Trips must be reserved in advance and hours vary.</p>

<p>
<img class="img_L" src="/students/images/trolley.jpg" _mce_src="/students/images/trolley.jpg" alt="Trolley Service" title="Trolley Service" width="93" height="46" />The free campus shuttle service is operated during the school year, transporting the campus community to and from the main campus, University Village, the Riverside Sports Center, Glen Mor, Bannockburn and various apartment complexes surrounding UCR.</p>

      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://parking.ucr.edu/">Transportation &amp; Parking at UCR</a></h4> 
      <p><a href="http://parking.ucr.edu/"><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/transportation_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />Transportation &amp; Parking Services (TAPS)</a> is a clearinghouse for transportation information. If you need to buy a parking permit, get information on public transportation and campus shuttles, or pay a parking citation, TAPS can help.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.zipcar.com/ucr/">Zipcar</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" title="Zipcar image" src="/students/images/zipcar.jpg" alt=" Zipcar image" width="93" height="46" />Zipcar is the perfect solution for individuals who occasionally need to use a car but don't have one of their own. UCR students, staff and faculty can sign up and become Zipcar members for just $35 per year, plus rental charges. You reserve your car online, then just walk up to it and run your errand. Hourly and daily rates always include gas and insurance.</p>

     <h3>Building the Present, Investing in the Future</h3> 
     <div class="contact-us float_R"> 
      <p>For more information about the UCR Foundation, contact:<br /><a href="mailto:pat.kohlmeier@ucr.edu">Pat Kohlmeier</a><br />(951) 827-6291 </p> 
     </div> 
     <p>The <a href="http://foundation.ucr.edu/">UC Riverside Foundation</a> is a nonprofit corporation through which philanthropic resources flow to the University of California, Riverside. Established in 1974, the foundation raises, records and manages gifts from individuals, corporations, organizations and foundations for the sole benefit of UCR in accordance with donors' wishes.</p> 
     <p>The growth of the foundation enables investment in the present and future of UCR's colleges, programs, research and students. Its success depends on the financial support of our dedicated and generous alumni, friends, parents and patrons who understand that the research and teaching taking place in UCR's classrooms and laboratories have the potential to change our world for the better.</p> 
     <h4>The UCR Foundation Leadership</h4> 
     <p>The foundation is also people — leaders who care about UCR and who advance its mission by serving on the board of trustees, by seeking and securing private support for all of its programs, and by representing UCR in the community.</p> 
     <p>The business affairs and property of the foundation are managed by, and all corporate powers of the foundation are vested in, its <a href="/giving/foundation_board.html">Board of Trustees</a>. S. Sue Johnson&nbsp;serves as chair of the organization. Through their individual generosity, our 50+ trustees have:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Named schools, colleges and programs.</li> 
      <li>Endowed academic chairs, created scholarships and fellowships.</li> 
      <li>Opened doors to increase support and collaboration between their industries and UCR.</li> 
     </ul>

      <h3>Live and learn in exciting Southern California!</h3> 
      <p> The University of California, Riverside is one of the world’s most highly ranked academic institutions, as well as one of the most diverse and friendly universities in the United States. As a member of the prestigious University of California, UC Riverside offers a world-class education to international students from more than 60 countries. UC Riverside’s 1,200 acre park-like campus is a short distance from Hollywood, Disneyland, snowy mountain resorts and Southern California’s legendary beaches! </p> 
      <br /> 
      <div class="thinBorderBottom"></div> 
      <br /> 
      <h3>Come to UC Riverside</h3> 
      <ul class="yellowBullet"> 
       <li>Perform undergraduate research at one of UC Riverside’s 24 nationally recognized, technologically advanced research centers and institutes.</li> 
       <li>Study and earn a respected University of California, Riverside degree, ranked among the top universities for research impact, and featuring the highest ranked engineering school for its size.</li> 
       <li>Learn from UC Riverside’s 900 prominent scholars and researchers <br />— 98 percent of whom have earned Ph.D.’s.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <div class="thinBorderBottom"></div> 
      <br /> 
      <h3>International Admission Requirements</h3> 
      <ul class="yellowBullet"> 
       <li>You are an <strong>international applicant</strong> if you need a visa to live and study in the United States.</li> 
       <li>You are a <strong>freshman applicant</strong> if you are still in secondary school or have never enrolled at a university.</li> 
       <li>You are a <strong>transfer applicant</strong> if you registered at a university or college after completing secondary school.</li> 
       <li>Permanent <strong>U.S. citizens</strong>, who have completed course work abroad, should follow the international admissions requirements for the course work that has been completed outside of the United States.</li> 
      </ul>
