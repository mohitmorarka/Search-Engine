    <h2 class="long">UCR Prospective Students</h2> 
    <a name="main"></a> 
    <h3>Ready, Set… Grow!</h3> 
    <p>Choosing where you’ll attend college is one of the most important decisions you’ll ever make, and making a good decision requires good information. No matter who you are or what your goals are, <strong>this is the place to start</strong>.</p> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box1"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/undergrad.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Undergraduates</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside combines the programs and prestige of a large, world-class university with the intimacy and personal contact of a much smaller school. Our Undergraduate Admissions office can help you with everything from admissions requirements to student-led campus tours and important deadlines. Check out our <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/">Undergraduate Admissions website</a> and create an account today!</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <br /> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://my.ucr.edu">Create a MyUCR Account</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Academics/collegesSchoolsMajors">Undergraduate Majors</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Admissions/pathsAdmissions">Paths to Admission</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife">Life at UCR</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR">Visit UCR</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://international.ucr.edu/">International Students</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://trc.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">Transfer Resource Center</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box_med"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/medicine.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Medicine</h3> 
       <p>The new School of Medicine will enroll its charter class of 50 students in August 2013. The four-year program leading to the M.D. degree, built upon a successful partnership with UCLA that trained more than 700 physicians, is designed to expand and diversify the region’s physician workforce with a focus on improving the health of medically underserved populations.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <br /> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/">School of Medicine website</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/future_students.html">Message to Prospective Students</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/admissions/">How to Apply</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/admissions/haider_program.html">Thomas Haider Program at the UCR School of Medicine</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box2"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/graduate.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Graduate Studies</h3> 
       <p>With 39 Ph.D. programs, 37 Master's programs and 17 state teaching credential programs, UCR offers a wide range of opportunities for graduate study and research. As the fastest growing university in the University of California system we have constructed new, state-of-the-art buildings and developed new graduate programs to provide unprecedented opportunities for our graduate students.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/">Graduate Division website</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/grad_admissions.html">Graduate Admission Requirements</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/app_deadlines.html">Dates &amp; Deadlines</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/online_app.html">How to Apply</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box3"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/financial.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Financial Aid</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside distributes more than $200 million annually in grant, loan, work-study, and scholarship aid each year. And, with programs like the Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan, we’re doing all we can to help our students fund their education. In short, if you’re ready to attend the University of California, Riverside, then money shouldn’t stand in your way.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/UCRCost/Pages/default.aspx">Cost to Attend</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://finaidestimator.ucr.edu/">Estimate Financial Aid Package</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/typesAid/Pages/Scholarships.aspx">Scholarship Opportunities</a></li> 
        <li><a class="external" title="external site: UC" href="http://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/blueandgold/">Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box4"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/international.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />International Students</h3> 
       <p>Located just a short drive from sunny beaches, Hollywood and Disneyland, UC Riverside offers a prestigious University of California degree in one of the world’s most exciting regions. We offer over 90 academic majors, plus highly respected advanced degrees, professional development programs, English language courses, and admissions counseling specifically for our international students.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://international.ucr.edu/">Undergraduate Admissions</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://internationalstudent.ucr.edu/">Graduate Admissions</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.iep.ucr.edu/english/">Training &amp; English Language Programs</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/international/">International Resources </a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box5"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/extension.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />UCR Extension/Continuing Education</h3> 
       <p>At UCR Extension, we create educational opportunities for learners of all ages. There are no admission requirements — only the desire to learn. Our learning opportunities range from academic enrichment programs for children to professional certificates to self-directed learning groups for individuals of retirement age.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/">UCR Extension</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/new_students.html">How to Enroll</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/admin/student/fees.html">Fees &amp; Tuition</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box6"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/summer.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Summer Sessions</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside's Summer Sessions features over 450 available courses — including many high-demand classes — with a relaxed campus atmosphere and a convenient class schedule. They also sponsor international study opportunities as well as summer programs for high school students and incoming freshmen.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/admission.html">Register for Summer Courses</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/deadlines.html">Deadlines and Dates</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summertravel.ucr.edu/">Summer Study Abroad</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summeracademy.ucr.edu/">Summer Academy</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summerbridge.ucr.edu/">Summer Bridge</a></li> 
       </ul>

    <h2 class="long">UCR Prospective Students</h2> 
    <a name="main"></a> 
    <h3>Ready, Set… Grow!</h3> 
    <p>Choosing where you’ll attend college is one of the most important decisions you’ll ever make, and making a good decision requires good information. No matter who you are or what your goals are, <strong>this is the place to start</strong>.</p> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box1"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/undergrad.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Undergraduates</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside combines the programs and prestige of a large, world-class university with the intimacy and personal contact of a much smaller school. Our Undergraduate Admissions office can help you with everything from admissions requirements to student-led campus tours and important deadlines. Check out our <a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/">Undergraduate Admissions website</a> and create an account today!</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <br /> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://my.ucr.edu">Create a MyUCR Account</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Academics/collegesSchoolsMajors">Undergraduate Majors</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/Admissions/pathsAdmissions">Paths to Admission</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/CampusLife">Life at UCR</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/VisitUCR">Visit UCR</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://international.ucr.edu/">International Students</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://trc.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">Transfer Resource Center</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box_med"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/medicine.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Medicine</h3> 
       <p>The new School of Medicine will enroll its charter class of 50 students in August 2013. The four-year program leading to the M.D. degree, built upon a successful partnership with UCLA that trained more than 700 physicians, is designed to expand and diversify the region’s physician workforce with a focus on improving the health of medically underserved populations.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <br /> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/">School of Medicine website</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/future_students.html">Message to Prospective Students</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/admissions/">How to Apply</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/admissions/haider_program.html">Thomas Haider Program at the UCR School of Medicine</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box2"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/graduate.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Graduate Studies</h3> 
       <p>With 39 Ph.D. programs, 37 Master's programs and 17 state teaching credential programs, UCR offers a wide range of opportunities for graduate study and research. As the fastest growing university in the University of California system we have constructed new, state-of-the-art buildings and developed new graduate programs to provide unprecedented opportunities for our graduate students.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/">Graduate Division website</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/grad_admissions.html">Graduate Admission Requirements</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/app_deadlines.html">Dates &amp; Deadlines</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/online_app.html">How to Apply</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box3"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/financial.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Financial Aid</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside distributes more than $200 million annually in grant, loan, work-study, and scholarship aid each year. And, with programs like the Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan, we’re doing all we can to help our students fund their education. In short, if you’re ready to attend the University of California, Riverside, then money shouldn’t stand in your way.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/UCRCost/Pages/default.aspx">Cost to Attend</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://finaidestimator.ucr.edu/">Estimate Financial Aid Package</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/typesAid/Pages/Scholarships.aspx">Scholarship Opportunities</a></li> 
        <li><a class="external" title="external site: UC" href="http://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/blueandgold/">Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box4"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/international.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />International Students</h3> 
       <p>Located just a short drive from sunny beaches, Hollywood and Disneyland, UC Riverside offers a prestigious University of California degree in one of the world’s most exciting regions. We offer over 90 academic majors, plus highly respected advanced degrees, professional development programs, English language courses, and admissions counseling specifically for our international students.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://international.ucr.edu/">Undergraduate Admissions</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://internationalstudent.ucr.edu/">Graduate Admissions</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.iep.ucr.edu/english/">Training &amp; English Language Programs</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/international/">International Resources </a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box5"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/extension.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />UCR Extension/Continuing Education</h3> 
       <p>At UCR Extension, we create educational opportunities for learners of all ages. There are no admission requirements — only the desire to learn. Our learning opportunities range from academic enrichment programs for children to professional certificates to self-directed learning groups for individuals of retirement age.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/">UCR Extension</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/new_students.html">How to Enroll</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/admin/student/fees.html">Fees &amp; Tuition</a></li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <div class="clear"></div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="l_box" id="l_box6"> 
     <div class="l_divided"> 
      <div class="columnD"> 
       <h3><img src="/future/images/summer.jpg" alt=" " width="87" height="99" />Summer Sessions</h3> 
       <p>UC Riverside's Summer Sessions features over 450 available courses — including many high-demand classes — with a relaxed campus atmosphere and a convenient class schedule. They also sponsor international study opportunities as well as summer programs for high school students and incoming freshmen.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column2R"> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/admission.html">Register for Summer Courses</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summer.ucr.edu/deadlines.html">Deadlines and Dates</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summertravel.ucr.edu/">Summer Study Abroad</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summeracademy.ucr.edu/">Summer Academy</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://summerbridge.ucr.edu/">Summer Bridge</a></li> 
       </ul>

    <div id="main"> 
     <p>Our donors come from all walks of life and each have their own reasons for giving to UCR. Whether from community members, family of graduates or graduates of UCR themselves, each generous donation ensures that UCR can continue its tradition of institutional excellence.</p> 
     <blockquote class="fancy2 section"> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/giving/images/8.2.2.2_give_why_we_give_frazier.jpg" alt="The Frazier Family" width="226" height="254" /><q>We are content in knowing that UCR will continue to provide innumerable opportunities for others as it did for our children. And for that reason we say <q>Thank you.</q></q></p> 
      <p class="quotee">Ruby Frazier<br />Trustee, UCR Foundation</p> 
     </blockquote> 
     <p>The Frazier family came to Riverside in the fall of 2002 when their daughter, Briana, began her college career as a freshman at UCR.</p> 
     <p>The Frazier family credits their move to Riverside as the impetus needed to fulfill their dreams of forming their own real estate company. For that reason and the countless opportunities provided by UCR and the city, Ruby Frazier and her daughters, Jessica ('05), Briana ('06) and Erica, felt compelled to give back to UCR through the Chancellor's Associates Program.</p> 
     <p>Through their contributions, the Fraziers are able to stay informed, become more actively involved and participate financially in the development and growth of the campus.</p> 
     <blockquote class="fancy2 section"> 
      <p><a name="norman"> </a> <img class="img_L" title="Drs. Tony Norman and Helen Henry" src="/giving/images/normanhenry84sm.jpg" alt="Drs. Tony Norman and Helen Henry" width="226" height="254" /><q>It has been very fulfilling to spend our careers on this campus and we want UCR to be able to continue to offer these opportunities to those who follow us.</q></p> 
      <p class="quotee">Dr. Helen Henry and Dr. Anthony Norman</p> 
     </blockquote> 
     <p>Dr. Anthony Norman and Dr. Helen Henry have served on the faculty of the Department of Biochemistry for 47 and 32 years, respectively. During this time they came to appreciate what they described as the &quot;extraordinary opportunity&quot; to spend their academic careers at the same outstanding university, allowing both independence and collaboration in their research.</p> 
     <p>The couple describes UCR as &quot;the jewel&quot; of the University of California, which they believe to be the greatest public university system in the world. They publicly demonstrated their affection and support through a legacy brick outside the Alumni and Visitors Center. Beneath their names, the brick reads: &quot;UCR: The Gem of UC.&quot;</p> 
     <p>Over the last two decades, the wife and husband team of Henry and Norman have demonstrated their gratitude towards the university by providing financial support to a variety of areas, including two where they had a personal interest: the Academic Senate and Intercollegiate Athletics. Both Henry and Norman have been very active in the senate, with Norman serving as chair from 2008 to 2010, and the two can frequently be found at Highlander sporting events. In addition, Norman, a collegiate tennis and soccer player himself at Oberlin College, served for over a decade as the faculty athletic representative for the Highlander program.</p> 
     <blockquote class="fancy2 section"> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/giving/images/8.2.2.2_give_why_we_give_tate_wright.jpg" alt="Dwight Tate and Dr. Katherine Wright" width="226" height="254" /><q>In no small measure, because of our education at UCR, we are fortunate to be able to donate to special UCR programs.</q></p> 
      <p class="quotee">Mr. Dwight Tate and<br /> Dr. Katherine Wright</p> 
     </blockquote> 
     <p>Dwight Tate and Dr. Katherine Wright have deep connections to UC Riverside. As a child, Kathy enjoyed riding her bike through campus and visiting the small market within the married student housing area. She went on to earn her California elementary teaching credential, master’s degree, and administrative services credential from UCR. She is a retired district administrator with Riverside's Alvord Unified School District and interacts with UCR's Graduate School of Education by serving on the Dean's Advisory Board.</p> 
     <p>Born and raised in Riverside, Dwight attended UCR from 1966-68, finishing his bachelor's degree at UC Berkeley. He returned to UCR to obtain a secondary teaching credential in 1971. Staying close to his roots, Dwight works in Riverside.</p>

     <h3 class="section3">Welcome from the Dean</h3> 
     <p>Read the <a href="/dean_letter.html">Dean's welcome message</a> to new and prospective graduate students<strong><br /></strong></p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Deadlines for Application</h3> 
     <p>Check the schedule to find the <a href="/app_deadlines.html">deadlines for applications</a></p> 
     <h3 class="section2">New Applicants</h3> 
     <p>If you are applying to UCR as a graduate student for the first time, or if you have applied before but were not admitted, complete all parts of the<a href="https://gradsis.ucr.edu/gradsis/GSIS_LOGIN.Login_student"> Online Application for Graduate Admission</a> and submit the appropriate application fee. PLEASE REVIEW ALL APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS BY CLICKING ON THE LINKS TO THE LEFT.</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>$80.00 for U.S. citizens and Permanent Residents <strong>(all applicants except those to the <strong><strong>MBA/MPAc/MFIN/FLEX MBA</strong></strong> program)</strong><br /> 
       <ul> 
        <li>$100.00 for U.S. citizens and Permanent Residents (<strong>MBA/MPAc/MFIN/FLEX MBA applicants only</strong>)</li> 
       </ul> </li> 
      <li>$100.00 for international/non-immigrant applicants <strong>(all applicants except those to the <strong><strong>MBA/MPAc/MFIN/FLEX MBA</strong></strong> program)</strong> 
       <ul> 
        <li>$125.00 for international/non-immigrant applicants (<strong>MBA/MPAc/MFIN/FLEX MBA applicants only</strong>)</li> 
       </ul> </li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>The nonrefundable application fee must be paid by credit card (VISA or MasterCard) prior to submission of your application.</p> 
     <p>If you are a <strong>domestic applicant</strong> who is a current or recent participant in a special program (i.e., GEM, McNair, UC LEADs, CAMP, LSAMP, etc.,), you may qualify for an application fee waiver. Please complete and save the first two sections of the application and contact the Graduate Admissions Office: grdadmis@ucr.edu or (951) 827-3313. <strong>Do not pay or submit your application prior to contacting our office, as we are unable to waive or refund an application fee once it has already been paid.</strong></p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Reapplication</h3> 
     <p>If you filed an application for graduate admission at UCR within the last year and were admitted but did not register, submit a new application and appropriate application fee, and any additional or updated information or records. If you applied for admission more than one year ago, you must submit a new application, and all required materials.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Readmission</h3> 
     <p>If you have ever registered as a graduate student at UCR, whether you completed a graduate program or not, complete all parts of the <a href="https://gradsis.ucr.edu/gradsis/GSIS_LOGIN.Login_student">Online Application for Graduate Admission</a>. The application fee must be submitted with the application.</p> 
     <br />Formal application for readmission is not required for: 
     <ul> 
      <li>Students returning from an official leave of absence</li> 
      <li>Currently enrolled UCR graduate students changing from one major to another as long as the there has been no lapse in registered status</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section2">Admission Criteria</h3> 
     <p>Read the <a href="/admiss_criteria.html">general requirements</a> for admission into UCR graduate programs</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li><a href="/domestic.html">US Citizens and US Permanent Residents</a></li> 
      <li><a href="/international.html">International Applicants</a></li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section2">Fees, Fellowships and Financial Assistance</h3> 
     <p>Quarterly <a href="http://classes.ucr.edu/fees/schedule.htm">schedule of fees and tuition</a> is located on the general UCR website. Additional information about <a href="/fin_aid.html">fellowship awards and other financial opportunities</a> for graduate students.</p> 
     <h4 style="padding-left: 30px;">California Residency for Tuition Purposes</h4> 
     <p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="http://www.ucop.edu/general-counsel/_files/ed-affairs/10-things-grad.pdf">10 Things Graduate Students Need To Know About CA Residence for Purposes Of Tuition And Fees</a></p> 
     <p style="padding-left: 30px;">Additional information about <a href="http://registrar.ucr.edu/registrar/tuition-and-fees/residency-for-tuition-purposes.aspx">California Residency for Tuition Purposes</a> can be found on the University of California, Riverside website.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Review and Notification</h3> 
     <p>Detailed review and initial recommendation to admit is made by the programs. However, only the official letter of admission from the Dean of the Graduate Division constitutes approval of admission to a graduate program at UCR, not correspondence with a program or with an individual faculty member.</p> 
     <p>The Graduate Admissions Office will make every effort to notify you of your admission status as soon as possible after receiving the admission recommendation from the program to which you are applying.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Duplication of Degree</h3> 
     <p>Permission to work for a second master's degree in a NEW area may be approved on the individual merit of the application when there is little or no close relationship between the two subjects. Duplication of a master's degree in the same field is not permitted, and duplication of a doctorate is rarely permitted, regardless of the field of study. For example, if you hold an MS in Entomology you will not be approved to enter our master's program in Entomology but could be approved to enter a master's program in Statistics.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Medical Insurance Requirement</h3> 
     <p>The University requires, as a condition of registration, that all graduate and professional students, including international students (on non-immigrant visas), have medical insurance coverage that meets the University's minimum requirements. A campus plan meeting these requirements is available. <a href="http://campushealth.ucr.edu/Insurance/Pages/Graduate.aspx" target="_blank">Find</a> the appropriate contact for details regarding the campus Graduate Student Health Insurance Plan (GSHIP) or regarding the campus minimum requirements.<br /><br />Fellowship recipients whose awards pay all assessed registration fees will have their premiums paid by the fellowship. Students who can demonstrate comparable insurance coverage from another source may apply to be exempted from the mandatory plan.&nbsp;Students awarded the exemption have the GSHIP fee removed from their bill but do not receive any monetary compensation<span style="color: red;">. </span>Deadlines for applying for the exemption are firm.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Ethnic Identity and U.S. Military Service</h3> 
     <p>Providing information about your ethnic identity or U.S. Military service is voluntary. The Graduate Admissions Office uses ethnicity information to measure the effectiveness of its efforts towards achieving diversity and to fulfill state and federal reporting requirements. Information concerning veteran status allows our campus to provide information about campus resources for veterans.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Social Security Number Disclosure</h3> 
     <p>Providing your Social Security Number on the UCR graduate application is voluntary. This information is used to match your applicant file to GRE/GMAT test scores received electronically and is required for the processing of the FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) application. Without this piece of matching criteria, all test score verification and subsequent application processing could be delayed. In addition, the Social Security Number is required before the dispersal of any stipend, fee payment or employment salary as part of a Graduate Fellowship or Financial Support package. Applicants without Social Security Numbers will have financial aid award packaging delayed. If not provided on the original graduate application, newly admitted students would be required upon arrival on campus to present the actual <strong>Social Security Number card</strong> for verification at the Office of the Registrar.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">State of California Information Practices Act</h3> 
     <p>The State of California Information Practices Act of 1977 requires the University to provide the following information to applicants for admission who are asked to supply information about themselves; The principal purpose for requesting the information on this form is to process your application for admission for which positive identification of academic records and test scores is required. In addition, if you are applying for financial aid, positive identification must be determined as well as eligibility for aid. Maintenance of this information is authorized by University policy. The officials responsible for maintaining the information contained on the application are the Chairperson of the program to which you are applying and the Dean of the Graduate Division.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">UCR Nondiscrimination Policy</h3> 
     <p>The University of California, in accordance with applicable Federal and State law and University policy, does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, disability, age, medical condition (cancer-related), ancestry, marital status, citizenship, sexual orientation, or status as a Vietnam-era veteran or special disabled veteran. The University also prohibits sexual harassment. This nondiscrimination policy covers admission, access, employment, and treatment in University programs and activities. Inquiries regarding the University's student-related nondiscrimination policies may be directed to the Affirmative Action officer, (951) 827-5604.</p>

     <p><a href="http://specialservices.ucr.edu" target="_blank">Student Special Services</a> provides services for students with disabilities, veteran's educational benefits, ROTC referral, Selective Service and Draft Information, voter registration</p>

     <p>UCR online <a href="http://catalog.ucr.edu/">general catalog</a></p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Campus Security and Crime Report</h3> 
     <p><a href="http://police.ucr.edu/clery/clery.html">The &quot;Jeanne CLERY Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics</a> Act of 1998,&quot; commonly referred to as the &quot;CLERY Act,&quot; requires institutions of higher education receiving federal financial aid to report specified crime statistics on college campuses and to provide other safety and crime information to members of the campus community.</p>

      <h3>Public Safety</h3> 
      <h4>UCR Police Department</h4> 
      <p>The <a href="http://police.ucr.edu/">UCR Police Department</a> is a professional police agency staffed by individuals who provide the highest standards of conduct and performance. To contact the police in a non-emergency situation, please call (951) 827-5222. In the event of an emergency, please call 911.</p> 
      <h4 class="section2">University Neighborhood Enhancement Team (UNET)</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://police.ucr.edu/unet/about.html">University Neighborhood Enhancement Team (UNET)</a> is a cooperative effort between the University of California Police Department at Riverside and the Riverside Police Department to cover the 17-square-mile area surrounding the university. In addition to responding to calls for service between the hours of 5:00am and 1:00am, officers are encouraged to identify community concerns and seek out novel approaches to solving those concerns.</p> 
      <h4 class="section2">City of Riverside Police Department</h4> 
      <p>The <a href="http://www.riversideca.gov/rpd/">City of Riverside Police Department</a> partners with the UCR Police Department to provide a safe and secure environment for all citizens. For general information, please call (951) 826-5700. </p>

       <h3>Yadong Yin</h3> 
       <p>The Idea Zoo, Inc., a leading developer and licensor of advanced materials and technologies, is partnering with BASF, the world’s leading chemical company, to jointly introduce COLR™ Technology, an on-demand photonic crystal color changing technology. Invented by UCR Prof. Yadong Yin, senior advisor to Idea Zoo, Inc., the technology utilizes a now patented synthesis process to create superparamagnetic crystals that can be controlled magnetically to refract specific wave lengths of light to create a form of structural color.</p>
