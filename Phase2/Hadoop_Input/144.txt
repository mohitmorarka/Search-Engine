      <h3>How to Schedule Academic Course Activities</h3> 
      <p>If you have been designated to schedule course activities for your department, we can help. Here is how it works:</p> 
      <div class="wizard-wrapper"> 
       <div class="wizard-content border1px" style="width: 378px; min-height: 310px; float: right; margin-right: 20px"> 
        <div id="tab1" class="verticalslider_contents  verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <h3>Receive the &quot;Call&quot;</h3> 
         <br /> 
         <p>Your department will receive a &quot;call&quot; memo before the quarter begins. The &quot;call&quot; memo includes these helpful documents: </p> 
         <ul class="orange-bullets wide"> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/call_memo_14W_060413.pdf" target="_blank">Current &quot;Call&quot; Memo</a></li> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/scheduling_guidelines  .pdf" target="_blank">Scheduling Guidelines</a></li> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/general_ assignment_classroom_list_111612.pdf" target="_blank">General Assignment Classroom List</a></li> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/standard_class_times_campus_and_ uvillage.pdf" target="_blank">Standard Class Times - Main Campus and University Village</a></li> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/memo_and_codes_for_sis_020613 MEMO AND CODES.pdf" target="_blank">Memo and Codes for SIS</a></li> 
          <li><a href="../Content/pdfs/sis_screen_131_prerequisites_060812.pdf" target="_blank">SIS Screen 131 - Registration Control Input Procedures</a></li> 
         </ul> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab2" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <h3>Scheduling Courses in SIS</h3> 
         <p>Using the “call&quot; memo documents, you will enter course information into the Student Information System (SIS) in accordance with <a href="Deadlines.aspx">Academic Scheduling deadlines.</a></p> 
         <p><a href="adding-updating-in-sis.aspx">Learn how to attend SIS training, gain access to the system and download a SIS manual here.</a></p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab3" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <h3>Scheduling Rooms and Special Requests</h3> 
         <p>You can request specific classrooms, buildings, equipment and more for your course. Be as specific as necessary. If for any reason we can’t accommodate your request, we will contact you with alternative solutions.</p> 
         <p>To reserve a room for a one-time event tied to your course, see <a href="one-time-events.aspx">One-Time Events.</a></p>

      <p>Communications places fundamental value on responsive customer service, timely infrastructure renewal and replacement, and providing a very robust and reliable production environment that offers a broad range of services and support. In addition, emphasis is placed on providing an appropriate level of network security and offering the campus an innovative vision of potential services and support that will enable the University to meet its teaching, research, and public service goals.</p>

      <p>The mission of C&amp;C's Communications unit is to facilitate UCR's teaching, research, and public service efforts by providing reliable, secure, innovative, and technically sophisticated infrastructure and services. As communications provides these services, the unit will operate within budget and per the campus approved rate schedule.</p>
