     <h3>UCR’s Annual New Student Convocation will take place on Monday, September 23, 2013 at 4:45 p.m. at the Student Recreation Center</h3> 
     <p>All incoming freshmen and transfer students are encouraged to attend this ceremonial induction into the UCR family designed to promote the shared values of the university – free inquiry, intellectual honesty, personal integrity, and respect for human dignity.</p> 
     <p>The ceremony will begin with a formal procession of faculty and administrators in full academic regalia. Chancellor Kim A. Wilcox, Vice Chancellor of Student Affairs James W. Sandoval, and Associated Students President Sahil Patadia will present remarks.</p> 
     <p>Immediately following the Convocation, please stay for the traditional R'Side Rally at the HUB plaza.</p> 
     <p>Questions about the New Student Convocation should be addressed to the office of Event Management &amp; Protocol at (951) 827-3144 or by e-mail to <a href="mailto:specialevents@ucr.edu?subject=Convocation%20Web%20Site%20Inquiry">specialevents@ucr.edu</a></p>
