     <h2 class="intro"> Who We Are</h2> 
     <h3 class="verd intro"> At UC Riverside, you are surrounded by the most diverse, <br /> talented and driven students in the world.</h3> 
     <div class="grid_4 alpha statsSection"> 
      <div> 
       <h3 class="darkBlue giant"> #1</h3> 
       <p> The place a <a href="http://www.engr.ucr.edu/"></a><a href="http://ucrtoday.ucr.edu/5235">Bourns College of Engineering student team</a> took at a 2012 international environmental design competition. They created a reusable storm drain oil filter out of 100 percent recycled materials.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <h3 class="lightBlue giant"> 800</h3> 
       <p> The number of volunteer hours <a href="http://soba.ucr.edu/">School of Business Administration</a> students have logged with Habitat for Humanity since 2011. Just one example of how students earned our spot on the <a href="http://ucrtoday.ucr.edu/4210">President’s Higher Education Community Service Honor Roll with Distinction.</a></p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <h3 class="darkBlue giant"> 21</h3> 
       <p> The age undergraduate <a href="http://ucrtoday.ucr.edu/7737">Brian Weden</a> was when he beat out graduate students from seven other countries (and Florida) to win the 2012 Institute of&nbsp;Materials, Minerals and Mining (IOM3) international lecture competition in London. </p> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="grid_4"> 
      <img class="shuffle" src="" alt="" />

     <div class="grid_4 omega"> 
      <div class="genericBody version2"> 
       <h3> Our Students Are…</h3> 
       <h4> Diverse</h4> 
       <p> We are the<a href="http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/rankings/national-universities/campus-ethnic-diversity"> 8th-most-diverse university in the nation</a> because our students come from multiple cultures, socioeconomic backgrounds and parts of the world. <a href="http://www.ucr.edu/about/facts.html"> Learn more about our diverse and high-achieving student body</a><a href="http://diversity.ucr.edu/"></a>. </p> 
       <h4> Talented</h4> 
       <p> From Nobel Prize-winning <a href="http://newsroom.ucr.edu/1173">Dr. Richard R. Schrock</a> to Pulitzer Prize-winning <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/page.aspx?pid=548">Steve Breen</a> to gifted US Poet Laureate <a href="http://newsroom.ucr.edu/118">Billy Collins</a>, it’s clear that talented people graduate from UCR. Get to know more <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/page.aspx?pid=551"> notable UCR alumni</a>. </p> 
       <h4> Driven</h4> 
       <p> More than half of our most recent graduates were the first in their family to go to college. <a href="http://www.ucr.edu/about/promise/education.html">We educate more first-generation college students than any other UC</a>. And our students don’t stop here. Many go on to graduate school, medical school and jobs with excellent earning potential. <a href="http://www.highlandernews.org/2082/uc-riverside-graduates-salaries-continue-to-rise/"> Our graduate salaries continue to rise</a>.</p> 
       <h4> Glad to meet you</h4> 
       <p> See what UCR students are up to right now on <a href="http://lifeatucr.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">Tumblr</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/ucradmissions" target="_blank">Twitter</a> and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ucradmissions?bcode=q099X" target="_blank">Facebook</a> or meet them in person at <a href="http://highlanderday.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">Highlander Day</a>. </p> 
      </div>
