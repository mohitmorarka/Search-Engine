     <div class="span-7"> 
      <h4>All About Waitlists</h4> 
      <p>Some full courses offer a waitlist option during registration. If so, you will either see a message inviting you to add yourself to the waitlist or the course status will read, <strong>“CLOSED, Waitlist Only.”</strong></p> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-11 last"> 
      <h3 class="warning">Important</h3> 
      <ul class="orange-bullets"> 
       <li>Being on a waitlist does not guarantee eventual enrollment in a course.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Waitlist units count toward the max number of units allowed. Once that max has been reached, you will not be able to add any more units to your course load until you either drop a class or remove yourself from a waitlist.</li> 
       <li>Because being on a waitlist impacts your total enrolled units, <strong>it may also impact your financial aid</strong>. Contact <a href="http://financialaid.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">Financial Aid</a> before you wait-list for a class.</li> 
      </ul> 
     </div> 
     <div class="span-18 last"> 
      <h3>Waitlist Essentials</h3> 
      <div class="wizard-wrapper"> 
       <div class="wizard-content border1px" style="width: 379px; min-height: 258px; float: right; margin-right: 20px"> 
        <div id="tab1" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <img src="../../Images/waitlist/waitlist.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 5px 0 25px 0;" /> 
         <h3>Add Yourself to a Waitlist</h3> 
         <p> Select &quot;Waitlist&quot; from the action menu. You will be added to the waitlist if you have met all course prerequisites, have no scheduling conflicts and have not yet reached your maximum units allowed. </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab2" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <img src="../../Images/waitlist/removeWaitlist.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 25px 0;" /> 
         <h3>Remove Yourself From a Waitlist</h3> 
         <p> If you do not want to wait-list for a course any longer, you must take action to remove yourself from it. Otherwise, you will be registered automatically if space becomes available! You can remove yourself from the waitlist by selecting “Drop” from the action menu (just as you would do to drop a class). </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab3" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <img src="../../Images/waitlist/testTubes.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 25px 0;" /> 
         <h3>Labs and Discussion Sections</h3> 
         <p> If you are registered in one lab or discussion section, but want to wait-list for another, you can do so for one section. After you have registered the first section, return to the enrollment menu and enter the call number for the section you want to wait-list for. Choose &quot;Waitlist&quot; from the action menu and submit your request. Your course confirmation will reflect registration in the first section and waitlist status in the second. </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="tab4" class="verticalslider_contents verticalSliderContentPadleft20"> 
         <img src="../../Images/waitlist/class.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 25px 0;" /> 
         <h3>How Do I Know if I Got Into a Class?</h3> 
         <p> If space becomes available in a class and you are on its waitlist, you will be enrolled automatically and an email will be sent to your R'Mail account. Likewise, if you didn’t make it into the class, you will be notified via R’Mail when the waitlist period is over (at the end of the second week of instruction). </p> 
        </div> 
       </div>
