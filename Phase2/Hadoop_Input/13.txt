     <p>Whether you're a freshman, a transfer student or you're about to graduate, we're here to support you academically, socially and physically. We want to make it easy for you to take advantage of the endless opportunities at UCR to learn, contribute, get fit and have fun. It's all here.</p>

      <h3>Richard and Laura Small</h3> 
      <p> Little did Dr. Richard Small know his student job at UCR's Citrus Experiment Station would come in handy years later. Richard and his wife, Laura, a food safety instructor at University of Nevada, Las Vegas, live on a 33-acre avocado farm in Fallbrook, Calif., near Escondido. </p> 
      <p> A zoology major at UCR, he graduated in 1961 with good friends Drs. Fred Bryant and Carl Fuglie. Richard went on to medical school at UCLA and became a radiation oncologist. </p> 
      <h4>The Gift </h4> 
      <p> The Smalls have supported UCR since 1977. Since 2004, they've donated $10,000 a year to the Carl Fuglie Scholarship fund, in memory of his friend who died of cancer in his 30s. Last year, Richard and Fred Bryant started the Bryant-Small Scholarship to also benefit a deserving medical student. </p> 
      <h4>Why did he do it? </h4> 
      <p> &quot;I think of it as payback to UCR,&quot; said Richard. And he means it in the nicest of ways. &quot;UCR was instrumental in guiding me to my occupation. To my goals. I want to help others get there, too.&quot; </p> 
      <h4>The Future </h4> 
      <p> One of the young people whom he's helping get there is daughter Jacquie, a community college student. She'd like to follow in her dad's footsteps and become a doctor. </p>

     <h3>Things You Can Do at UCR</h3> 
     <p> There is more to life at UC Riverside than just books and class. We invite you to visit our beautiful campus, to attend and participate in some of our events and become a part of our community and our family. </p> 
     <p>UCR hosts a variety of events and performers each week, all made available to the public via our <a href="/happenings/">Happenings page</a>.&nbsp; A day-to-day list of every event scheduled on campus can be found on the online <a href="http://www.events.ucr.edu/cgi-bin/display.cgi">Events Calendar</a>.</p> 
     <p>For more on some of the things that UCR has to offer, <a href="/resources/">please visit our Campus Resources website</a>.</p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4>ARTSblock</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://www.artsblock.ucr.edu/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/artsblock2.jpg" alt="ARTSblock" width="93" height="46" /></a>Three spaces  the<a href="http://culvercenter.ucr.edu/"> Barbara and Art Culver Center of the Arts</a>, the <a href="http://www.cmp.ucr.edu/">California Museum of Photography</a> and the <a href="http://sweeney.ucr.edu/">Sweeney Art Gallery</a>  form <a href="http://www.artsblock.ucr.edu/">ARTSblock</a>; a regional performing and visual arts center.</p> 
      <p>ARTSblock is the result of a collaborative effort between UCR and the city of Riverside to bring new and ambitious art exhibits and events to Southern California.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">UC Riverside Presents</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://culturalevents.ucr.edu/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/dance.jpg" alt="UCR Presents" width="93" height="46" /></a> <a href="http://culturalevents.ucr.edu/">UC Riverside Presents</a> is a bi-monthly performing arts presentation that brings performers and performances to the university for the enjoyment of students and art lovers alike. From contemporary dance to classical jazz, the program attracts artists of exceptional quality. All events are open to the public.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">Intercollegiate Athletics</h4> 
      <p><a href="/athletics/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/athletics_thumb.jpg" alt="Athletics" width="93" height="46" /></a><a href="/athletics/">UC Riverside's Intercollegiate Athletics program</a> features 17 sports competing at the NCAA Division I level in the Big West Conference.</p> 
      <p>All Highlander home athletics events are open to the public. Admission prices vary, depending on the event. <a href="http://www.gohighlanders.com">Visit gohighlanders.com, the official athletics website, for up-to-date schedules and announcements</a>.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">UCR Botanic Gardens</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://gardens.ucr.edu/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/gardens.jpg" alt="UCR Botanic Gardens" width="93" height="46" /></a> The <a href="http://gardens.ucr.edu/">UCR Botanic Gardens</a> is a living plant museum with more than 3,500 plant species from around the world for visitors and researchers to see and study. It was established primarily for teaching purposes. In a beautiful setting, the gardens serves to provide a wide assortment of plant materials for courses at UCR and other local schools.</p> 
      <h4 class="section">Lifelong Learning (Select UNEX Classes)</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/osher/"><img class="img_L" src="/friends/images/lifelong_thumb.jpg" alt="Lifelong Learning" width="93" height="46" /></a> The <a href="http://www.extension.ucr.edu/osher/">UC Riverside Osher Lifelong Learning Institute</a> was created to provide people over the age of 50 the opportunity to continue their education through stimulating courses taught in a variety of subjects.</p> 
      <p>Our courses are only open to OLLI members, so learning takes place in a peer environment. There are no tests or grades and no entrance exams or minimum educational background restrictions  the only requirement is a love of learning.</p>

     <p>We understand the challenges you face as an employer in recruiting college graduates.</p> 
     <p>Recruiting at UC Riverside brings with it all the advantages of the prestigious University of California system, as well as our own unique traditions and heritage. Our students have unparalleled access to cutting-edge research with world-class faculty, providing them with invaluable training and experience for todays jobs. UC Riverside students are prepared to fit the needs of your company today. We offer the largest Business Administration undergraduate degree program of any UC in Southern California, and our students excel in high demand fields such as engineering, nano-technology, bio-engineering, computer science, chemistry and biological sciences.</p>
