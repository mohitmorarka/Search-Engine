       <h3>New Conference Service: ReadyTalk</h3> 
       <p>Computing &amp; Communications is pleased to announce the availability of a new conference call service. Provided by ReadyTalk, this service offers both audio and web conferencing services.</p> 
      </div>

      <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="maincontent" group="Everyone" button="657" -->

      <h3>What is ReadyTalk?</h3> 
      <p>ReadyTalk easily provides Audio and Web Conferencing Services.</p> 
      <p>This service offers many features, is available at any time, requires no reservations, and can support up to 96 participants per call. ReadyTalk offers significant cost savings over AT&amp;T and other conference call services.</p> 
      <p>ReadyTalk can help to reduce department’s travel costs by connecting people anywhere in the world.</p> 
      <p><a class="linkMore" href="/conference_calls/faq.html">ReadyTalk FAQs</a></p>
