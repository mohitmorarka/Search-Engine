     <h3>Staying Busy, Both On- and Off-Campus</h3> 
     <p>There is always something to do in Riverside. UCR&nbsp; is a campus that surges with activity. With over 300 clubs, exciting Division I athletics and fine arts and musical performances, including up-and-coming bands at the Barn and the Bell Tower, there is enough to keep anyone busy. Most on campus events feature free or discounted admission to current undergraduate students with a UCR ID.</p> 
     <p>Off campus, the Riverside community teems with entertainment and culinary opportunities. If you can't find something to do, you just need to look around a bit.</p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <ul class="big_links2"> 
       <li><a href="/resources/art_culture/"><img src="/students/images/arts_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>Arts &amp; Culture</strong> The arts at UCR will engage your senses, ignite your intellect and spark your imagination. From concerts and theater to dance and art exhibits, UCR's creative spirit and energetic diversity have earned us a reputation across the region as a thriving arts community.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="/happenings/"><img src="/students/images/events_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>Events Calendar</strong> Check out our Happenings Calendar to keep up-to-date on upcoming concerts, sporting events, guest lectures, performing arts, exhibits and other special events at UCR!</a></li> 
       <li><a href="/resources/kucr.html"><img src="/students/images/kucr_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>KUCR</strong> From cutting-edge indie rock and progressive hip-hop to jazz and classical by the world's great performers and orchestras, KUCR has been the voice of UC Riverside since 1966. As the educational, noncommercial radio station managed and operated by UCR students, faculty and staff, KUCR’s mission is to offer an alternative to what’s available in the commercial broadcast marketplace. Check us out at 88.3 FM!</a></li> 
       <li><a href="/students/clubs.html"><img src="/students/images/clubs_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>Get Involved with Clubs and Orgs</strong>Whatever your interests, you'll find a club or organization with other like-minded students at UC Riverside. With nearly 300 clubs and organizations, including fraternities and sororities, there are always lots of opportunities to get involved. </a></li> 
       <li><a href="/students/campusevents.html"><img class="border" src="/students/images/rside_events.jpg" alt="R'side Events" title="R'side Events" width="93" height="46" /><strong>R'Side Events</strong> From Block Party to HEAT to Spring Splash, find out about our annual festivals that bring big named artists to campus.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://www.recreation.ucr.edu/"><img src="/students/images/rec_center_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>Student Recreation Center</strong> Take part in a game of pick-up basketball or try a yoga class at our 80,000-square-foot Student Recreation Center. The SRC is the hub of recreational, intramural and club sports on campus, as well as our Outdoor Excursions Program.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="/athletics/"><img src="/students/images/athletics_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" /><strong>Support Your Highlanders</strong> UC Riverside Intercollegiate Athletics is a proud member on NCAA Division I and the Big West Conference. The Highlanders compete in 17 sports. Home events are free for undergraduate students to attend (with UCR ID). Get out and support your fellow students and show your UCR spirit!</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://sustainability.ucr.edu/special/"><img src="/students/images/sustainability2.jpg" alt="sustainability image" width="93" height="46" /><strong>Sustainability</strong> Along with implementing environmentally responsible standards campus-wide, the Office of Sustainability creates opportunities for you to become educated and involved with green practices. Find out about special events, programming and volunteer and internship positions, and help us build a promising future.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://www.facebook.com/UCRiverside"><img class="border" src="/future/images/facebook.jpg" alt="UCR on Facebook" width="93" height="46" /><strong>UCR on Facebook</strong> Are you on Facebook? Become a fan of UC Riverside! Visit our page to get the pulse of life at UCR.</a></li> 
       <li><a href="http://www.riversideca.gov/sg/"><img class="border" src="/students/images/riverside_todo.jpg" alt="Things to Do in Riverside" width="93" height="46" /><strong>Things for Students to Do in Riverside</strong> The City of Riverside has put together &quot;Stomping Grounds,&quot; which will help you discover the vast array of unique shops, bookstores, markets, restaurants, theaters, galleries and more, which Riverside has to offer.</a></li> 
      </ul>

UCR’s <a href="http://provost.ucr.edu/5yrcal.html">Five-Year Academic Calendar</a> has all the important dates your student needs to know for the next several quarters. Enrollment deadlines are on our current year <a href="http://registrar.ucr.edu/registrar/academic-calendar/default.aspx">Academic Calendar</a>. And our <a href="http://events.ucr.edu/cgi-bin/display.cgi">Events Calendar</a> keeps you up to date on what’s happening on campus.</p>

      <p><img class="img_L" src="/parents/images/orientation_thumb.jpg" alt=" " width="93" height="46" />UCR's <a href="http://orientation.ucr.edu/">Highlander Orientation</a> Program is mandatory for all incoming first-year students. It gives students the opportunity to enroll in fall classes, meet with an advisor, learn their way around campus, meet other students, and find out tips that will help them academically. And we invite parents to be a part of it, too. The <a href="http://orientation.ucr.edu/family/Pages/Family.aspx">Highlander Family Orientation</a> (on Day 1 of the student orientation), provides you with the tools and information needed to assist in your student's transition to college life and academic success.</p>

With <a href="/future/clubs.html">about 200 clubs and organizations</a>, including fraternities and sororities, there are always lots of opportunities to get involved. <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/">Search our database of groups</a>.</p>

    <!-- Start Sidebar --> 
    <div id="sidebar"> 
     <div class="nav"> 
      <h3 class="accessibility">Main Navigation</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a href="/giving/" title="Giving Home">Giving Home<br /><span>A smart investment... with tangible results</span></a></li>
       <li class="currentpage"><h3><dfn>Current page is: </dfn>How to Give to UCR</h3></li>
       <li><a href="/giving/areas-support.html" title="Areas to Support">Areas to Support<br /><span>Areas and opportunities for giving</span></a></li>

     <p> <a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/GivingForm.aspx" id="l_give_now"><span class="accessibility">Give Now</span></a> </p>

    <hr /> 
    <!-- End Sidebar --> 
    <!-- Start Content Area --> 
    <div id="main"> 
     <p><img src="/giving/images/main/howtogive.jpg" alt=" " width="484" height="145" /></p> 
     <div class="expander_shaded_box"> 
      <h3><img src="/giving/images/ttl_how_to_support.png" alt=" " width="447" height="32" /><span class="accessibility">How to Support UCR Right Now</span></h3> 
      <p>These gifts&nbsp;— often consisting of cash and/or appreciated securities&nbsp;— benefit the university immediately and provide you, the donor, with immediate tax savings.</p> 
      <ul class="striped alt2"> 
       <li><strong>By Check </strong><br />
        <ol class="tight"> 
         <li>Make your check payable to &quot;UCR Foundation&quot;.</li> 
         <li>If not supporting the general fund, please indicate a fund you would like to support, such as a specific scholarship fund.</li> 
         <li>Send to: UCR Foundation, PO Box 112, Riverside, CA 92502-0112</li> 
        </ol><span class="xpander">If you would like to hand-deliver your gift, please bring it to our office at:<br />Hinderaker Hall #1100,<br />900 University Avenue,<br />Riverside, CA<br />Please phone us at (951) 827-5611 to confirm our office hours.</span></li> 
       <li><strong>By Credit Card</strong><br /><span class="xpander"><span class="xpander">Our <a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/Givingform.aspx">online giving page</a>allows you to easily make a credit card gift or pledge payment.</span></span>
        <ol class="tight"> 
         <li>Click the &quot;<a href="https://advancementservices.ucr.edu/Givingform.aspx">Give Online Now</a>&quot; link.</li> 
         <li>Complete secured online credit card form.</li> 
         <li>Verify and submit credit card gift.</li> 
         <li>Print credit card confirmation.</li> 
        </ol><span class="xpander"><br />To make you gift by phone, please call (951) 827-5611. </span></li> 
       <li><strong>By Payroll Deduction</strong><br /><span class="xpander"><span class="xpander">If you are a UCR employee, you may authorize UCR’s Payroll Department to take a monthly deduction from your paycheck and designate a campus program, school or department to receive your gift.</span></span>
        <ol class="tight"> 
         <li><a href="/giving/doc/payrolldeduct.doc">Download the payroll deduction form.</a>.</li> 
         <li>Print the form, fill it out, sign and send this form to:</li> 
        </ol> <p>UCR Foundation<br />Hinderaker Hall #1100<br />900 University Ave.<br />Riverside, CA 92521</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
     </div> 
     <div class="expander_shaded_box">
      <h3><img src="/giving/images/ttl_supporting_ucr.png" alt=" " width="433" height="27" /><span class="accessibility">Supporting UCR With Other Gifts</span></h3> 
      <p>Whether you are looking to make a gift now or planning for future giving through bequests in your will, IRA or retirement accounts, charitable annuities or trusts, the Office of Planned Giving can help. Please contact us at (951) 827-3793 or e-mail <a href="mailto:giftplanning@ucr.edu">giftplanning@ucr.edu</a>.</p> 
      <ul class="striped alt2"> 
       <li><strong>Gifts of Real Estate</strong><br /> <span class="xpander">Gifts of appreciated real estate provide you with an immediate tax deduction and capital gains tax savings. Real estate donations can be made immediately or through your will or living trust. Donors can also make a tax-deductible current gift of their home while continuing to live in and enjoy it for their lifetimes with a gift of retained life interest.</span></li> 
       <li><strong>Gifts of Personal Property</strong><br /> <span class="xpander">Almost any form of property, such as works of art, antiques and other personal items can be a suitable charitable gift. In many cases, the full appraised value of the property on the date of the gift will be credited to the university.</span></li> 
       <li><strong>Gifts of Intellectual Property</strong><br /> <span class="xpander">Gifts of intellectual property such as copyrights, patents, royalties, licenses, and technical data are welcome. The tax and legal provisions that govern these gifts vary according to the type of property.</span></li>

        <section class="column first tl"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           Where will I live?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>UC Riverside offers a variety of on-campus housing options including three residence hall communities, seven apartment communities and a 55-acre community for students with families. Some living styles include fully furnished rooms and the option of a dining plan.</p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="/CampusLife/housing">Learn More About Your Housing Options</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           Can I afford it?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>You can afford UCR! Not only are we a <a href="http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/rankings/national-universities/best-value">Top 25 Best Value School, </a>we rank higher than <em>any other UC campus</em>. Eighty-three percent of our undergraduate students receive financial aid. Of those students, 70% have <em>all of their system-wide fees</em> covered with scholarships and grants. </p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="/PayingForCollege/costEstimator">See How Affordable it is to Attend UC Riverside</a> <a href="/PayingForCollege/financialAidScholarships">Learn More About Scholarships, Grants and Loans</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           Will I get accepted? 
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>UC Riverside seeks extraordinary students and admission is competitive. We consider your academic record, test scores and the challenge of your coursework. We give special consideration to first-generation college students and those from low-income families. We also offer programs that guarantee your admission. </p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="/Admissions/pathsAdmissions">See Admission Requirements and Learn More About Our Selection Process.</a> <a href="/WhyUCR/ourGuarantee">Guarantee Your Admission</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column tr"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           When are the deadlines?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>Remember, November! Submit your UC application for undergraduate admission, November 1-30. Other dates and deadlines, plus detailed timeline checklists, can be found on our <a href="/Admissions/datesDeadlines">Dates and Deadlines page.</a> </p> 
           <p><a href="https://twitter.com/ucradmissions">Follow us on Twitter</a> for Deadline Reminders</p> 
          </div> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column bl"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           How do I apply?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>You may begin your <a href="http://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/admissions/how-to-apply/apply-online/index.html">online UC application</a> in October and submit it November 1-30. Let us help you with step-by-step instructions for completing your application with success. </p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="/Apply">Learn How to Apply to UC Riverside</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote>
           What about diversity?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>UC Riverside is proud to be the 8th most diverse university in the nation. Not only are our students exceptionally diverse, they're high achieving and academically successful. </p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="http://diversity.ucr.edu/">Learn More About Diversity at UC Riverside</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section> 
        <section class="column"> 
         <article class="article"> 
          <span>See The Answer</span> 
          <blockquote style="padding-left: 30px;">
           Will I fit in?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>Yes! We rank among the top ten most diverse universities in the nation. Connect through our <a href="http://studentlife.ucr.edu/STUDENTORGANIZATIONS/Pages/studentOrganizations.aspx">many student organizations</a>, feel at home in <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/liveUCR/special-interest-housing.aspx">special interest housing</a> and get involved with our historic <a href="https://www.ucr.edu/students/ethnic_gender.html">Ethnic and Gender Programs</a>. Fitting in is easy at UCR and that makes us stand out! </p> 
          </div> 
          <p class="readMore clearfix"> <a href="/WhyUCR/ourStudents">Learn More About Our Students</a> <a href="/CampusLife">Explore Campus Life</a> </p> 
         </article> 
        </section>

           What should I major in?
          </blockquote> 
          <div> 
           <p>Find your perfect major by exploring the programs, benefits, opportunities and faculty connected to our unique colleges and schools. When you choose a major at UC Riverside, you select from some of the best academic programs in the nation.</p>

          <p class="readMore"> <a href="/Academics/collegesSchoolsMajors">Explore UC Riverside's Colleges, Schools and Majors</a> </p>

     <h3>Become Part of the UCR Family</h3> 
     <p> UCR is an exciting place to work. We take pride in our nationally-recognized diversity, working together to prepare the next generation of world leaders and to support pioneering research with economic, scientific and social impact on real-world challenges faced by Californians and society at large. </p> 
     <ul> 
      <li> <a href="/faculty/">The </a><a href="/faculty/personnel.html">Office of Academic Personnel</a> is a key resource for the recruitment, development and retention of faculty at UCR. </li> 
      <li><a href="/faculty/applying.html">Learn about the process of applying</a> for and receiving a faculty appointment to UC Riverside. </li> 
     </ul>
     <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     <div class="nota-bene">

      <h4>University Scholars Program: Recognizing Great Educators</h4> 
      <p> The university recognizes its outstanding young faculty through the University Scholars program, which is designed for tenured faculty members who are in the early stages of their careers. </p> 
      <p> The awards are selected and given by the <a href="http://provost.ucr.edu/">Office of the Executive Vice-Chancellor and Provost </a>, and are designed to help young scholars reach their full potential.</p>
      <!-- /com.omniupdate.div -->

     <h3><img id="backpack" class="img_R_borderless" src="/faculty/images/backpack.png" alt=" " width="168" height="174" />Going Abroad — Not Just for Students</h3> 
     <p> The <a href="http://summerstudyabroad.ucr.edu/ucrfaculty.html">Summer Travel Program</a> features UCR faculty teaching a general catalog course to students in an academic environment in a foreign land. Participation of faculty is what makes this program work. Visit the <a href="http://summerstudyabroad.ucr.edu/ucrfaculty.html">Summer Travel Program</a> website for more information on how to submit a proposal.</p>
