    <div id="main"> 
     <p> <img alt="No matter who you are or what your career goals are, UC Riverside has an opportunity for you." src="/images/employ/employment_main.jpg" height="214" width="484" /></p> 
     <p> Regardless of whether you are a current member of the UCR family, or just considering joining us as a student, staff member or faculty, we have opportunities for you. UCR is the premier research university in Inland Southern California and we are seeking the best to join us. Explore your opportunities below.</p> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/staff_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Staff</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li><a href="http://hr.ucr.edu/jobs/">The Jobs Page at UCR Human Resources</a> is the place to find complete listings of all staff employment opportunities on campus.</li> 
      <li><a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/?content=EducationAndDevelopment/abouted.htm">Education and Development courses</a>, offered through <a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/">UCR Human Resources</a>, give employees the opportunity for career growth that can enhance their contributions to the University’s missions and goals.</li> 
      <li>Labor Relations, including Collective Bargaining, and Workers’ Compensation are handled through <a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/">UCR Human Resources</a>.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/faculty_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Faculty</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li>The <a href="http://academicpersonnel.ucr.edu/employment/">Office of Academic Personnel</a> oversees the hiring of faculty and academic staff at UC Riverside. Position openings are listed on departmental sites as well as on the <a class="external" title="external site: SoCalHERC" href="http://socalherc.org/home/index.cfm?site_id=761">Southern California Higher Education Recruitment Consortium (SoCalHERC)</a>.</li> 
      <li>There are great opportunities for faculty at the <a href="http://washingtoncenter.ucr.edu/faculty.html">UC Washington DC Center</a>. UCR sends a faculty member to teach and work at the center every academic quarter.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://internationalscholars.ucr.edu/">International Scholars Center</a> processes visas for international faculty scholars.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/students_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Students</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li>The <a href="http://cnas.ucr.edu/">College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences</a> features many <a href="http://cnas-ugresearch.ucr.edu/">undergraduate research opportunities</a> during the year.</li> 
      <li>Undergraduate research is also offered through the <a href="http://or.ucr.edu/">UCR Office of Research</a>.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">UCR Career Center</a> features <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/jobs/Pages/Jobs.aspx">Job and Internship Listings</a> that help students find the perfect employment opportunity.</li> 
      <li><a href="http://washingtoncenter.ucr.edu/">UC Washington DC Academic Internship Program</a> offers qualified undergraduates an opportunity to combine course work, field research and work experience during a quarter's residence in our nation's capital.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://internationalcenter.ucr.edu/">International Education Center</a> presents volunteer and internship positions around the world, allowing students a chance to grow through unique opportunity.</li> 
      <li>UCR's <a href="http://www.graduate.ucr.edu/">Graduate Division</a> hosts the <a href="http://www.graduate.ucr.edu/MSRIP.html">Mentoring Summer Research Internship Program (MSRIP)</a>, an eight-week summer research program for rising juniors and seniors from educationally and/or economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The goal is to increase the number of students from diverse backgrounds who go on to pursue Ph.D.</li> 
     </ul>

      <h3> Getting Started</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>When can I apply?</p> <p>You may access the <a href="https://admissions.universityofcalifornia.edu/applicant/login.htm"> online UC application</a> beginning October 1. Submit your application November 1 – 30. All applications are due November 30. <a href="/Apply"> Learn more about how to apply to UC Riverside.</a></p></li> 
       <li> <p>Where do I apply? </p> You may apply to UC Riverside using the University of California’s online <a href="https://admissions.universityofcalifornia.edu/">common application system</a>. For more detailed directions, see our <a href="/Apply"> Apply Now page.</a></li> 
       <li> <p>How much will my application cost?</p> <p> The application fee is $70 for each UC campus to which you apply. For international applicants, the fee is $80 for each campus.</p> <p> Fee waivers are available for up to four campuses for eligible United States citizens and permanent residents. You can apply for a fee waiver within <a href="https://admissions.universityofcalifornia.edu/applicant/login.htm"> the online application</a> and will be notified immediately whether or not you have qualified.</p></li> 
       <li> <p>What is the personal statement? </p> <p>The personal statement is your chance to tell us about yourself within your UC application. You will receive two essay prompts. Responses to both prompts must be a maximum of 1,000 words total. That means you can choose to respond to one prompt at greater length; however, we suggest that your shorter answer be at least 250 words. <a href="../Apply#write"> Learn more about the personal statement, get tips on writing it and see the prompts here. </a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>What about test scores? </p> <p>Incoming freshmen must take the <a href="http://www.actstudent.org/writing/">ACT Plus Writing</a> <em>or </em>the <a href="http://sat.collegeboard.org/home">SAT Reasoning Test</a> no later than December of their senior year. </p> <p>For ACT, SAT, AP, and IB scores, you will need to order score reports from the appropriate testing agency. We can only accept score reports from the testing agency – you can't email or fax them to our offices.</p> <p>Transfer students will be notified on <a href="http://my.ucr.edu/">MyUCR</a> if any test score reports are required.</p> <p>For the ACT Plus Writing and SAT Reasoning Test, we will focus on the highest total score from a single test date (or single sitting). </p> <p>We do not require <a href="http://sat.collegeboard.org/about-tests/sat-subject-tests"> SAT Subject Tests</a>, but some academic departments and competitive majors do recommend them. We recommend that students interested in the <a href="http://www.engr.ucr.edu/"> Bourns College of Engineering</a> or the <a href="http://cnas.ucr.edu/">College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences</a> take the Math 2 Subject Test and either Chemistry or Physics.</p> </li> 
       <li> <p>How will I know if I have been admitted? </p> <p>After you receive notification that we have received your application, you should set up a MyUCR account at <a href="http://vcsaweb.ucr.edu/MyUCR/CreateAccount.aspx"> My.UCR.edu</a> and link it to your application. Admission decisions will be posted on MyUCR starting February 1 for freshmen and March 1 for transfers. Check your MyUCR account frequently to ensure that you respond immediately to any alerts and/or document requests from your admissions counselor.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <hr class="style1" /> 
      <h3> Freshman Admission</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>I’m a freshman. What are my admission requirements? </p> <p>As a freshman applicant, you must complete “a-g” high school courses, take certain exams and earn a minimum GPA. <a href="../Admissions/pathsAdmissions#incomingFreshman"> Get the details and see all freshmen admission requirements here.</a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>How can I find out if my high school class is an “a-g” class?</p> <p>Visit <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/doorways" target="_blank">www.ucop.edu/doorways</a> to see your school’s UC-approved “a-g” course list. </p> </li> 
       <li> <p>What is the Guaranteed Admission Program? </p> <p>The Guaranteed Admission Program (GAP) is a non-binding program that guarantees exceptional California high school juniors admission to UCR. <a href="../WhyUCR/ourGuarantee#highSchool"> Learn more about GAP, including how to qualify and how to apply, here. </a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>How do I appeal an admission decision? </p> <p>You can access instructions for submitting an appeal <a href="../Content/PDFs/appeal_process.pdf">here</a>.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <hr class="style1" /> 
      <h3> Transfer Admission</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>I’m a transfer student. What are my admission requirements?</p> <p>As a transfer applicant, you must complete certain classes, earn a minimum GPA and meet selection requirements for your intended major. <a href="../Admissions/pathsAdmissions#transferStudents"> Get the details and see all transfer admission requirements here. </a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>What is the Transfer Admission Guarantee? </p> <p>UC Riverside’s Transfer Admission Guarantee (TAG) program guarantees highly qualified California community college students admission to UCR. Students must meet TAG eligibility requirements and complete the <a href="https://uctag.universityofcalifornia.edu/students/"> online TAG application</a>, which also serves as a course planner. Submit your TAG application September 1-30 for the following fall entry term. <a href="../WhyUCR/ourGuarantee#transferStudents">Learn more about TAG.</a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>How do I appeal an admission decision? </p> <p>You can access instructions for submitting an appeal <a href="../Content/PDFs/appeal_process.pdf">here</a>.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <h3> International Admission</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>I’m a freshman international student. What are my admission requirements?</p> <p>As a freshman international applicant, you must complete a rigorous college preparatory program, take specific exams and reach a certain level of academic achievement. <a href="http://international.ucr.edu/HowToApply.aspx" target="_blank"> Get the details and see all international freshmen admission requirements here. </a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>I’m a transfer international applicant. What are my admission requirements?</p> <p>As an international transfer applicant, you must complete certain required classes, earn a minimum GPA and meet requirements for your intended major. You may also need to take an English-proficiency exam. <a href="http://international.ucr.edu/HowToApply.aspx" target="_blank"> Get the details and see all international admission requirements here. </a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>Do you require TOEFL or IELTS scores?</p> <p>Yes. If you are an international student applying to UCR from a country where English is not the language of instruction, you must pass either the <a href="http://www.ets.org/portal/site/ets/menuitem.fab2360b1645a1de9b3a0779f1751509/?vgnextoid=69c0197a484f4010VgnVCM10000022f95190RCRD" target="_blank">Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL)</a> with a score of 550 or higher (PBT) or 80 or higher (iBT) or you must pass the <a href="http://www.ielts.org/" target="_blank">International English Language Testing System (IELTS)</a> with a score of 7.0 or higher.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <hr class="style1" /> 
      <h3> Academics</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>How can I check if my major is open? </p> <p> <a href="http://admission.universityofcalifornia.edu/how-to-apply/check-majors/index.html">You can review current open and closed majors here.</a></p> </li> 
       <li> <p>Do you have an academic catalog? </p> <p>Yes. Please see <a href="http://catalog.ucr.edu/">catalog.ucr.edu</a> </p> </li> 
       <li> <p>I’m a transfer student. Will my classes transfer to UCR?</p> <p>California community college students can use <a href="http://www.assist.org/web-assist/welcome.html">ASSIST.org </a>to obtain information regarding University of California transfer course agreements. <a href="mailto:transfer@ucr.edu">Contact a transfer admissions counselor</a> if you have coursework from a four-year, out-of-state or international school.</p> </li>
       <li> <p>Do you have a Student Special Services department? </p> <p>Yes. Our <a href="http://www.specialservices.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">Student Special Services</a> office provides services for students with disabilities, veterans and their eligible family members and ROTC referrals.</p> </li> 
       <li> <p>How does UCR compare to other schools? </p> <p>UCR is a university of choice. We believe in supporting our students, broadening our horizons and welcoming unique perspectives. Because of this, UCR has been recognized nationally and internationally as a top-tier university by U.S. News &amp; World Report, The Princeton Review, Forbes and more.</p> <p>We are a <a href="http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/rankings/national-universities/best-value"> Top 25 Best Value School</a>, the <a href="http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/rankings/national-universities/campus-ethnic-diversity"> 8th most diverse university</a> in the country and ranked <a href="http://www.washingtonmonthly.com/college_guide/rankings_2012/national_university_rank.php"> 9th in the nation for social mobility, research and public service</a>. </p> <p>When you combine our rankings with <a href="/WhyUCR/ourStudents">our diverse student body</a>, <a href="/Academics/faculty">award-winning faculty</a> and <a href="/WhyUCR/ourResearch">incredible research opportunities</a>, we have a lot to be proud of. But really, there’s only so much we can tell you online. We encourage you to visit our beautiful campus anytime during the year. Go to <a href="http://visit.ucr.edu">visit.ucr.edu</a> for more information about scheduling a tour.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <hr class="style1" /> 
      <h3> Paying for School</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>How much does UCR cost? </p> <p><a href="/PayingForCollege/costEstimator"> Find out how affordable it is to attend UCR.</a></p></li> 
       <li> <p>Do you offer financial aid? </p> <p> Yes! <a href="http://finaid.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">UCR’s Financial Aid Office</a> is committed to making UCR possible for all admitted students, regardless of income. <a href="../PayingForCollege/financialAidScholarships"> UCR offers a variety of grants, scholarships, work-study programs and loans. </a> About 83% of UCR undergraduate students receive some form of financial aid, and of those students, 70% had their full fees covered with grants and/or scholarships. Use our cost estimator to <a href="http://vcsaweb.ucr.edu/FinAidManualCalculator/">see how much financial aid you would get if you chose UCR</a>.</p> </li> 
       <li> <p>Will I qualify for financial aid as a nonresident? </p> <p>Maybe. US citizens, US nationals, permanent residents, refugees, asylees, other eligible noncitizens, and other non-immigrant students who meet the provisions of AB 540 are eligible for financial aid. <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/general-counsel/_files/ed-affairs/student-information-sheet.pdf" target="_blank">Learn more about AB 540</a>. </p> </li> 
      </ul>

      <h3> Getting Involved</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>Does UCR offer any student organizations? </p> <p>Absolutely! UCR offers a diverse array of student organizations ranging from academic organizations to community service groups to social clubs. Browse <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/"> our official Student Org Network</a> to see them all.</p> </li> 
       <li> <p>I want to talk to current students. Where can I go?</p> <p>Touring campus is a great way to interact with current and prospective UCR students. We offer many <a href="/VisitUCR/tourOptions">tour options</a> and it’s easy to <a href="http://student08.ucr.edu/em/visitUCR/default.aspx">schedule a tour</a>. If you are a transfer student, feel free to stop by the <a href="http://trc.ucr.edu"> Transfer Resource Center</a> to talk with Transfer Link mentors (former California community college students who are currently UCR students). You can also <a href="http://lifeatucr.tumblr.com/ask">contact the staff at Life at UCR</a>, a student-run blog about student life as a Highlander! View their blog at <a href="http://lifeatucr.tumblr.com"> LifeatUCR.tumblr.com</a> </p></li>

Plus, <a href="http://imsports.ucr.edu/">intramural sports competitions</a>, <a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/residential-life/programs-activities.aspx">residence hall activities</a>, and <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/">student org outings</a> are always occurring around campus. From<a href="http://gohighlanders.com/" target="_blank"> cheering at a game</a> to snowboarding with <a href="http://recreation.ucr.edu/outdoor_excursions/Pages/OutdoorExcursionsHome.aspx" target="_blank">Outdoor Excursions </a>to exploring <a href="/CampusLife/artsEvents">entertaining arts and events</a>, UCR is a fun place to be. <br /> <br /> Downtown Riverside is also home to a thriving arts scene, and many students frequent open mic performances, free museums and cozy cafes throughout the city. <a href="/VisitUCR/planVisit">Learn more about Riverside.</a></p></li> 
       <li> <p>Does UCR have a Greek community? </p> <p>Yes, we offer social, cultural and academic Greek organizations. <a href="http://studentlife.ucr.edu/studentOrganizations/Pages/FraternityandSororityLife.aspx"> Learn more about Greek life at UCR</a>.</p> </li>

      <h2>Extensi&oacute;n Cooperativa de la UC: 100 a&ntilde;os y seguimos contando</h2> 
      <p>Aportando soluciones, sirviendo como catalizador de cambio, estableciendo colaboraciones, protegiendo los recursos naturales, educando a la gente. Desde 1913, cient&iacute;ficos de <a href="http://ucanr.edu/sites/Spanish/Qu%C3%A9_es_ANR/Que_es_Extensi%C3%B3n_Cooperativa_de_la_UC/">Extensi&oacute;n Cooperativa</a> de la Universidad de California, junto con investigadores en los planteles educativos, han venido trabajando para hacer de California el principal estado agr&iacute;cola de la naci&oacute;n. Inf&oacute;rmese m&aacute;s acerca de la <a href="http://ucanr.edu/sites/Spanish/Qu%C3%A9_es_ANR/">Divisi&oacute;n de Agricultura y Recursos Naturales</a> (ANR) de la UC haciendo clic en el bot&oacute;n “<a href="http://ucanr.edu/sites/Spanish/Qu%C3%A9_es_ANR/">Qu&eacute; es ANR</a>”.</p>

     <h3>Great Things to Do and See</h3> 
     <p> Whether you want to hop in your car and see the sights, or stay close to home here in Riverside, there are plenty of things to do and see. From the desert to the sea, all of Southern California is within reach. </p> 
     <p> The city of Riverside's website has great listings of&nbsp; <a class="external" href="http://www.riversideca.gov">things to do, the city's thriving arts community, places to shop and eat, and more!</a> </p> 
     <div class="column23"> 
      <p> Our location puts us within an easy drive of: </p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Beaches throughout Southern California.</li> 
       <li>Mountain resorts, including those at Big Bear and Lake Arrowhead.</li> 
       <li>Palm Springs and many the desert recreational areas.</li> 
       <li>World famous amusement parks such as Disneyland and Knott's Berry Farm.</li> 
       <li>Great shopping, including the Ontario Mills Mall, Victoria Gardens Shopping Complex and the Promanade at Dos Lagos. </li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4>Right Here in Riverside!</h4> 
      <ul> 
       <li>The Mission Inn </li> 
       <li>Riverside Plaza </li> 
       <li>Riverside Metropolitan Museum </li> 
       <li>Sweeney Art Gallery </li> 
       <li>Tyler Mall </li> 
       <li>UCR ARTS Block </li> 
       <li>UCR/California Museum of Photography </li> 
       <li>University Village </li> 
      </ul>

     <h1>About us</h1> 
     <div class="wysiwyg"> 
      <p><strong>Our Mission</strong></p> 
      <p><strong></strong>Based in San Francisco, California, StopTheHacker is one of the tech industry’s most respected leaders; a provider of SaaS services focused on cyber warfare in the arenas of web malware, security and reputation protection. StopTheHacker’s Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning based technology is supported by the National Science Foundation and has won multiple awards since 2009. StopTheHacker has become widely recognized globally, protecting website owners ranging from large multi-nationals to web hosting companies and small business owners, all who are vulnerable to malicious hacker attacks. </p> 
      <p>Follow us on Twitter at <a href="http://www.twitter.com/stopthehacker" target="_blank">twitter.com/stopthehacker</a> and Facebook at <a href="http://www.facebook.com/stopthehacker" target="_blank">facebook.com/stopthehacker</a> for up to the minute updates.</p>
