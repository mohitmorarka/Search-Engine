     <p><img src="/students/images/services_main.jpg" alt="Services for Students" width="484" height="180" /> </p> 
     <h3>Supporting Your Needs as You Shape Your Future</h3> 
     <p>There's more to college life than the classroom. It’s also about the quality of your daily life and preparing you for life after college. UCR has services and resources that can support you every step of the way, whether it is deciding where to eat between classes or making plans for your future. </p> 
     <div class="boxB"> 
      <h4><a class="standout2" href="/students/campussafety.html">Campus Safety</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/cses.jpg" alt="Dining Services" width="93" height="46" /> General information on Campus Police, Campus Safety Escort Service, the Campus Emergency Notification System and Environmental Health and Safety.&nbsp; </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">Career Center</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/career_center2.jpg" alt="Career Center thumbnail" width="93" height="46" /> UCR’s <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">Career Center</a> provides students and alumni with access to an extensive job database, career counseling, offer resume and interview advice and alert you to upcoming workshops and job fairs.&nbsp; </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.learningcenter.ucr.edu/">Child Development Center</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/childcare_thumb.jpg" alt="Child development " width="93" height="46" />Our <a href="http://www.childrenservices.ucr.edu/">Child Development Center</a> provides a safe, nurturing environment for the children of UCR students, staff and faculty. The center has programs for children ages four months to kindergarten age. <a href="http://www.childrenservices.ucr.edu/"> </a></p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://dining.ucr.edu/">Dining Services</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/dining_thumbs.jpg" alt="Dining Services" width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://dining.ucr.edu/">UCR Dining Services</a> provides the campus with great food as well as a variety of locations to relax, socialize or study. From the dining halls to our retail locations, we offer high-quality and healthy dining options in a friendly, welcoming environment. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/students/ethnic_gender.html">Ethnic &amp; Gender Program Offices</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/e_gthumbnail.jpg" alt="Dining Services" width="93" height="46" /> The <a href="/students/ethnic_gender.html">Ethnic and Gender Program Offices</a> feature student support services, community involvement, multi-cultural events and leadership training that allows students to engage in cultural growth and development and build the skills needed to lead and succeed in a diverse world. </p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/students/health.html">Health, Fitness and Wellbeing</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/health_fitness.jpg" alt="health, fitness and wellbeing" title="health, fitness and wellbeing" width="93" height="46" /> UCR is an environment for students, staff and faculty that promotes a vibrant, healthy and balanced lifestyle. We have several integrated wellness programs that foster a culture of health and wellness and promote the importance of maintaining an optimal quality of life.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://housing.ucr.edu/">Housing Services</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/housing_thumb.jpg" alt="Housing Services" width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://housing.ucr.edu/">UCR Housing Services</a> provides our students with safe, convenient, student-oriented housing. We offer three housing styles — residence halls, campus apartments and family housing — to meet the needs of all our students. Each provides the opportunity to participate in activities that enrich your college experience. Our on-call housing staff, supported by the campus police force, are committed to providing a safe and secure environment.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="/international/">International Resources</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/irthumbnail.jpg" alt="international Resources" /> The <a href="/international/">International Resources</a> website features a variety of links for students seeking information on international programs here at UCR as well as other opportunities around the globe.</p> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://ucrcard.ucr.edu/">R'Card</a></h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/rcard.png" alt="R'Card" width="93" height="46" /> Your <a href="http://ucrcard.ucr.edu/">R'Card</a> is your single most important piece of student identification. </p> 
      <p>This multi-purpose ID allows you to: </p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li>Verify your identity on campus and in the community. </li> 
       <li>Pay for meals. </li> 
       <li>Borrow books from the library. </li> 
       <li>Gain access to the Student Recreation Center (SRC). </li> 
       <li>Get into UCR home athletic events. </li> 
       <li>Gain access to some campus buildings. </li> 
       <li>Make purchases on campus using&nbsp; &quot;Bear Bucks.&quot; </li> 
       <li>Use vending machines, laundry services, and copy machines. </li> 
       <li>Ride the Riverside Transit Agency bus for free. </li> 
       <li>Use registrar services. </li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://specialservices.ucr.edu/">Student Special Services</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/services_for_students.JPG" alt="Student Special Services" width="93" height="46" /><a href="http://specialservices.ucr.edu/">Student Special Services</a> helps to create an accessible university environment for all students.&nbsp; </p> 
      <p>Resources include: </p> 
      <ul class="tight"> 
       <li>Services for students with disabilities.</li> 
       <li>Veterans' educational benefits.</li> 
       <li>Voter registration.</li> 
       <li>ROTC referral.</li> 
       <li>Selective Service information.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h4 class="section"><a class="standout2" href="http://www.ucrcampusstore.ucr.edu/home.aspx">UCR Campus Store</a> </h4> 
      <p><img class="img_L" src="/students/images/campus_store2.jpg" alt="campus store thumbnail" width="93" height="46" /> The <a href="http://www.ucrcampusstore.ucr.edu/home.aspx">UCR Campus Store</a> is your best choice for textbooks and UCR merchandise. You’ll find the complete booklist for all your classes, as well as the largest selection of UCR apparel, school spirit items and gifts. CompUCR features electronics, computers, software and supplies.</p>

     <h3>Stay Involved and Informed</h3> 
     <p> Since 1988, the <a href="http://parents.ucr.edu/">UCR Parents Association</a> has helped parents stay informed about their students’ undergraduate experiences and provided opportunities for them to get involved on campus. </p> 
     <p> Your free membership entitles you to: </p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Attend quarterly meetings, where you’ll hear from administrators, staff and faculty about what’s happening on campus.</li> 
      <li>VIP invitations to special campus events.</li> 
      <li>Volunteer opportunities at campus activities, such as orientation and commencement.</li> 
      <li>The opportunity to earn points, which you can use to get special seating at campus events.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p> To sign up for your free membership, or find out about volunteer opportunities, e-mail or call: </p> 
     <address> <strong>Parents Association Coordinator, <a href="mailto:allison.tavaglione@ucr.edu">Allison Tavaglione</a></strong> <br /> (800) 426-ALUM or (951) UCR-ALUM </address> 
     <h4>Meetings</h4> 
     <p> The Parents Association’s quarterly meetings feature presentations from some of our most prominent administrators, faculty and staff members. <a href="http://parents.ucr.edu/meetings_events.html">Find out about our upcoming meetings</a>. </p> 
     <h4>Parent of the Year Award</h4> 
     <p> The UCR Parents Association invites students to nominate a parent or guardian who has been an inspiration to them or others. <a href="http://parents.ucr.edu/poy.html">Learn more about the nomination process and submission deadlines.</a> </p> 
     <h4>Earn Points</h4> 
     <p> For every quarterly meeting you attend and campus event you volunteer at, we'll award you &quot;<a href="http://parents.ucr.edu/volunteer.html">Participation Points</a>.&quot; Earn enough and you'll be awarded the coveted &quot;Chancellor's Reserved Seating&quot; section at Commencement! </p>

     <h3 class="accessibility">Who Gives to UC Riverside?</h3> 
     <p id="l_top_paragaph"> <strong>Every year, thousands of people make gifts to UCR.</strong> Their reasons for giving are as diverse as the donors themselves:</p>
     <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     <div class="clear"></div> 
     <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="maincontent1" group="Everyone" button="707" -->
     <!-- com.omniupdate.editor csspath="/z-omniupdate/css/style.css" cssmenu="/z-omniupdate/css/style.txt" width="484" --> 
     <ul> 
      <li> Grateful alumni looking to give back.</li> 
      <li>Parents of students honoring their children's education. </li> 
      <li>Staff, faculty, friends and neighbors who believe in the university's mission.</li> 
      <li>Corporations and foundations who want to make a difference in students' lives.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p> Who are these donors? <a href="/giving/who_gives.html">They are people just like you</a> who have chosen to make a <a href="/giving/scholarship_stories.html">difference in the lives of others</a>.</p>

      <p>As a college basketball player at UC Riverside, Allen Fletcher knew a lot about assists. But the biggest assist in Fletcher's UCR career was the scholarship that enabled him to complete his degree in Sociology/Law and Society while playing the sport he loved.</p>

     <h3>UCR: A Place to Make a Connection</h3> 
     <p> UC Riverside is made up of a diverse and welcoming community that is forward-looking in its commitment to learning and exploration. Reflecting California's intricate cultural landscape is an ongoing process at UCR, where faculty and staff know they are part of building and maintaining a great research university in a comfortable and inclusive setting.</p> 
     <p> Faculty and academic staff have a shared commitment to producing relevant research. Through such research, UCR exerts a positive influence on the rest of Inland Southern California, helping the growing region shape its future. </p> 
     <ul class="standout"> 
      <li>Want to learn more about UCR? We invite you to come see it for yourself.<br /> <a href="/about/tours.html">Learn more about visiting the campus or arranging a tour. </a></li> 
     </ul>
     <!-- /com.omniupdate.div --> 
     <div class="nota-bene"> 
      <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="maincontent2" group="Everyone" button="707" -->
      <!-- com.omniupdate.editor csspath="/z-omniupdate/css/style.css" cssmenu="/z-omniupdate/css/style.txt" width="484" -->
      <h4>Making a Difference in the Local Community</h4> 
      <p> UCR is not an island of academia lost in the sprawl of Southern California. We are a key contributor to the growth of Inland Southern California and among the top employers in the region. We make a difference in the economic development, cultural amenities, and intellectual resources of Riverside and San Bernardino counties. </p> 
      <ul>
        We partner with area business and government to foster solutions to complex problems. This relationship gives faculty members the opportunity to help shape the development of the Inland region, a key area of California's growth during the next decade: 
       <li>We help the region by bringing national leaders in the arts and sciences to the area. With them come new ideas, new ways of thinking and fresh approaches to persistent challenges. </li> 
       <li>The <a href="http://medschool.ucr.edu/">UCR School of Medicine</a> will help alleviate a projected 53 percent shortage of physicians in the Inland area and will bring many accompanying economic benefits.</li> 
       <li>The <a href="http://palmdesert.ucr.edu/">UCR Palm Desert Graduate Center</a> gives the university a strong presence in the Coachella Valley.</li> 
       <li>The university works with the city of Riverside and the local school district to provide tutoring and after-school activities to economically challenged youth through the University Eastside Community Collaborative/AmeriCorps project.</li> 
       <li>The <a href="http://www.gluckprogram.ucr.edu/">Gluck Fellows Program of the Arts</a> ensures an arts presence in schools, community centers and other facilities.</li> 
       <li>The university's Cooperative Extension provides cutting-edge research improvements the local environment and enhances the state's $32 billion agricultural industry. </li> 
      </ul> 
      <p> Through such programs, UCR faculty and staff have the opportunity to play leadership roles in the development of the region. Such roles are not always available at many other institutions of higher learning, but it is integral to UCR's mission. </p>
