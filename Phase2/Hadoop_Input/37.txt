      <h3>Basic Directions to UC Riverside</h3> 
      <h4>From Los Angeles County</h4> 
      <p><strong>From the 91 Freeway: </strong>Take CA-91 east to the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <p><strong>From the 10 Freeway:</strong> Take the I-10 east to the I-15 south and then to CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <p><strong>From the 60 Freeway:</strong> Take the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <h4>From Ontario Airport</h4> 
      <p><strong>From the 10 Freeway:</strong> Take the I-10 east, to the I-15 south and then to the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <p><strong>From the 60 Freeway:</strong> Take the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <h4>From Orange County</h4> 
      <p>Take the CA-91 east to the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <h4>From Palm Springs</h4> 
      <p>Take the I-10 west to CA-60 west. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <h4>From San Bernardino </h4> 
      <p>Take the I-215 south to the CA-60 east. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
      <h4> From San Diego County</h4> 
      <p>Take the I-15 north to the I-215 north to the CA-60 west. Exit at University Avenue and turn left. At the second light, take a right onto West Campus Drive.</p> 
     </div>

      <h3>Parking, Traffic &amp; Campus Information</h3> 
      <h4>Printable Campus Map</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://www.campusmap.ucr.edu/map.pdf">Download an 8&frac12;&times;11 PDF UCR campus map.</a></p> 
      <h4>Online Campus Map</h4> 
      <p><a href="http://www.campusmap.ucr.edu/campusMap.php">Visit our online campus map.</a></p> 
      <h4>Visitor Parking</h4> 
      <p>University visitors may obtain parking permits at the Information Kiosk or self-service dispensers. Permits are valid in visitor lots or Gold parking zones. Zones are enforced 7 a.m. to 10 p.m., 7 days a week.</p> 
      <p>Every vehicle, unless otherwise exempt, must display a valid parking permit when parking on UCR property.</p> 
      <p>For more information on parking at UCR, <a href="http://parking.ucr.edu/visitor/kiosk.html">visit the Parking website.</a></p> 
      <h4>Traffic Information</h4>

    <h2 class="long">Career Opportunities at UC Riverside</h2> 
    <!-- Start Sidebar --> 
    <div id="sidebar"> 
     <div class="nav">

       <li><a href="/privacy.html" title="Privacy Policy">Privacy Policy<br /><span></span></a></li>

    <div id="main"> 
     <p> <img alt="No matter who you are or what your career goals are, UC Riverside has an opportunity for you." src="/images/employ/employment_main.jpg" height="214" width="484" /></p> 
     <p> Regardless of whether you are a current member of the UCR family, or just considering joining us as a student, staff member or faculty, we have opportunities for you. UCR is the premier research university in Inland Southern California and we are seeking the best to join us. Explore your opportunities below.</p> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/staff_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Staff</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li><a href="http://hr.ucr.edu/jobs/">The Jobs Page at UCR Human Resources</a> is the place to find complete listings of all staff employment opportunities on campus.</li> 
      <li><a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/?content=EducationAndDevelopment/abouted.htm">Education and Development courses</a>, offered through <a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/">UCR Human Resources</a>, give employees the opportunity for career growth that can enhance their contributions to the University’s missions and goals.</li> 
      <li>Labor Relations, including Collective Bargaining, and Workers’ Compensation are handled through <a href="http://humanresources.ucr.edu/">UCR Human Resources</a>.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/faculty_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Faculty</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li>The <a href="http://academicpersonnel.ucr.edu/employment/">Office of Academic Personnel</a> oversees the hiring of faculty and academic staff at UC Riverside. Position openings are listed on departmental sites as well as on the <a class="external" title="external site: SoCalHERC" href="http://socalherc.org/home/index.cfm?site_id=761">Southern California Higher Education Recruitment Consortium (SoCalHERC)</a>.</li> 
      <li>There are great opportunities for faculty at the <a href="http://washingtoncenter.ucr.edu/faculty.html">UC Washington DC Center</a>. UCR sends a faculty member to teach and work at the center every academic quarter.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://internationalscholars.ucr.edu/">International Scholars Center</a> processes visas for international faculty scholars.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3><img alt=" " src="/images/employ/students_tab.gif" height="33" width="484" /><span class="accessibility">Opportunities for Students</span></h3> 
     <ul> 
      <li>The <a href="http://cnas.ucr.edu/">College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences</a> features many <a href="http://cnas-ugresearch.ucr.edu/">undergraduate research opportunities</a> during the year.</li> 
      <li>Undergraduate research is also offered through the <a href="http://or.ucr.edu/">UCR Office of Research</a>.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/">UCR Career Center</a> features <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu/jobs/Pages/Jobs.aspx">Job and Internship Listings</a> that help students find the perfect employment opportunity.</li> 
      <li><a href="http://washingtoncenter.ucr.edu/">UC Washington DC Academic Internship Program</a> offers qualified undergraduates an opportunity to combine course work, field research and work experience during a quarter's residence in our nation's capital.</li> 
      <li>The <a href="http://internationalcenter.ucr.edu/">International Education Center</a> presents volunteer and internship positions around the world, allowing students a chance to grow through unique opportunity.</li> 
      <li>UCR's <a href="http://www.graduate.ucr.edu/">Graduate Division</a> hosts the <a href="http://www.graduate.ucr.edu/MSRIP.html">Mentoring Summer Research Internship Program (MSRIP)</a>, an eight-week summer research program for rising juniors and seniors from educationally and/or economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The goal is to increase the number of students from diverse backgrounds who go on to pursue Ph.D.</li> 
     </ul>
