      <h3>How to Adjust Your Classes Before Week Two</h3> 
      <p>You can add, drop or change classes in <a href="https://ucribm.ucr.edu/Paws/PAWS.html" target="_blank">Growl</a> until the end of the second week of instruction. </p> 
      <h3>How to Adjust Your Classes After Week Two</h3> 
      <p>After week two, all changes must be made with an online<a href="http://student08.ucr.edu/Em/myforms/Forms.aspx" target="_blank"> Enrollment Adjustment Form</a>. Undergraduate students can access the form at noon beginning the Friday of week two. There is a $4 fee after the Friday of week three.</p> 
      <div class="accordion"> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> About The Enrollment Adjustment Form</h4> 
       <div class="addBullet"> 
        <p> <strong><a href="http://student08.ucr.edu/Em/myforms/Forms.aspx" target="_blank">The Enrollment Adjustment Form</a> includes instructions on how to: </strong> </p> 
        <ul style="margin-top: 1px;"> 
         <li>Add a class</li> 
         <li>Change a section</li> 
         <li>Withdraw from (drop) a class</li> 
         <li>Change your grading basis</li> 
         <li>Change variable units </li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>The “Learn More” section has info for international students, those concerned about financial aid and those making retroactive enrollment adjustments. </p> 
       </div> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> You May Need to Obtain Signatures</h4> 
       <div class="addBullet"> 
        <p>After filling out the form, you will know if you need to print it out and obtain signatures. </p> 
        <p style="margin-bottom: 1px;"> A dean’s signature is required when you: </p> 
        <ul style="margin-top: 1px;"> 
         <li>Add or change a class after week three</li> 
         <li>Withdraw from a class after week six </li> 
         <li>Change your grading basis after week eight</li> 
         <li>Change variable units after week eight</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>Once you have obtained all signatures, turn the form in to your academic advisor.</p> 
       </div> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> After You Submit Your Form</h4> 
       <div class="addBullet"> 
        <p>You will receive emails notifying you of the status of your request. You may also visit <a href="https://auth.ucr.edu/cas/login?service=http://student08.ucr.edu/em/myforms/myforms.aspx" target="_blank">MyForms.ucr.edu</a> to check your progress.</p> 
       </div> 
       <h4> <img src="/Images/square-bullet.png" alt="" /> Graduate Students</h4> 
       <div class="addBullet"> 
        <p>You may obtain a paper Enrollment Adjustment Form from your department’s graduate program coordinator beginning Friday of week two. Directions and required signatures will be made clear when you obtain the form. Bring your completed form to the <a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">Graduate Division</a>.</p> 
        <p>If you are adding a class after week three, or dropping a class after week six, you may need to complete an additional form called the <a href="http://graduate.ucr.edu/forms/Late%20enrollment.pdf" target="_blank">Graduate Petition for Late or Retroactive Enrollment Adjustment</a>.</p>
