     <h3>A University of Distinction and Diversity</h3> 
     <p>The University of California, Riverside is one of 10 universities within the prestigious University of California system, and the only UC located in Inland Southern California.</p> 
     <p>Widely recognized as one of the most ethnically diverse research universities in the nation, UCR's current enrollment is more than 21,000 students, with a goal of 25,000 students by 2020. The campus is in the midst of a tremendous growth spurt with new and remodeled facilities coming on-line on a regular basis.</p> 
     <p>We are located approximately 50 miles east of downtown Los Angeles. UCR is also within easy driving distance of dozens of major cultural and recreational sites, as well as desert, mountain and coastal destinations.</p>

     <h3>A University of Distinction and Diversity</h3> 
     <p>The University of California, Riverside is one of 10 universities within the prestigious University of California system, and the only UC located in Inland Southern California.</p> 
     <p>Widely recognized as one of the most ethnically diverse research universities in the nation, UCR's current enrollment is more than 21,000 students, with a goal of 25,000 students by 2020. The campus is in the midst of a tremendous growth spurt with new and remodeled facilities coming on-line on a regular basis.</p> 
     <p>We are located approximately 50 miles east of downtown Los Angeles. UCR is also within easy driving distance of dozens of major cultural and recreational sites, as well as desert, mountain and coastal destinations.</p>

     <p> Give to UCR and you’ll be making a lasting contribution, furthering our mission to provide life-changing teaching and world-changing research.      <h3>A Legacy of Promise</h3> 
     <p>Help UCR students and faculty to fulfill their promise by investing in a planned gift. UCR has been served by planned gifts in a variety of ways – from scholarships and program support to preservation of treasured places on campus. Whether you establish a bequest or a life income gift, you will benefit scholars at UCR for generations to come.</p> 
     <p>For in-depth information on planned giving options,<a class="external" href="http://www.gftpln.org/Home.do?orgId=6404"> please visit our Gift Planning website</a>. It features up-to-date information on the best giving options to meet your financial goals.</p> 
     <div class="contact-us"> 
      <p>For more information, <a href="mailto:giftplanning@ucr.edu">contact the Office of Gift Planning</a> by phone at (877) 249-0181 toll-free or via e-mail at <a href="mailto:giftplanning@ucr.edu">giftplanning@ucr.edu.</a> </p> 
     </div> 
     <h3 class="section3">Life Income Gifts</h3> 
     <p>Life income gifts pay income to you and loved ones while providing future support to UCR. These gifts also provide a charitable deduction and other tax advantages. </p> 
     <ul class="l_expander_border"> 
      <li> <h4>Charitable Gift Annuity </h4> <span class="xpander"> <p>Charitable gift annuities pay a fixed sum each year to you or anyone you name (up to two individuals) for life—unaffected by market fluctuations. UCR Charitable Gift Annuities are issued by the UC Regents and are backed by UC systemwide assets. Higher rates took effect on July 1, 2010!</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a class="external" href="http://www.gftpln.org/Article.do?orgId=6404&amp;articleId=9416">Learn more about Charitable Gift Annuities</a></li> 
        </ul> </span> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Deferred Gift Annuity</h4> <span class="xpander"> <p>Looking to increase retirement income? With a Deferred Gift Annuity, you secure a higher payout for life by starting income at a later date. You also get an immediate charitable deduction and ongoing tax savings. Higher rates took effect on July 1, 2010!</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a href="http://www.gftpln.org/Article.do?orgId=6404&amp;articleId=2591">Learn more about Deferred Gift Annuities</a></li> 
        </ul> </span> </li> 
      <li> <h4>Charitable Remainder Unitrust </h4> <span class="xpander"> <p>Turn appreciated assets into an income stream while minimizing capital gains. Payouts of a Charitable Remainder Unitrust can be set to grow as trust principal grows.</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a class="external" href="http://www.gftpln.org/Article.do?orgId=6404&amp;articleId=9523">Learn more about Charitable Remainder Unitrusts</a></li> 
        </ul> </span> </li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section2">Charitable Lead Trust</h3> 
     <p>You transfer your cash or property to fund a lead trust that makes gifts to us for a number of years. You receive a charitable deduction for the gift. Your family receives the remainder at substantial tax savings.</p> 
     <ul class="tight"> 
      <li><a class="external" href="http://www.gftpln.org/Article.do?orgId=6404&amp;articleId=9527">Learn more about the Charitable Lead Trust</a></li> 
     </ul> 
     <h3 class="section2">Bequests and Wills</h3> 
     <p><a class="external" href="http://www.gftpln.org/Article.do?orgId=6404&amp;articleId=9529">Bequests are an easy way to make a difference at UCR</a>. <strong>To make a gift by bequest,</strong> simply name UC Riverside Foundation (tax ID: #23-7433570) as a beneficiary of one or more of the following:</p> 
     <ul class="tight"> 
      <li>Will</li> 
      <li>Trust</li> 
      <li>Retirement account</li> 
      <li>Insurance plan</li> 
      <li>Bank account</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>Please let us know if you plan to include UCR in your plans so that we can ensure that your wishes can be fulfilled. <a href="mailto:giftplanning@ucr.edu">Contact us</a> for sample bequest language.</p> 
     <h3 class="section2">Resources for Professional Advisors</h3> 
     <p>Through our partnership with Crescendo Interactive, we are pleased to provide financial professionals access to the GiftLaw website, which features information and resources that allow them to provide the best-possible service to their clients. </p>

     <h2 class="intro">Action-Packed</h2> 
     <h3 class="verd intro"> Amped events, big-time athletics, cool housing, total wellness and countless opportunities to plug into our vibrant campus life. </h3> 
     <img alt="Five UCR students smiling" class="centerImg" width="557" height="323" src="/Images/CampusLifeLanding/topPhoto.png" />
     <br /> 
     <div class="grid_10 prefix_1 suffix_1 alpha omega alternatingBoxes"> 
      <p style="margin-bottom: 40px;"> We are the fastest growing campus in the UC system. Our signature music festivals, talented sports teams, and one-of-a-kind student organizations all contribute to the excitement that permeates UCR. See all that you have to look forward to. </p> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="Two female students hugging" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/Celebrate.png" /> 
       <h3> Celebrate</h3> 
       <p> From <a href="http://rside.ucr.edu/blockparty/">Block Party</a> and <a href="http://heat.ucr.edu/">Heat</a> to special film screenings and art exhibitions, our campus comes alive with <a href="/CampusLife/artsEvents">arts and events</a>.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="Highlander fans cheering" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/cheer.png" /> 
       <h3> Cheer</h3> 
       <p> We have <a href="http://gohighlanders.com/">17 sports teams</a> that compete in the NCAA Division 1 Big West Conference. Join the thousands of fellow students who cheer for our Highlanders– or compete as an athlete! <a href="/CampusLife/athleticsRecreation">Athletics and recreation</a> thunder at UCR.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="UCR students playing tug-of-war" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/Participate.png" /> 
       <h3> Participate</h3> 
       <p> Be a <a href="http://well.ucr.edu/Peers/Pages/NetworkofPeers.aspx">peer mentor</a>, join a <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organizations?SearchValue=Recreational&amp;SearchType=Category&amp;CurrentPage=1&amp;SelectedCategoryId=4173 ">sports club</a> or write for a <a href="http://www.highlandernews.org/ ">student publication</a>. Pledge a <a href="http://studentlife.ucr.edu/STUDENTORGANIZATIONS/Pages/FraternityandSororityLife.aspx">fraternity or sorority</a>, participate in <a href="http://asucr.ucr.edu/">student government</a> or give back with <a href="http://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organizations?SearchValue=Service&amp;SearchType=Category&amp;CurrentPage=1&amp;SelectedCategoryId=4079">community service</a> organizations. <a href="/CampusLife/gettingInvolved">Get involved at UCR</a>!</p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="Martial arts demonstration" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/feelgood.png" /> 
       <h3> Feel Good</h3> 
       <p> Your total-body wellness is supported on our campus. From <a href="http://well.ucr.edu/upcomingevents/Pages/WELLPrograms.aspx">meditation classes</a> and <a href="http://counseling.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx">compassionate counselors</a> to a<a href="http://www.recreation.ucr.edu/Pages/default.aspx"> state-of-the-art gym</a> and unique wellness programs, your <a href="/CampusLife/safetyWellness">safety and wellness</a> will thrive here. </p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="Two students relaxing near a residence hall" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/live.png" /> 
       <h3> Live</h3> 
       <p> At UCR, you live in comfortable housing within walking distance of movie theaters, coffee shops and your classes! Theme halls are an added bonus. <a href="/CampusLife/housing">Explore UCR’s housing communities</a>. </p> 
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <img alt="Students using computers" width="266" height="177" src="/Images/campuslifelanding/stayconnected.png" /> 
       <h3> Stay Connected</h3> 
       <p> See what’s happening on campus by following us on: <br /> <a href="http://lifeatucr.tumblr.com/">Tumblr</a><br /> <a href="https://twitter.com/ucradmissions" target="_blank">Twitter</a><br /> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ucradmissions?bcode=q099X" target="_blank">Facebook </a><br /> </p> 
      </div>
