     <h3>Welcome to Conflict of Commitment &amp; Compensated Outside Activities</h3> 
     <p>The University of California requires its faculty to submit an annual report indicating whether or not they have engaged in outside compensated activities during the fiscal year. Those faculty that have engaged in this type of activity are asked to provide information to the University about the activity and any related compensation.</p> 
     <h4>Easy to Use System for Reporting</h4> 
     <p>UC Riverside's Conflict of Commitment (COC) system provides an easy to use, online system for reporting this information on an annual basis. Faculty at UC Riverside can log on to COC and enter all pertinent information relating to outside compensated activities, which is then electronically routed to the appropriate administrative personnel.</p> 
     <h4>Proper Approval and Disclosure</h4> 
     <p>Compensated outside professional activity may raise the appearance, or the reality, of a conflict of commitment to the faculty members' University obligations. The prior approval for and disclosure of outside professional activities ensures the proper oversight of these activities in context of the faculty members' defined University responsibilities.</p> 
     <h4>Applying the APM-025 Policy</h4> 
     <p>The <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/acadadv/acadpers/apm/apm-025-07-01.pdf" target="_blank">APM-025</a> policy affirms faculty responsibilities as members of the University of California and provides mechanisms to ensure that activities outside the University do not interfere with fulfillment of these responsibilities. The policy specifies limits on the time that can be devoted by faculty members to certain types of compensated and non-compensated outside professional activities.</p> 
     <p><a href="http://www.ucop.edu/acadadv/acadpers/apm/apm-025-07-01.pdf" target="_blank">APM-025</a> applies only to Academic Senate members (full-time or part time). However, it does not apply to emeritus faculty members unless they are on recall status. In addition, it applies to all academic or other administrators who hold academic appointments in a faculty title series regardless of the current percentage of time related to the academic appointment.</p> 
     <p>Key provisions of the policy are the requirements that faculty must:</p> 
     <ol> 
      <li> Obtain approval before engaging in certain specified types of compensated activities (&quot;Category I&quot; activities, as defined in <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/acadadv/acadpers/apm/apm-025-07-01.pdf" target="_blank">APM-025</a>)</li> 
      <li>Submit reports documenting the Category I and/or Category II compensated activities in which they engaged for the Fiscal Year ending June 30 annually.</li> 
     </ol>

       <a id="q1" name="q1"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 1: How can I obtain a copy of my transcript, obtain a copy of my diploma, or receive degree verification for another university or employer?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: Visit the Registrar online at&nbsp;
       <a href="http://registrar.ucr.edu">http://registrar.ucr.edu</a> or contact the office by phone at 951-827-7284.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q2" name="q2"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 2: Where do I go to retrieve letters of recommendation on file?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: Visit the Career Center online at 
       <a href="http://careers.ucr.edu">http://careers.ucr.edu</a>or contact the office by phone at 951-827-3631.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q3" name="q3"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 3: How do I become a member of the Alumni Association and what are the benefits?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: There are three easy ways to join. You can join online at 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/membership">www.alumni.ucr.edu/membership</a>, call us at 951-827-2586/800-426-2586 or visit us at the Alumni &amp; Visitors Center on campus. Members receive the UCR Magazine, gain access to all UC campus libraries and enjoy discounts off travel and entertainment. For more information, please go to 
       <a href="http://alumni.ucr.edu/benefits">http://alumni.ucr.edu/benefits</a>.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q4" name="q4"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 4: How do I contact other alumni?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=609">Sign up for the Highlanders Online Community</a> and use the search directory. You may also contact our office to see if we have current information for the alumnus/a you are searching for and if we do we can send him/her a contact letter with your information to contact you.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q5" name="q5"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 5: How do I set up email forwarding?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: You can setup email forwarding by logging in to 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/Page.aspx?pid=609">Highlanders Online</a>. If you don't have an account you can register for free. Go to 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/">http://www.alumni.ucr.edu</a>&nbsp;and click login at the top right of the screen. Enter your username and password to enter Highlanders Online. Once logged in click on the 
       <img height="16" src="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/view.image?Id=1621" width="16" />&nbsp;Email link.&nbsp; On the next page, make sure to check &quot;Yes, I want email forwarding.&quot; then enter your “Incoming Email Address” and “Forwarding Address” and click &quot;Update&quot;.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q6" name="q6"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 6: My webmail account was deactivated. What do I do?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: The 
       <a href="http://cnc.ucr.edu/">Office of Computing &amp; Communications </a>is responsible for all UCR Webmail accounts and will be able to provide you with assistance. If you are interested in a UCR alumni email address (e.g. john.doe@alumni.ucr.edu) you can get one by registering for a free Highlanders Online account.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q7" name="q7"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 7: What alumni events are taking place in my area?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: Please visit the Club and Chapter Events page: 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/events">http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/events</a>
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q8" name="q8"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 8: How do I give to the alumni scholarship fund?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: Please visit the Scholarship page: 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/involved/scholarships">http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/involved/scholarships</a>
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q9" name="q9"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 9: How do I get my Alumni Association discount for classes at the UCR Extension Center?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: Please contact the Office of Alumni &amp; Constituent Relations at 951-827-2586 for the appropriate code.
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>
      </div> 
      <hr /> 
      <div>
       <a id="q10" name="q10"></a>
      </div> 
      <h4>Question 10: How do I purchase alumni gear?</h4> 
      <div>
       A: UCR alumni gear can be purchased at the 
       <a href="http://www.ucrcampusstore.ucr.edu/MerchList.aspx?ID=8527">UCR campus bookstore</a>. UCRAA members receive save 10% on clothing and emblematic items. 
       <a href="http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/membership/categories">Join Now!</a>
      </div> 
      <div>
       <a href="#top">[top]</a>

function scarousel_initCallback(carousel) {
  $('#jd_news_sidebar').hover(function() {
	ishover = true;
    carousel.startAuto(0);
    carousel.animation(0);
  }, function() {
    ishover = false;
    carousel.animation(animrate);
    carousel.startAuto(autorate);
  });

}

$(document).ready(function(){

  var path = document.location.pathname;
  var dir = path.substring(path.indexOf('/', 0)+1, path.lastIndexOf('/'));

  if (dir == "calendar") {
    $("#sidebar_events").hide();
  } else {
    $.getJSON(calserver + "ae_get_website_sidebar.php?jsoncallback=?&prog="+prog,
    function(data) {
      $("#jd_news_sidebar").html(data.sidebar);
      $('#jd_news_sidebar ul').attr('id','sidebar_carousel');
      //$('div.jd_news_scroll').jdNewsScroll();
      $('#sidebar_carousel').jcarousel({
        initCallback: scarousel_initCallback,
        vertical: true,
        scroll: 1,
        auto: autorate,
		wrap: 'last',
		animation: animrate
      });
    });
  }

});
